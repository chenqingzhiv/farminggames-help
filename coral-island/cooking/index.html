    🍳 Coral Island Cooking Guide — All Recipes, Ingredients, and Meal Benefits

    Cooking in Coral Island is essential for restoring energy, getting temporary buffs, and building relationships. Unlike some farming sims, cooking here also contributes to a dedicated Cooking Skill with its own perk tree. This guide covers every recipe, the best dishes for every situation, and how to level up your cooking fast.

    Tip: Don't sell raw ingredients for pocket change. Even the simplest cooked meal is worth 2-3× the raw ingredients. Cooking is your best profit multiplier outside of artisan machines.

    🔥 Cooking Overview

    You start with a basic campfire stove. Upgrading your kitchen unlocks more cooking stations and better recipes.

    Station Unlock Function
    🔥 Basic Stove Starter tent Simple dishes (boiled, grilled)
    🍳 Kitchen Stove Wood Cabin upgrade All intermediate recipes
    🧑‍🍳 Gourmet Stove Stone House upgrade Advanced recipes, buff bonuses
    🧊 Fridge Stone House Ingredient storage, extends food shelf life
    🔬 Kitchen Lab Brick House Fermentation, aging, special recipes

    Ingredient Sources

    Source What You Get
    🌱 Farm Crops Vegetables, fruits, grains
    🐄 Animal Products Eggs, milk, cheese, butter
    🐟 Fishing All seafood types
    🤿 Diving Shells, seaweed, rare ocean ingredients
    🌿 Foraging Wild greens, mushrooms, berries
    🏪 Store Bought Spices, oil, sugar, flour

    ⚡ Best Meals for Energy

    When you're deep in the mines or spending a full day diving, energy management is everything. These dishes give you the best energy-per-slot:

    Dish Energy Ingredients Source Energy Per Ingredient Verdict
    🍲 Hearty Stew 200 Meat 2× + Potato + Carrot + Spice 40/ingredient ✅ Best all-around
    🥣 Seafood Stew 220 Fish 2× + Shell 2× + Spice 36/ingredient Great if you fish
    🥩 Roast Meat 250 Meat 3× + Potato + Onion + Spice 42/ingredient ✅ Highest single item
    🥟 Spring Rolls 120 Rice 2× + Cabbage + Soy 30/ingredient Cheap to mass-produce
    🥚 Fried Egg 40 Egg + Oil 20/ingredient Emergencies only

    Best Energy Strategy: Mass-produce Spring Rolls early game (rice and cabbage are cheap). Mid-game, switch to Hearty Stew or Seafood Stew. Late-game, Roast Meat stacks give you 250 energy per slot, enough for full-day mining runs.

    🎯 Cooking Skill Progression

    Every dish you cook gives Cooking XP. The amount depends on the complexity and tier of the recipe.

    Level XP Needed Perk Unlocked Effect
    1 0 Basic Recipes Access to campfire recipes
    2 100 Quick Hands 10% faster cooking
    3 250 Kitchen Intuition 5% chance to double a dish
    4 500 Gourmet Touch Unlock Gourmet Stove recipes
    5 800 Quality Cooking +1 quality tier to all cooked dishes
    6 1,200 Efficient Chef 10% ingredient reduction
    7 1,800 Double Batch 15% chance to cook 2×
    8 2,500 Master Chef Unlock all recipes
    9 3,500 Perfect Presentation +50% sell price on cooked dishes
    10 5,000 Culinary Legend 25% chance to cook osmium quality

    Fastest XP Strategy: Cook the highest-tier recipe you can consistently make. XP is based on recipe complexity, not quantity. One Roast Meat (200 XP) = four Fried Eggs (50 XP each). Always cook the best dish you can, not the cheapest.

    Cooking Skill Perk Priority

    Priority Perk Why
    🥇 1st Quality Cooking (Lv.5) +1 tier to everything — huge profit and energy boost
    🥇 2nd Efficient Chef (Lv.6) -10% ingredients — saves materials over time
    🥇 3rd Culinary Legend (Lv.10) 25% osmium — best endgame perk
    🥈 4th Double Batch (Lv.7) Good but inconsistent
    🥉 5th Perfect Presentation (Lv.9) Only matters if you sell
