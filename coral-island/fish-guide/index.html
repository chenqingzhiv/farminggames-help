    Coral Island — Fish Guide & Best Fishing Spots

    Complete guide to every fish in Coral Island, including season, location, difficulty, sell price, and usage. Whether you're completing the museum collection or maximizing cooking ingredients, this guide has you covered.

    Note: Fish difficulty is rated 1–100 (higher = harder to catch). Some fish are only available at certain times of day or weather conditions.

    Fishing Zones Overview

    Zone Description Best Catches
    Ocean The main beach and pier area Tuna, Sardine, Clownfish
    Ocean - Deep Reef edge, south dock Grouper, Red Snapper, Shark
    River Freshwater throughout the island Bass, Trout, Salmon
    River - Mountain Upstream near the waterfall Sturgeon, Salmon
    Lake The lake in the mid-island Carp, Catfish, Eel
    Pond Small ponds on the farm Bluegill, Perch
    Mines Underground fishing spots Cave Fish, Lava Eel
    Giant's Lake Special lake in Giant Village area Giant Catfish, Ancient Fish

    Fishing Skill & Mechanics

    Level Unlock
    1 Basic Rod, Bait unlocked
    2 Fiberglass Rod, Small Bait
    3 Iridium Rod, Medium Bait
    4 Fisher's Net, Large Bait
    5 Profession: Fisher (fish sell +25%) or Trapper (crab pots +50%)
    6 Quality Bait (attracts silver-quality fish)
    7 Deluxe Bait (attracts gold-quality fish)
    8 Sonar Bobber (shows fish location while casting)
    9 Magnet Bait (catch trash with rare finds)
    10 Profession: Angler (fish sell +50%) or Mariner (crab pots no longer catch trash)
