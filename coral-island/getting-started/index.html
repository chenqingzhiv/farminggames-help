    🏝️ Coral Island — Day 1 Beginner Guide

    Arriving on the Island

    Your first day begins as you step off the boat into the charming town of Coral Island. You'll meet several villagers immediately — follow them to your grandparent's overgrown farm to begin. The tutorial walks you through clearing basic debris, meeting key NPCs, and planting your first seeds.

    Day 1 Priority List

    1. Clear your farmhouse — Place your bed and storage chest inside
    2. Visit Sam's General Store — Buy the cheapest seeds available (parsnips or similar)
    3. Plant and water 6-8 crops — Don't overextend your stamina
    4. Talk to at least 5 villagers — Building relationships early pays off
    5. Check your mailbox — The tutorial quest rewards are inside

    💰 Making Money Fast

    In the first week, gold is tight. Your best money-makers are:

    Method Estimated Daily Income Notes
    🌾 Foraging 200-400g Wild fruits, flowers, and shells
    🗑️ Trash cleanup 150-300g Sell scrap and recyclable materials
    🎣 Fishing 300-600g Requires practice but reliable
    🤿 Diving (from Day 3) 500-1000g Best income once unlocked

    📅 First Week Schedule

    Day Focus
    Day 1 Plant seeds, meet townsfolk, forage
    Day 2 Water crops, explore beach area, fish
    Day 3 Unlock diving — clean ocean trash for big money
    Day 4 Mine for ore to upgrade tools
    Day 5 Donate artifacts to museum, unlock new areas
    Day 6-7 Harvest first crops, reinvest in seeds

    Tip: Do not sell raw ore in the first week — you need every scrap for tool upgrades. The pickaxe upgrade to copper should be your first priority, as it dramatically speeds up mining and debris clearing, saving you hours across the first season.
