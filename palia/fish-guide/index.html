    Palia — Fishing Guide

    Complete fishing guide for Palia covering every fish species, including location, time of day, quality levels, and sell prices. Fishing is a great way to earn gold, complete bundles, and build relationships through gifting.

    Note: Fishing levels unlock new rods, bait types, and zones. Higher fishing skill also increases the chance of catching rare and star-quality fish.

    Fishing Mechanics

    Mechanic Detail
    Rod Levels Makeshift Rod → Standard Rod → Fine Rod → Exquisite Rod
    Bait Types Worm (common), Glow Worm (uncommon), Honey Lure (rare)
    Bobbler Types Standard, Radon, Survey (each affects fish behavior)
    Fishing Zones Rivers, Lakes, Ponds, Coast/Ocean, Underground
    Time of Day Some fish only appear at Day (6AM–6PM) or Night (6PM–6AM)
    Weather Some fish are more common during rain

    Star Quality Prices

    All fish can be caught in star quality (⭐), which sells for significantly more:

    Fish Base Price ⭐ Star Price ⭐⭐ Gold Star
    Sardine 20 30 40
    Sturgeon 220 330 440
    Swordfish 500 750 1,000
    Void Ray 600 900 1,200
    Ancient Fish 1,200 1,800 2,400

    Star quality is determined by: - Fishing Level — Higher level = better star chance - Bait Quality — Honey Lures increase star quality chance significantly - Perfect Catch — Reeling in without the fish escaping gives a quality bonus - Bobbler — Survey Bobbler improves star quality odds

    Fishing Level Rewards

    Level Unlock
    1 Makeshift Fishing Rod
    2 Worm Bait recipe, Standard Fishing Rod
    3 Fishing Skill: Fisher (Fish sell for +25%) or Forager (Double catch on rare fish)
    4 Glow Worm Bait recipe
    5 Fine Fishing Rod
    6 Honey Lure recipe
    7 Fishing Skill: Angler (Fish sell for +50%) or Collector (Chance to catch 2 of any fish)
    8 Radon Bobbler
    9 Survey Bobbler
    10 Exquisite Fishing Rod, Master Angler title
