    🆕 Palworld — Getting Started Guide for Beginners

    Welcome, Pal Tamer! This guide will take you from your first spawn to defeating the first tower boss — everything you need to thrive in Palworld's dangerous open world.

    🥇 Your First 15 Minutes

    1️⃣ Wake Up & Gather

    You spawn on a beach with nothing. Immediately: - Punch trees for Wood - Pick up stone from the ground - Collect berries from bushes (food!) - Kill a Lamball for wool and meat (use a stick as a club)

    2️⃣ Build Your First Workbench

    • Press B to open the build menu
    • Place a Primitive Workbench (5 Wood)
    • Craft a Stone Pickaxe (5 Wood, 5 Stone)
    • Craft a Stone Club (5 Wood, 10 Stone) — your first weapon

    3️⃣ Catch Your First Pal

    • Find a Lamball or Cattiva (both common near spawn)
    • Weaken it to ~30% HP with your club
    • Open the Pal Sphere in your inventory
    • Press Q to aim, Left-click to throw
    • Watch the capture animation — success!

    💡 Tip: Aim for the back of the Pal for a higher capture chance!

    4️⃣ Craft Better Gear

    Item Materials Why You Need It
    🔥 Campfire 10 Wood, 5 Stone Cooking food, light
    🛏️ Bed 20 Wood, 5 Fibre, 5 Wool Set spawn, skip night
    🏹 Bow 20 Wood, 10 Stone, 5 Fibre Ranged combat
    🎒 Small Pouch 10 Leather, 5 Fibre +50 carry weight

    🏠 Building Your First Base

    Choosing a Location

    Pick a spot that is: - ✅ Flat — you need room for buildings - ✅ Near water — for Pals that need to water crops - ✅ Near ore/stone — early resource nodes - ❌ Not in a Pal spawn zone — they'll attack your base - ✅ Near a fast travel point — the Starting Plateau is perfect

    Base Building Priority

    Level Build This Purpose
    1 Pal Box (Level 2) This IS your base — place it first
    2 Primitive Workbench Basic crafting
    3 Campfire + Berry Plantation Food production
    4 Wooden Chest × 2 Storage
    5 Feed Box Auto-feed your Pals
    6 Pal Bed × 3–5 Pals sleep to recover SAN

    Pal Assignment Basics

    Once Pals are in your base's Pal Box: 1. Approach a workstation (e.g., Berry Plantation) 2. Press F to open the assignment menu 3. Select a Pal with the matching work suitability 4. The Pal will automatically work when assigned

    💡 Pro Tip: Cattiva is the best early all-rounder — it has Handiwork, Lumbering, Transport, and Gathering. Catch multiple!

    🐾 Catching Pals: Full Mechanics

    Capture Formula

    Capture Chance = Base Rate × (Current HP / Max HP) × Pal Sphere Multiplier × Back Bonus
    Pal Sphere Type Materials Catch Rate Bonus Unlock Level
    🔵 Pal Sphere 3 Paldium Fragment, 1 Wood, 1 Stone 1.0x 1
    🟢 Mega Sphere 5 Paldium, 3 Ingot, 2 Wood 1.5x 14
    🟣 Giga Sphere 8 Paldium, 5 Ingot, 3 Refined Ingot 2.0x 20
    🟡 Hyper Sphere 10 Paldium, 3 Polymer, 5 Carbon Fibre 3.0x 26
    🟠 Ultra Sphere 15 Paldium, 10 Carbon Fibre, 5 Circuit Board 4.0x 35
    ❄️ Legendary Sphere 50 Paldium, 30 Carbon Fibre, 20 Circuit Board 5.0x 44

    Best Early Pals to Catch

    Pal Location Work Skills Why It's Great
    🐑 Lamball Starting area Farming 1 Easy wool for cloth
    🐱 Cattiva Starting area Handiwork 1, Lumbering 1, Transport 1, Gathering 1 Best starter worker
    🐿️ Lifmunk Starting forest Planting 1, Gathering 1, Lumbering 1 Handy for farm work
    🐸 Fuack Ponds/rivers Watering 1 Only early Watering Pal
    🐒 Tanzee Forest woods Planting 1, Handiwork 1, Lumbering 1, Transport 1 Great all-rounder

    ⚔️ Combat & Boss Progression

    Tower Bosses (Main Story)

    Boss Location Recommended Level Weakness
    🟢 Zoe & Grizzbolt Rayne Syndicate Tower 10–15 Ground-type Pals
    🔵 Lily & Lyleen Sealed Realm of the Frozen Wings 20–25 Fire-type Pals
    🟠 Axel & Orserk Mossy Forest Tower 30–35 Ground-type Pals
    🟣 Marcus & Faleris Volcanic Tower 40–45 Water-type Pals
    ⚪ Victor & Shadowbeak Deep Sand Tower 45–50 Dragon-type Pals
    🔴 Bjorn & Bastigor Mountain Tower 50–55 Fire-type Pals

    Combat Tips for Beginners

    • Always bring 5+ cooked food for HP regen
    • Craft a shield (after level 6) — it regenerates
    • Use Pals of the opposite element for 2x damage
    • Dodge roll (spacebar during sprint) — invincibility frames
    • Capture, don't kill — you get XP for captures

    🌱 Farming & Food Production

    Food Production Chain

    1. Level 3: Unlock Berry Plantation
    2. Craft Berry Plantation (20 Wood, 10 Fibre, 5 Stone)
    3. Assign a Fuack or Tanzee to watering/planting
    4. Harvest berries automatically every few minutes
    5. Cook berries at a campfire for +50% hunger restoration

    Early food goes a long way. 2 cooked berries per day keeps your character fed, and Pals eat raw berries from the Feed Box.

    Crop Progression

    Crop Unlock Level Growth Time Use
    🫐 Berry 3 (Default) ~3 min Basic food for Pals + you
    🥕 Carrot 12 ~5 min Better food, breeding ingredient
    🌾 Wheat 15 ~6 min For making Bread (best early food)
    🍅 Tomato 21 ~8 min High-value food, cake ingredient
    🥬 Lettuce 24 ~8 min High-value food, cake ingredient

    🔟 Essential Beginner Tips

    1. Build a Pal Box first — it's your base centre and respawn point
    2. Catch 10 of each Pal — you get bonus XP for 10+ captures
    3. Don't overheat/freeze — craft appropriate armour for each biome
    4. Use Pal Souls to upgrade your Pals at the Pal Statue
    5. Prioritise the Grappling Gun (Level 12) — best mobility tool
    6. Build a Hot Spring (Level 10) — restores Pals' SAN
    7. Never fight bosses at night — harder to see, Pals get sleepy
    8. Breeding unlocks rare Pals — set up a Breeding Farm (Level 19)
    9. Craft a flying mount as soon as possible (Nitewing → Level 15 saddle)
    10. Turn on "Auto Drop Pals to Base" in settings — reduces micro-management
