    Fields of Mistria — NPC Gift Guide & Friendship

    Build relationships with the residents of Mistria! This guide covers every NPC's gift preferences, organized by Loved, Liked, Neutral, Disliked, and Hated items.

    Friendship Tip: Gifting on a character's birthday gives 3× the usual friendship points. Loved gifts on birthdays are the fastest path to max hearts.

    Gift Quality Multiplier

    Quality Friendship Multiplier
    Normal 1.0×
    Silver 1.1×
    Gold 1.25×
    Iridium 1.5×
