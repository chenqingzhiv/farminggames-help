    🌾 Fields of Mistria — Game Overview

    About the Game

    Fields of Mistria is a farming simulation RPG developed by NPC Studio, released in August 2024 via Early Access on Steam. Set in a charming pastoral town recovering from a mysterious earthquake, players inherit a run-down farm and work to restore both the land and the nearby village.

    Key Features

    Feature Details
    🎮 Genre Farming Sim / RPG / Life Sim
    👥 Mode Single-player
    🏭 Developer NPC Studio
    📅 Release August 2024 (Early Access)
    🖥️ Platform PC (Steam)

    🪄 Magic & Skills

    Unlike traditional farming sims, Fields of Mistria introduces a magic system that evolves alongside your farming skills. Players learn spells tied to seasonal elements — water magic for irrigating crops, earth magic for tilling, and fire magic for smithing. The magic system ties directly into skill progression, with higher levels unlocking more powerful enchantments for tools and crops.

    Skill Categories

    • 🌱 Farming — Plant, water, and harvest crops; magic-enhanced yields
    • ⛏️ Mining — Explore the mines beneath Mistria, collect ores and gems
    • 🎣 Fishing — Catch freshwater and ocean fish in various biomes
    • 🌿 Foraging — Gather wild ingredients and discover rare herbs
    • 🧙 Magic — Unlock and upgrade spells through practice and quests

    🏘️ Town & NPCs

    Mistria is home to a cast of fully voiced NPCs with deep relationship mechanics. Each villager has daily routines, seasonal events, and personal story arcs tied to the town's recovery. Building friendships unlocks new spells, recipes, and story progression.

    Tip: Spend your first few days clearing only enough land to plant a small crop patch. Use the rest of your energy exploring town and talking to villagers — early relationships unlock important tool upgrades and magic abilities faster than rushing farm expansion.
