    🌱 Fields of Mistria — Beginner's Guide

    Your First Day

    When you arrive at your overgrown farm in Mistria, the first priority is survival. You'll start with basic tools — a watering can, hoe, axe, and pickaxe — and a small patch of arable land.

    Day 1 Checklist

    • [x] Clear weeds and small debris from 6-8 farm tiles
    • [x] Visit the general store in town for starter seeds
    • [x] Plant your first crops and water them
    • [x] Meet at least 3 villagers to start building relationships
    • [x] Check the town notice board for available quests

    🌾 First Season Strategy

    Spring (Season 1) is about building a foundation. Focus on crops with short grow times — turnips and spring greens mature in 4-5 days and provide quick gold to reinvest. Avoid large fields until you have the stamina to water them daily.

    Recommended Early Crops

    Crop Grow Time Sell Price Notes
    Turnip 4 days 60g Fastest turnaround
    Spring Green 5 days 90g Good early profit
    Tomato 7 days 120g Regrows after harvest

    🧙 Unlocking Magic Early

    Magic unlocks around day 5 after you complete your first town quest. To speed this up:

    1. Complete the "Clearing the Path" quest by removing large debris
    2. Talk to Valen, the town elder, after clearing 10 debris tiles
    3. Accept the Spell Font quest to learn your first water spell
    4. Use magic to water crops — it covers a 3×3 area and saves stamina

    Tip: Always keep 200-300g in reserve for seed purchases. A common beginner mistake is spending all gold on tool upgrades before securing a steady crop income. Prioritize reinvesting in seeds until you have at least 10 planted tiles producing consistently.
