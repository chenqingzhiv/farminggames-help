    🌱 Fields of Mistria — Complete Beginner's Guide

    Your First Day in Mistria

    When you arrive at your overgrown farm in the charming town of Mistria, you will have basic tools — a watering can, hoe, axe, and pickaxe — plus a small patch of arable land covered in weeds and debris. Your first day sets the tone for the entire season, so let's make every minute count.

    Day 1 Checklist

    Step Task Details
    ✅ Clear 6-8 farm tiles Remove small weeds and debris — save large stumps for later
    ✅ Visit general store Buy starter seeds (turnips or spring greens)
    ✅ Plant crops 6-8 plants is the sweet spot for day 1 stamina
    ✅ Water everything Use the basic watering can — each tile consumes stamina
    ✅ Meet 3+ villagers Talk to NPCs you encounter at the store and town square
    ✅ Check notice board Town quests provide early gold and reputation

    Stamina Management

    Your stamina bar is small and depletes fast on day 1. Prioritize:

    1. Clearing enough tiles to plant your starter seeds
    2. Actually planting and watering them
    3. Talking to villagers (costs no stamina)

    Avoid: chopping large stumps, mining, or clearing the entire field on day 1. Those tasks are better saved for after your first harvest when you have food to restore energy.

    Tip: Forage every berry bush and flower you pass. Even if you don't sell them, they serve as early gifts and quest items. Wild berries (restore 15 energy each) are especially valuable for keeping you working longer on day 1-3.

    🌾 First Season Strategy — Spring

    Spring is your foundation season. Every decision you make here affects your summer capabilities.

    Best Early Crops

    Crop Seed Cost Grow Time Sell Price Profit/Day Notes
    Turnip 15g 4 days 60g 11.3g/day Fastest turnaround — ideal for rapid reinvestment
    Spring Green 25g 5 days 90g 13.0g/day Best profit-per-day of basic crops
    Tomato 30g 7 days 120g 12.9g/day Regrows after harvest — plant 3-4 for passive income
    Potato 20g 6 days 75g 9.2g/day Decent all-rounder

    Recommended planting strategy: - Day 1: Plant 4 turnips + 4 spring greens (uses ~1,200g of starter gold) - Day 5: Harvest turnips → buy 6 more spring greens - Day 10: Harvest everything → reinvest in tomatoes + more spring greens - Day 15+: You should have 12-15 tiles producing consistently

    Money-Making Outside Crops

    Method Daily Income Requirements Notes
    🌿 Foraging 200-400g None Berries, flowers, seasonal herbs
    🎣 Fishing 300-700g Fishing rod (800g from general store) Requires practice but pays well
    ⛏️ Mining 400-900g Pickaxe (free starter tool) Ores and gems from mines
    📋 Town Quests 150-500g per quest Check notice board daily Easy reputation + money
    🗑️ Trash 50-150g None Recycled items from town bins

    🧙 Unlocking Magic (Days 3-7)

    Magic is Fields of Mistria's unique feature — the ability to cast spells that help with farming, combat, and exploration.

    How to Unlock

    Step Action Requirement
    1 Complete "Clearing the Path" Remove 10 large debris items from your farm
    2 Talk to Valen (town elder) Visit her house east of the town square after step 1
    3 Complete "Spell Font" quest Collect 5 mana crystals from the mine (floors 1-5)
    4 Reward: Water Spell Water a 3×3 area in one cast — massive stamina saver

    First Magic Tips

    • The Water Spell is your biggest quality-of-life unlock in Spring — it reduces watering time by 60%
    • Check the Mana Shrine in the forest west of town weekly — it refills your mana
    • Mana crystals glow faintly in mine walls; break every suspicious rock
    • Always carry 20+ energy worth of food when mining for mana crystals

    Tip: Rush the Spell Font quest. The Water Spell turns a 15-minute watering session into a 4-minute one. That saved time can go directly into mining or fishing, boosting your income by 40-50% in the second half of Spring.

    ⛏️ Mining in Your First Season

    The Mistria mines are unlockable around day 4-5. Enter through the cave in the northwest ridge of town.

    Floor Progression Goals

    Floors Ores Found Goal Unlock
    1-5 Copper, Stone Get 10 copper + 5 mana crystals First tool upgrade + Water Spell
    6-10 Copper, Iron Collect 15 iron for pickaxe upgrade Break mid-tier rocks on farm
    11-15 Iron, Silver Deep mine access Better weapons and tools

    Combat Basics

    The mines contain slimes, bats, and cave spiders. Your starter sword is weak but enough for floors 1-5:

    1. Kite enemies — hit, dodge back, hit again
    2. Eat food between fights — don't let HP drop below 50%
    3. Save monster drops — slime goo and bat wings are used in early crafting recipes
    4. Use the elevator — every 5 floors has a save point

    🏠 First Season Milestones

    Timeframe Goal Why It Matters
    Day 1-5 Plant 8+ tiles, visit town, start mining Foundation for all income
    Day 5-10 Copper pickaxe upgrade, unlock magic Double mining speed, save stamina
    Day 10-15 15+ crop tiles, visit mine floor 10 Consistent daily income
    Day 15-20 Iron tools, mining floor 15 Unlock mid-game content
    Day 20-28 Save 5,000g for house upgrade Unlock cooking and kitchen

    Common Beginner Mistakes

    Mistake Why It Hurts Fix
    Ignoring the notice board Misses easy gold and reputation Check it every single day
    Selling all monster drops Blocks crafting progression Keep 5-10 of each type before selling
    Over-clearing farm day 1 Waste stamina you need for planting Clear only 6-8 tiles initially
    Not saving for house upgrade Cooked meals are 3x more stamina-efficient Set aside 200g/day starting week 2

    Final tip: Fields of Mistria rewards steady, balanced progress over sprinting. Focus on getting magic unlocked by day 7, a copper pickaxe by day 10, and 15+ crop tiles before summer. If you hit those three milestones, you'll enter Summer with 3,000-4,000g in savings and iron-tier tools — a fantastic position for season two.
