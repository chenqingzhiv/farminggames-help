    📋 Complete Community Center Bundle Reference

    Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6.15 | Every bundle, every item, every reward — quick reference format

    A compact, printable reference for all Community Center bundles. Use this alongside the interactive Bundle Tracker for planning your completion run.

    Data sourced from Stardew Valley Wiki v1.6

    🌸 Spring Foraging Bundle

    Item Quantity Where to Find
    Wild Horseradish 1 Farm, Forest — Spring foraging
    Daffodil 1 Pelican Town — Spring foraging
    Leek 1 Mountain — Spring foraging
    Dandelion 1 Farm, Forest — Spring foraging

    Reward: 🧑‍🌾 30 Spring Seeds (3 packets of 10)

    ☀️ Summer Foraging Bundle

    Item Quantity Where to Find
    Grape 1 Forest — Summer foraging
    Spice Berry 1 Farm, Bus Stop — Summer foraging
    Sweet Pea 1 Farm, Forest — Summer foraging

    Reward: 🧑‍🌾 30 Summer Seeds

    🍂 Fall Foraging Bundle

    Item Quantity Where to Find
    Common Mushroom 1 Secret Woods, Farm Cave — Fall foraging
    Wild Plum 1 Farm, Forest — Fall foraging
    Hazelnut 1 Farm, Mountain — Fall foraging
    Blackberry 1 Farm, Forest — Fall foraging (Blackberry Season: Fall 8-11)

    Reward: 🧑‍🌾 30 Fall Seeds

    ❄️ Winter Foraging Bundle

    Item Quantity Where to Find
    Winter Root 1 Farm, Mountain — Winter foraging
    Crystal Fruit 1 Mines, Farm — Winter foraging
    Snow Yam 1 Mountain — Winter foraging (dig with hoe)
    Crocus 1 Pelican Town — Winter foraging

    Reward: 🧑‍🌾 30 Winter Seeds

    🌳 Construction Bundle (Pantry)

    Item Quantity Where to Find
    Wood 99 Chop trees anywhere
    Stone 99 Mine rocks
    Hardwood 10 Large stumps in Secret Woods (daily)

    Reward: 💧 Charcoal Kiln — turn 10 Wood into 1 Coal

    🧺 Pantry Bundles

    Spring Crops Bundle

    Item Quantity Notes
    Parsnip 5 4 days to grow, 20g seed
    Green Bean 5 Trellis, 10 days + regrowth
    Cauliflower 5 12 days, can be giant
    Potato 5 6 days, chance for multiple

    Reward: 💪 Speed-Gro (20)

    Summer Crops Bundle

    Item Quantity Notes
    Tomato 5 11 days + regrowth
    Hot Pepper 5 5 days + regrowth
    Blueberry 5 13 days + regrowth — best summer crop
    Melon 5 12 days, giant crop possible

    Reward: 💪 Quality Speed-Gro (20)

    Fall Crops Bundle

    Item Quantity Notes
    Corn 5 Spans Summer→Fall
    Eggplant 5 7 days + regrowth
    Pumpkin 5 13 days, giant crop
    Yam 5 10 days

    Reward: 🐔 Autumn's Bounty (recipe) — Cooking

    Quality Crops Bundle

    Item Quantity Quality Required
    Parsnip 5 Gold (or higher)
    Melon 5 Gold
    Corn 5 Gold
    Pumpkin 5 Gold

    Alternative: Any 5 Gold-quality crops from the same season work. Best to grow extra Melons (Summer) or Pumpkins (Fall) with Quality Fertilizer.

    Reward: 💧 Preserves Jar (recipe)

    Animal Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Large Milk 1 Happy cow (Barn)
    Large Egg 1 Happy chicken (Coop)
    Large Goat Milk 1 Happy goat (Barn)
    Wool 1 Rabbit or Sheep
    Duck Egg 1 Happy duck (Coop)

    Reward: 🌀 Cheese Press (recipe) — 1,000g value

    Artisan Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Truffle Oil 1 Pig truffle → Oil Maker
    Cloth 1 Wool → Loom
    Goat Cheese 1 Goat Milk → Cheese Press
    Cheese 1 Large Milk → Cheese Press
    Honey 1 Bee House
    Jelly 1 Preserves Jar
    Apple 1 Apple Tree (Fall)
    Apricot 1 Apricot Tree (Spring)
    Orange 1 Orange Tree (Summer)
    Peach 1 Peach Tree (Summer)
    Pomegranate 1 Pomegranate Tree (Fall)
    Cherry 1 Cherry Tree (Spring)

    Only 6 of the above 12 are needed.

    Reward: 🪣 Keg (recipe) — the most important reward in the game

    ⛏️ Blacksmith's Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Copper Bar 1 Smelt 5 Copper Ore
    Iron Bar 1 Smelt 5 Iron Ore
    Gold Bar 1 Smelt 5 Gold Ore

    Reward: 🧨 Furnace (recipe) — 1,500g value. Note: You already get a Furnace from Clint's tutorial, but this is a backup.

    Alternate: If you chose Joja Mart, this bundle costs 2,500g to buy out.

    🏭 Geologist's Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Quartz 1 Any mine floor
    Earth Crystal 1 Frozen/Magma Geodes
    Frozen Tear 1 Ice levels (41-79)
    Fire Quartz 1 Magma levels (80-119)

    Reward: 💎 Omni Geode x5

    🔬 Adventurer's Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Slime (Slime Ball) 99 Slimes drop (blue/green)
    Bat Wing 10 Bats in Mines (31-79)
    Solar Essence 1 Ghosts, Metal Heads
    Void Essence 1 Serpents, Shadows, Skeletons

    Reward: 🧪 Crab Pot (recipe) — infinite crab pots

    Tip: Slime (99) is the bottleneck here. Kill green slimes on floors 1-19, or farm a Slime Hutch. Bat Wings and Solar/Void Essence will accumulate naturally during mining runs.

    💧 Fish Tank Bundles

    River Fish Bundle

    Item Quantity Best Time/Location
    Sunfish 1 Spring, River, 6AM-7PM
    Catfish 1 Spring/Fall, River, Rain
    Shad 1 Spring/Summer/Fall, River, Rain
    Tiger Trout 1 Summer/Fall, Creek/Lake

    Reward: 🎣 Bait (30)

    Lake Fish Bundle

    Item Quantity Best Time/Location
    Largemouth Bass 1 All seasons, Lake
    Carp 1 All seasons, Lake
    Bullhead 1 All seasons, Lake
    Sturgeon 1 Summer/Winter, Lake, can be tricky

    Reward: 🎣 Dressed Spinner — fishing tackle

    Ocean Fish Bundle

    Item Quantity Best Time/Location
    Sardine 1 Spring/Fall/Winter, Ocean
    Tuna 1 Summer/Winter, Ocean
    Red Snapper 1 Summer/Fall, Ocean, Rain
    Tilapia 1 Summer/Fall, Ocean
    Halibut 1 Spring/Summer/Fall, Ocean, 6AM-11AM

    Reward: 🪝 Warp Totem: Beach (recipe)

    Night Fishing Bundle

    Item Quantity Time
    Walleye 1 Fall, Rain, 12PM-2AM, River/Lake
    Bream 1 All seasons, 6PM-2AM, River
    Eel 1 Spring/Fall, Rain, 4PM-2AM, Ocean

    Reward: 🧲 Magnet (recipe) — for fishing treasure chests

    Crab Pot Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Lobster 1 Crab Pot (Ocean)
    Crab 1 Crab Pot (Ocean) or rock crabs in Mines
    Cockle 1 Beach foraging
    Mussel 1 Beach foraging / Crab Pot
    Shrimp 1 Crab Pot (Ocean)
    Snail 1 Freshwater Crab Pot
    Periwinkle 1 Freshwater Crab Pot
    Oyster 1 Beach foraging / Crab Pot

    Only 5 of the above 8 are needed.

    Reward: 🦀 Crab Pot (3) — 3 free crab pots

    Specialty Fish Bundle (1.6 Added)

    Item Quantity Source
    Scorpion Carp 1 Desert, any time
    Ghostfish 1 Mines (20, 60 — glowing ring floors)
    Sandfish 1 Desert, any time
    Woodskip 1 Secret Woods pond
    Glacierfish 1 Winter, Arrowhead Island, 6AM-2AM
    Legend 1 Spring, Rain, Mountain Lake

    Reward: 🎁 Stardrop — +34 max energy (permanent)

    🏠 Boiler Room Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Copper Bar 3 Smelt 15 Copper Ore
    Iron Bar 3 Smelt 15 Iron Ore
    Gold Bar 1 Smelt 5 Gold Ore

    Reward: 🚐 Minecart Repair — fast travel to/from the Mines, Bus Stop, Blacksmith, and Town

    🌲 Vault Room (Money Bundles)

    Bundle Cost Reward
    2,500g Bundle 2,500g 🪙 Gold
    5,000g Bundle 5,000g 🪙 Chocolate Cake (recipe)
    10,000g Bundle 10,000g 🪙 Diamond
    25,000g Bundle 25,000g 🪙 Crystalarium (recipe) — infinite gem duplication

    Total: 42,500g — unlock the bus repair before completing this.

    🦉 Bulletin Board (Friendship Bundles)

    Chef's Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Maple Syrup 1 Maple Tree + Tapper
    Fiddlehead Fern 1 Secret Woods (Summer)
    Truffle 1 Pig (Barn)
    Poppy 1 Summer crop
    Maki Roll 1 Cooking (any fish + rice + seaweed)
    Fried Egg 1 Cooking (egg + oil)

    Only 4 of the above 6 are needed.

    Reward: 🎛️ Stove (recipe) — a kitchen for your house faster

    Dye Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Red Mushroom 1 Secret Woods, Mines
    Sea Urchin 1 Beach foraging
    Duck Feather 1 Happy duck (rare)
    Aquamarine 1 Any geode
    Red Cabbage 1 Summer crop — Year 2+ from Pierre
    Saffron (1.6) 1 Spring/Fall crop

    Only 4 of the above 6 are needed.

    Reward: 🎨 Seed Maker (recipe) — essential for Starfruit and Sweet Gem Berry propagation

    Field Research Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Purple Mushroom 1 Mines 80+
    Nautilus Shell 1 Beach foraging (Winter)
    Chub 1 Mountain Lake, all seasons
    Frozen Geode 1 Mine floors 41-79

    Reward: 🧴 Glowstone Ring (recipe)

    Fodder Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Wheat 10 Summer/Fall crop
    Hay 10 Scythe grass
    Apple 3 Apple Tree (Fall)

    Need to build a Silo first to store Hay.

    Reward: 🥕 Heater (recipe) — keeps barn animals happy in winter

    Enchanter's Bundle

    Item Quantity Source
    Oak Resin 1 Oak Tree + Tapper
    Wine 1 Keg
    Rainbow Shell 1 Beach foraging (Summer)
    Taro Tuber 1 Ginger Island
    Cactus Fruit 1 Calico Desert
    Coconut 1 Calico Desert

    Only 4 of the above 6 are needed.

    Reward: 🌟 Gold Bar (5) + unlocks Cindersap Forest foraging

    🏆 Gold Clock Tier — Rewards Summary

    Bundle Room Completed Reward
    Pantry (Complete) 🪄 Greenhouse — unlimited crops year-round
    Crafts Room (Complete) 🪓 Bridge Repair — access to Quarry
    Fish Tank (Complete) 🗿 Glittering Boulder Removed — opens up Mountain Lake area
    Blacksmith (Complete) 🔥 Minecart Service — fast travel
    Boiler Room (Complete) 🚐 Minecart Repair
    Vault (Complete) 🚌 Bus Repair — access to Calico Desert
    Bulletin Board (Complete) 🍭 Stardrop — permanent +34 energy
    ALL Bundles Complete 🥚 ?? (Secret) — endgame content + access to >!Spicy Eels!<

    📊 Bundle Difficulty Ranking

    Rank Bundle Bottleneck Item Difficulty
    🟢 Spring Foraging — (free) Easy
    🟢 Summer Foraging — (free) Easy
    🟢 Fall Foraging Blackberry (wait for Fall 8) Easy
    🟢 Winter Foraging Snow Yam (dig, not pick) Easy
    🟢 Construction Hardwood (Secret Woods) Easy
    🟡 Spring Crops Time-gated to Spring Medium
    🟡 Summer Crops Must wait for Summer Medium
    🟡 Fall Crops Must wait for Fall Medium
    🟡 Crab Pot Need Fiberglass Rod first Medium
    🔴 Animal Large Goat Milk (need upgraded Barn) Hard
    🔴 Artisan Fruit tree fruit + Tapper (time-gated) Hard
    🔴 Quality Crops Gold-quality Melon (needs fertilizer) Hard
    🔴 Enchanter's Oak Resin (28 days from Tapper) Hard
    🔴 Chef's Truffle (need Pig → Deluxe Barn) Hard
    🔴 Specialty Fish Legend (Spring, Rain, Level 10 Fishing) Expert

    Tip: Complete the Artisan Bundle as early as possible. The Keg recipe is the single most valuable bundle reward in the game. Plant your Spring fruit trees (Apricot, Cherry) on Day 1 of Year 1 and put Tappers on Oak Trees immediately — you'll need Oak Resin anyway.
