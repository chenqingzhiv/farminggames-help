    Top 5 Keg / Preserves Profit

    Crop Base Price Product Product Price Multiplier
    Starfruit ⭐ 750g Starfruit Wine 🍷 2250g 3x
    Melon 🍈 250g Melon Wine 🍷 750g 3x
    Pumpkin 🎃 320g Pumpkin Juice 🧃 720g 2.25x
    Hops 🌿 25g Pale Ale 🍺 300g 12x
    Wheat 🌾 10g Beer 🍺 200g 20x

    Hops are the Keg efficiency king! Though the base price is low, they're harvested daily, take 11 days to mature, then produce every day — a year-round Keg assembly line.

    Note: Fruits go into Kegs to make Wine (3x base price). Vegetables go into Kegs to make Juice (2.25x base price). Pumpkin is a vegetable, so it produces Pumpkin Juice, not wine. Blueberries (fruit) produce Blueberry Wine at 150g (50g × 3).

    Greenhouse Planting Recommendations

    Priority Crop Reason
    1 Starfruit ⭐ Grow year-round, Keg and sell
    2 Ancient Fruit 🍇 Single seed, regrows every 7 days forever
    3 Hops 🌿 Daily harvest, huge Keg profit
    4 Coffee Bean ☕ Mass plant, drink coffee daily for speed boost
    5 Blueberry 🫐 Regrows, low effort
    6 Cranberry 🫐 Regrows, winter supplement

    💡 Beginner Tip: In the first year, Spring is mainly about making money by fishing. Only grow enough parsnips to complete the bundle, save your gold for Strawberries (buy at Egg Festival on Spring 13).

    💡 Intermediate Tip: Once you unlock the Greenhouse, immediately plant Starfruit + Hops in it. With Kegs, earning over 10k/day is easy.

    💡 1.6 New Crop Tip: Seed Spot crops (Carrot, Summer Squash, Broccoli, Powdermelon) are bonus income — no gold investment needed. Powdermelon in Winter is the only seasonal Winter crop and can form giant crops!
