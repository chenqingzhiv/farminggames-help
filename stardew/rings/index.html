    💍 Complete Ring Catalog

    Rings provide passive buffs and are one of the most important equipment slots in Stardew Valley. In version 1.6 you can equip two rings and combine them at the Forge on Ginger Island for powerful dual effects. This guide covers every ring in the game with detailed data.

    ⚔️ Combat Ring Deep Dive

    S-Tier Rings (Must-Have)

    Ring Why S-Tier Best Combo
    Iridium Band Provides glow + magnet + 10% damage all in one slot — the most versatile ring in the game Combine with Lucky Ring or Burglar's Ring
    Slime Charmer Ring Makes slimes completely harmless — essential for Skull Cavern runs and Slime Hutch Combine with Iridium Band
    Burglar's Ring Doubles monster loot drops — critical for farming coal, iridium, and rare items Combine with Iridium Band
    Napalm Ring Enemies explode on death, clearing nearby rocks and monsters — massive QoL in Skull Cavern Combine with Burglar's Ring
    Lucky Ring +1 Luck affects everything — better drops, fishing, mining, and casino Combine with Iridium Band

    🧪 Combined Ring Recipes (Forge)

    At the Volcano Forge on Ginger Island, you can combine two rings using 20 Cinder Shards. The combined ring keeps both effects.

    Best Combos Primary Ring Secondary Ring Why
    🥇 Iridium Band Lucky Ring Best all-purpose — damage + luck + light + magnet
    🥇 Iridium Band Burglar's Ring Best for loot farming — double drops + damage
    🥇 Iridium Band Slime Charmer Best for slime hutch / Skull Cavern comfort
    🥈 Napalm Ring Burglar's Ring Speedrunner build — clear floors fast
    🥈 Iridium Band Hot Java Ring Coffee + damage = infinite energy
    🥈 Iridium Band Phoenix Ring Survival build — never die
    🥉 Savage Ring Napalm Ring Speed + explosion — ultimate floor clearing

    🎯 How to Farm Specific Rings

    Burglar's Ring

    Goal:      Kill 500 Dust Sprites
Location:  Mines (Floors 40-79)
Strategy:  Run floors 45-55 repeatedly
           Wear Burglar's Ring when farming for Dust Sprites!
Reward:    Adventurer's Guild — best loot ring in the game
Difficulty: ⭐⭐ (Easy to farm in one day with level 10+ combat)

    Slime Charmer Ring

    Goal:      Kill 1,000 Slimes
Location:  Mines + Secret Woods + Slime Hutch
Strategy:  Use Slime Incubator at home for infinite slime spawns
           Floor 5 + 15 of Mines have the highest density
Reward:    Adventurer's Guild — essential for Skull Cavern
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (Takes patience but worth it)

    Napalm Ring

    Goal:      Kill 250 Serpents
Location:  Skull Cavern
Strategy:  Reach floor 50+ in Skull Cavern where serpents are dense
           Use stairs aggressively
Reward:    Adventurer's Guild — game-changer for Skull Cavern
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Requires Skull Cavern access)

    Lucky Ring

    Goal:      Find through fishing or panning
Method:    Fish in deep ocean with Magnet tackle
           Pan in the river (Mountain Lake river)
           Check Traveling Cart (rare)
Drop Rate: ~0.8% from Fishing Treasure
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Pure RNG)

    💡 Pro Tips

    1. Always wear two rings — you have two slots, use them!
    2. Iridium Band is the foundation — craft it as soon as you can
    3. Combine rings at the Volcano Forge — this doubles your effective ring power
    4. Burglar's Ring before skull caverns — doubles iridium ore drops
    5. Slime Charmer makes Skull Cavern much easier — prioritize it
    6. Napalm Ring saves time and bombs — ultimate QoL for deep diving
    7. Keep a Lucky Ring in your inventory — swap it in for important activities (mining, fishing, artifact digging)
