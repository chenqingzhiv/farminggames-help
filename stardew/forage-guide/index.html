    🌿 Complete Forage Guide

    Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6.15 | All forageables, wild seeds, mushroom logs, and foraging strategies

    Foraging is one of the most accessible income sources in Year 1 and remains relevant throughout the game. This guide covers every forageable item, wild seed mechanics, mushroom log production, and profession-based profit optimization.

    Data sourced from Stardew Valley Wiki v1.6

    📊 Forageables by Season

    Spring Forageables

    Item Sell Price Iridium Location Used In
    Wild Horseradish 🌱 50g 100g Farm, Forest Spring Seeds
    Daffodil 🌼 30g 60g Pelican Town Spring Seeds
    Leek 🧅 60g 120g Mountain Spring Seeds, Salad
    Dandelion 🌸 40g 80g Farm, Forest Spring Seeds, Salad
    Salmonberry 🫐 5g 10g Bushes (Salmonberry Season!) —
    Morel 🍄 150g 300g Secret Woods Spring Seeds, Fried Mushroom

    Salmonberry Season: Spring 15-18. Bushes across the map yield 5 Salmonberries per bush per day. With ~40 bushes, you can collect 600+ berries across the 4 days. Base profit is only 5g/berry, but with Botanist (Iridium quality) they sell for 10g each.

    Summer Forageables

    Item Sell Price Iridium Location Used In
    Spice Berry 🌶️ 80g 160g Farm, Bus Stop, Forest Summer Seeds
    Sweet Pea 🌸 60g 120g Farm, Forest Summer Seeds
    Grape 🍇 80g 160g Forest Summer Seeds, Keg (240g Wine)
    Red Mushroom 🍄 75g 150g Mines, Secret Woods Life Elixir
    Fiddlehead Fern 🌿 90g 180g Secret Woods Summer Seeds, Fiddlehead Risotto

    Tip: Summer grape is often overlooked — foraging grapes can be kegged into wine (240g base). A single stack of 10 iridium grapes = 1,600g sold raw, or 2,400g as wine. But with Botanist quality bonuses, selling raw is simpler unless you have spare kegs.

    Fall Forageables

    Item Sell Price Iridium Location Used In
    Blackberry 🫐 20g 40g Farm, Forest (Blackberry Season!) Fall Seeds, Blackberry Cobbler
    Common Mushroom 🍄 40g 80g Secret Woods, Farm Cave Fall Seeds, Stir Fry, Fried Mushroom
    Hazelnut 🌰 70g 140g Farm, Mountain Fall Seeds, Roasted Hazelnuts
    Chanterelle 🍄 160g 320g Secret Woods, Farm Cave Fall Seeds
    Purple Mushroom 🍄 250g 500g Mines (80+) Life Elixir, Purple Mushroom Cave

    Blackberry Season: Fall 8-11. Same mechanic as Salmonberry — ~40 bushes, 4 days, 800+ berries total. Blackberries sell for 20g base (40g iridium) vs Salmonberry's 5g — more worthwhile to collect.

    Blackberry vs Salmonberry: Blackberries at iridium quality (40g) are 4× more valuable than iridium Salmonberries (10g). Prioritize Blackberry Season collection, especially if you've taken Botanist.

    Winter Forageables

    Item Sell Price Iridium Location Used In
    Winter Root 🌱 70g 140g Farm, Mountain Winter Seeds
    Crystal Fruit 💎 150g 300g Mines, Farm Winter Seeds
    Snow Yam 🍠 100g 200g Mountain Winter Seeds
    Crocus 🌷 60g 120g Pelican Town Winter Seeds
    Holly 🌿 80g 160g Farm, Forest Winter Seeds (NOT wild seeds)

    Tip: Winter foraging with the Botanist profession is the most profitable foraging season. Crystal Fruit at iridium quality sells for 300g each and can be found on almost any open ground tile plus mine levels 40-80.

    🔄 Crops from Wild Seeds

    Wild Seeds (grown from Seasonal Wild Seeds packets) produce forage-quality items that benefit from the Tillager (crops) profession, not Botanist. This makes them unique — they're the only forage-type items that scale with Level 10 Farming (Tiller), not Foraging.

    Season Seed Source Grows Yield Profit/Tile/Day
    🌱 Spring Seeds 1 Wild Horseradish + 1 Daffodil + 1 Leek + 1 Dandelion = 10 Seeds 7 days 1 per harvest 20.55g/day (Tiller + Iridium)
    ☀️ Summer Seeds 1 Spice Berry + 1 Sweet Pea + 1 Summer Grape = 10 Seeds 7 days 1 per harvest 24.38g/day (Tiller + Iridium)
    🍂 Fall Seeds 1 Common Mushroom + 1 Blackberry + 1 Hazelnut + 1 Chanterelle = 10 Seeds 7 days 1 per harvest 25.62g/day (Tiller + Iridium)
    ❄️ Winter Seeds 1 Winter Root + 1 Crystal Fruit + 1 Snow Yam + 1 Crocus = 10 Seeds 7 days 1 per harvest 22.19g/day (Tiller + Iridium)

    Key Insight: Wild Seeds cost nothing to craft (four foraged items → 10 seeds). The seeds sell for the same price as crafted goods — crafting is always better than selling the ingredients. One Spring Wild Seed packet crafted from 4 foraged items (worth ~180g total) produces 10 seeds worth 350g planted, or can be sold for 350g immediately.

    Wild Seed Profit Ranking

    Rank Season Profit/Seed (Tiller) Notes
    🥇 Fall 25.62g/day Chanterelle baseline value makes these powerful
    🥈 Summer 24.38g/day Spice Berry + Grape are already valuable
    🥉 Winter 22.19g/day Crystal Fruit sells well even without Tiller
    4th Spring 20.55g/day Wild Horseradish is the weak link

    Tip: Winter Seeds are uniquely valuable because Crystal Fruit (iridium = 300g) is a high-value gift loved by several NPCs. Grow winter seeds in Garden Pots in your greenhouse or Ginger Island for year-round Crystal Fruit production.

    🪵 Mushroom Logs (1.6)

    Mushroom Logs were introduced in 1.6 and produce wild mushrooms based on nearby trees. They're a passive income source that scales with tree density.

    What Mushroom Logs Produce

    Nearby Wild Trees Possible Outputs Frequency
    0-1 trees Common Mushroom, Red Mushroom Common
    2-5 trees + Purple Mushroom, Morel Mixed
    6-10 trees + Chanterelle, Morel (increased chance) Good
    10+ trees All mushroom types, more high-tier Best

    Profit Analysis (Mushroom Log)

    Trees Nearby Avg Daily Profit/Log Notes
    0-1 ~15g/day Not worth building
    2-5 ~50g/day Decent with fruit tree adjacency
    6-10 ~100-120g/day Good — plant standard trees
    10+ ~150-180g/day Excellent — tap-and-log synergy

    Tip: Each Mushroom Log produces every 4 days (on average) regardless of season. Placing them next to a cluster of 10+ Maple/Oak/Pine trees maximizes their output. Each log costs 10 Hardwood + 10 Moss — you'll need a hardwood farm (Secret Woods daily) to mass-produce these.

    🧑‍🌾 Profession Impact on Foraging

    Profession Effect Best For
    Level 5: Gatherer 20% chance double harvest Any foraging build
    Level 10: Botanist All foraged items are Iridium quality 🏆 Maximum profit from raw forage
    Level 10: Tracker Reveal forage locations on map Completionists, artifact hunting

    Botanist vs. Tiller for Wild Seeds

    Scenario Botanist (sell raw) Tiller (grow seeds)
    Single Spring foraged stack 1.6× price (iridium) N/A
    10 Spring Seeds grown N/A 20.55g/day/tile
    Best overall strategy Sell foraged items raw Grow wild seeds in spring, sell raw other seasons

    Optimal Strategy: Take Gatherer (L5) → Botanist (L10) at Level 10 foraging. Craft wild seeds from foraged items in bulk, grow them in Spring, then sell raw forage at iridium quality for the rest of the year. If you need Life Elixirs (Purple/Red Mushrooms), remember to stockpile some before selling.

    🗺️ Best Foraging Routes by Season

    Spring — Salmonberry Blitz (Days 15-18)

    Route: Bus Stop → Farm → Forest → Mountain — hit all Salmonberry bushes. Collect 150+ berries per day.

    Summer — Secret Woods + Forest

    Route: Farm → Secret Woods (Fiddlehead Fern, Red Mushroom, Morel) → Forest (Grape, Spice Berry). Repeat every 3 days.

    Fall — Blackberry Harvest (Days 8-11)

    Same route as Salmonberry. Collect 200+ blackberries per day. These are better than Salmonberries — worth the time.

    Winter — Crystal Fruit Express

    Route: Mountain (Snow Yams, Crystal Fruit) → Bus Stop → Farm → Town (Crocus). Crystal Fruit is your money maker. Also check mine floors 40-80 for Crystal Fruit nodes.
