    🎩 Complete Hat Collection Guide

    Hats are purely cosmetic headwear in Stardew Valley — they provide no stat benefits but are essential for fashion-conscious farmers and completionists. There are 86+ hats available in Stardew Valley 1.6, acquired through events, shops, fishing treasure, monsters, and secret quests.

    Data from Stardew Valley Wiki v1.6

    💡 Hat Collection Tips

    1. Check the Hat Mouse weekly — new stock appears as you progress
    2. Fish with Magnet tackle — increases treasure chest spawn chance for rare hats
    3. Farm Dust Sprites (Floors 40-79) — best spot for Adventurer's Hat
    4. Search all trash cans daily — Trash Can Hat is a rare but real drop
    5. Complete the Museum — unlocks the prestigious Golden Helmet
    6. Crack every Golden Coconut — Deluxe Pirate Hat is a top-tier flex
    7. Kill Wilderness Golems — 1 in 100,000 chance for Living Hat (late-game grind)
