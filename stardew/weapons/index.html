    ⚔️ Complete Weapons & Combat Guide

    Weapons are essential for surviving the Mines, Skull Cavern, and Volcano Dungeon. This guide covers every weapon in Stardew Valley 1.6, including swords, clubs, daggers, and slingshots, with full stats, acquisition methods, and ratings.

    📊 Slingshots

    Slingshots use ammunition (stones, ores, or gems) and are best for triggering explosive ammo. Tricky to aim on console/controller.

    Weapon Damage (Stone) Damage (Explosive) How to Get Value Rating
    Slingshot 5–10 per hit 20–30 AoE Mines F40 reward 500g ⭐⭐⭐
    Master Slingshot 5–10 per hit 20–30 AoE Mines F80 reward 1,000g ⭐⭐⭐⭐

    Best ammo: Explosive Ammo (1 Copper Ore + 1 Iron Ore + 1 Coal) for mining and crowd control.

    ⭐ Best Weapons by Category

    🥇 Best Overall: Infinity Sword

    • Damage: 80–100 | Speed: +6 | Bonuses: +4% crit rate, +25 knockback
    • Requires: Galaxy Sword + 3 Galaxy Souls (Qi's Walnut Room, 50 Qi Gems each)
    • S-tier for all content, especially Skull Cavern and Volcano.

    🥇 Best Club: Infinity Club

    • Damage: 90–110 | Speed: +2 | Bonuses: +4% crit rate, massive knockback
    • Best for knockback-heavy combat and crowd control.

    🥇 Best Dagger: Infinity Dagger

    • Damage: 65–85 | Speed: +8 | Bonuses: +8% crit rate
    • Best for crit-fishing builds using the Acrobat profession.

    🥇 Best Early Game: Lava Katana

    • Damage: 55–64 | Speed: +0 | Cost: 25,000g at Adventurer's Guild
    • Buy this as soon as you can afford it — carries you to the Skull Cavern.

    🔧 Forge Enchantments (Ginger Island)

    Once you reach the Volcano Forge on Ginger Island, you can add enchantments to weapons using Cinder Shards:

    Enchantment Effect Best For
    Artful Reduces special move cooldown by 50% Clubs (slam spam)
    Bugs +50% damage to bugs Insect Head users
    Crusader +50% damage to mummies, ghosts, void spirits, skeletons Skull Cavern runs
    Haymaker Enemies drop hay 50% of the time Passive farming
    Vampiric 9% chance to heal 10% of damage dealt Survivability builds
    Powerful +15% weapon damage General DPS increase
    Swift +5 speed Daggers (max speed)
