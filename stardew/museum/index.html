    🏛️ Museum Donation Guide — Complete Checklist

    The Stardew Valley Museum & Library, run by Gunther, accepts donations of artifacts and minerals. Completing the full collection unlocks rare rewards including the Rusty Key, Dwarf Scroll translation, Stardrops, and more. There are 95 total items to donate: 42 minerals and 53 artifacts.

    🗺️ Farming Strategy by Location

    Town & Bus Stop

    • Best artifact spots: Outside Pierre's, near Community Center, Bus Stop path
    • Target artifacts: Ancient Doll, Chicken Statue, Chipped Amphora, Rusty items
    • Tip: Check daily — the Bus Stop area has a high spawn rate

    Mountains & Railroad

    • Best artifact spots: Near Robin's house, around the lake, Railroad tracks
    • Target artifacts: Prehistoric skeleton set, Dwarf Scrolls, Arrowhead
    • Tip: Mountain artifacts are the hardest to find — use a Magnet Ring + Burglar Ring

    Cindersap Forest & Secret Woods

    • Best artifact spots: South of Marnie's, near the Sewer entrance, Secret Woods
    • Target artifacts: Ancient Seed (rare), Fern, Prehistoric Handaxe
    • Tip: The Sewer entrance area is the most reliable spot for Ancient Seeds

    Beach

    • Best artifacts: Fishing treasure (Anchor, Casket, Excalibur, Nose Ornament)
    • Tip: Pirate profession (Fishing level 10) doubles treasure chance — farm here for rare artifacts

    Desert

    • Best artifacts: Golden Mask, Golden Relic, Palmtop Computer — only found here
    • Tip: Artifact spots in the Desert are rare but always give unique Desert-only items

    Ginger Island

    • Best artifacts: Tropical Fossil, Yellow Curly Fossil, Golden Walnuts
    • Tip: Dig everywhere — Ginger Island has unique fossils for the Island Field Office

    🎯 Priority Donation Order

    1. Ancient Seed → Donate → Get Ancient Seed recipe & Plantable Ancient Fruit seed
    2. Dwarf Scrolls I–IV → Donate → Learn Dwarvish to talk to Dwarf
    3. Prismatic Shard → First one: get Galaxy Sword, second: donate
    4. Dinosaur Egg → Donate (or incubate first, DONATE the second one)
    5. Rusty Key (5 items) → Unlocks Sewers, Krobus, and Shadow Guy drops
    6. Complete 50 items → Stardrop! (+34 max energy)
    7. Complete 95 items (All) → Final Stardrop + Tree Sapling reward

    🔧 Quick Tips

    • Geodes: Take them ALL to Clint. Open in stacks and check for missing minerals
    • Dwarf Scrolls: Kill Blue Slimes (F1–40) for Scroll I & III, Dust Sprites (F40–79) for Scroll II, and anything deep (F80+) for Scroll IV
    • Fishing: The Pirate profession at level 10 doubles treasure frequency — respec at the Dog Statue if needed
    • Artifact spots respawn daily — walk every zone every day
    • Winter is the best season for artifact hunting — no crops to water, more time to dig
    • The Burglar Ring (kill 500 Dust Sprites) doubles monster loot, making artifact hunting more efficient
