    🏆 Stardew Valley — 100% Completion Checklist

    Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6.15 | Updated for 1.6 content including new crops, recipes, trinkets, and mastery items

    The Perfection Tracker (accessible from Qi's Walnut Room on Ginger Island) tracks your progress toward 100% completion. This checklist covers every category so you can check off items as you go.

    📊 What Is Perfection?

    Introduced in 1.5 and expanded in 1.6, Perfection measures your progress across 7 main categories:

    Category Total Required How to Check
    📦 Shipping Ship 1 of every item Shipping screen collection tab
    🍳 Cooking Cook every recipe Cooking tab in collections
    🔨 Crafting Craft every recipe Crafting tab in collections
    🐟 Fishing Catch every fish Fish tab in collections
    🏺 Museum Donate all artifacts + minerals Museum collection screen
    ⭐ Stardrops Find all 7 Energy bar shows count
    🌴 Golden Walnuts All 130 Qi's Walnut Room tracker

    Tip: The Perfection Tracker in Qi's Walnut Room gives you exact counts for each category. You can check your progress at any time after unlocking Ginger Island.

    Forage & Artisan

    Fishing, minerals, artifacts, cooked dishes, and monster drops all need to be shipped once. The collections tab shows exactly what's missing.

    🍳 Cooking All Recipes

    1.6 added more recipes. Total is now 80+ recipes.

    How to Get Recipes
    TV (Queen of Sauce) 60+ recipes air on random days
    Skill level-ups Cooking Lv 1, 3, 6
    Heart events Multiple NPCs give recipes at 3-heart events
    Ginger Island Qi's Walnut Room has exclusive recipes
    1.6 New New recipes from Cooking skill mastery

    Queen of Sauce Schedule (Remaining Episodes)

    The Queen of Sauce airs on Sunday and Wednesday. If you missed an episode, check the re-run on Wednesday of the same season in Year 2+.

    Tip: The Stardrop Saloon sells some recipes (Cookies, Rhubarb Pie, etc.). If you're missing one, check Gus.

    ⭐ Stardrops (All 7 Locations)

    Stardrops permanently increase your max energy. There are 7 total:

    # Stardrop How to Get Max Energy
    1 Mine Chest F100 Reach floor 100 in Mines +34
    2 Stardrop Saloon Buy for 20,000g from Gus +34
    3 Old Master Cannoli Secret Woods — give Stardrop to the statue +34
    4 Penny's 14-Heart Event Married to Penny / or complete her 14-heart** +34
    5 Krobus Buy for 20,000g from Krobus (Sewers) +34
    6 JojaMart For Sale (Joja route) Buy via Joja membership +34
    7 Museum Collection Reward Donate 95+ items to museum +34

    Total max energy with all Stardrops: 508 (base 270 + 34×7)

    Tip: The Old Master Cannoli stardrop in the Secret Woods requires you to put a Sweet Gem Berry into the statue. Save your first Sweet Gem Berry for this — don't sell it!

    🌴 Golden Walnuts Tracker

    You need 130 Golden Walnuts for full Ginger Island completion.

    Location Total Best Method
    Island West (Beach + Farm) 20 Digging, fishing, farming
    Island Jungle 20 Puzzles, bushes, fishing
    Island East (Volcano) 40 Mining, chests, monster drops
    Island North (Docks) 10 Fishing, farming
    Island Pirate Cove 10 Mermaid puzzle (rain only)
    Island Gem Birds 10 Solve the gem-bird puzzle
    Island Volcano Forge 10 Forge combos
    Various 10 Miscellaneous (parrot express, etc.)

    Quick Walnut Tips

    1. Parrot Express — Each upgrade costs walnuts but makes finding the rest easier
    2. Shake every bush — About 20 walnuts are hidden in bushes
    3. Fish everywhere — Walnuts are in fishing bubbles on island shores
    4. Volcano mining — Chests on every 5th floor guaranteed have walnuts
    5. Mermaid puzzle — Only doable during rain, play the flute block puzzle

    Tip: Don't spend walnuts on the Parrot Express until you've unlocked all island areas (at least 20 walnuts for the initial island upgrades). The express is a convenience, not a requirement.

    🔍 Perfection Tracker — How to Check Your Progress

    Once you've collected 100 Golden Walnuts, unlock Qi's Walnut Room on Ginger Island:

    1. Enter the door behind the parrot
    2. Walk to the Perfection Tracker on the left wall
    3. It shows exact progress in every category

    Perfection Reward

    When you reach 100% Perfection:

    Reward Effect
    Statue of True Perfection Produces 1 Prismatic Shard per day
    Golden Clock discount Not applicable (already free at perfection)
    Perfection score displayed Shows 100% on title screen
    Golden Chicken Buy from Marnie after perfection

    The Statue of True Perfection is incredible — a free Prismatic Shard every single day. That's 5,000g/day or infinite tool enchanting.

    Tip: Don't stress about Perfection. Stardew Valley is meant to be enjoyed. The Perfection tracker is a guide, not a homework assignment. Most players reach 100% in Year 4–6 naturally.
