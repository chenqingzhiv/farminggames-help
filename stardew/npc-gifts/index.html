    💝 NPC Gift Guide

    Stardew Valley 1.6 | Quick NPC Preference Reference

    🌟 Basic Rules

    • Birthday gift ×8 friendship points (always give a Loved gift!)
    • Gifts twice per week, birthday doesn't count toward limit
    • Quality matters: Iridium > Gold > Normal

    💡 Gift-Giving Tips

    1. Easiest gifts to obtain:
    2. Coffee (Harvey loves it, buy at Saloon, available year-round)
    3. Amethyst (Abigail loves it, mine it)

    4. Diamond (almost all NPCs at least like it)

    5. Weekly gift route:

    6. Monday: Go to the Saloon, buy coffee → give to Harvey/Shane/Pam
    7. Mid-week: Gift NPCs you run into

    8. Weekend: Focus on anyone you missed

    9. Bachelor/Bachelorette guide using bouquet:

    10. Give loved gifts until 10 hearts
    11. Buy bouquet from Pierre's → give it → become relationship
    12. Keep gifting until 14 hearts → buy Mermaid Pendant to propose
