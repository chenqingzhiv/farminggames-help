# 🗺️ Sun Haven — Zone & Map Guide

> Data version: Sun Haven 1.7 | Covers all 3 major zones, how to unlock them, key locations in each area, and the fast travel system

Sun Haven is a farming RPG with a sprawling world split across **three distinct zones**, each with its own theme, shops, farmable land, NPCs, dungeons, and story arcs. Understanding how to navigate these zones — and more importantly, how to unlock them efficiently — is critical to progressing through the game. This guide breaks down every zone, its key locations, and the travel systems that connect them.

---

## 🌍 Three Zones Overview

| Zone | Theme | How to Unlock | Key Features |
|:-----|:------|:--------------|:-------------|
| **☀️ Sun Haven** | Human/demon/monster fantasy town | Starting zone | Main farm, Sun Haven Mines, Beach, General Store |
| **🌿 Nel'Vari** | Elven forest sanctuary | Repair bridge (5,000 Tickets + materials) | Second farm, Nel'Vari mine, unique crops/animals |
| **💀 Withergate** | Undead industrial city | Repair boat (8,000 Tickets + advanced materials) | City rank system, Withergate mines, unique gear |

> **Tip:** Don't rush to unlock both zones immediately. Focus on Sun Haven first until you have Gold-tier tools and at least 10,000 Tickets in savings. Nel'Vari is accessible earlier (around Summer Year 1) but Withergate should wait until you have strong combat gear.

## ☀️ Sun Haven — The Main Area

Sun Haven is your home base. It's the largest zone by surface area and contains the most shops, NPCs, and daily activities.

### Key Locations

| Location | Description | Highlights |
|:---------|:------------|:-----------|
| **Your Farm** | Starting farm plot (30×40 tiles) | Expandable, upgradable farmhouse, barns/coops |
| **Town Square** | Center of Sun Haven | Bulletin board, festivals, marriage ceremonies |
| **General Store (Eli's)** | Main shop | Seeds, tools, furniture, basic goods (open 9 AM–6 PM) |
| **Carpenter Shop** | Building upgrades | House upgrades, farm buildings, sprinkler crafting |
| **Blacksmith (Liam's)** | Tool and weapon upgrades | Tool upgrades cost tickets + bars |
| **Clinic (Dr. Cheng's)** | Healing and buffs | HP/Mana restoration, antidotes for poison |
| **Tavern (Anne's)** | Social hub | Buy food, meet NPCs, accept side quests |
| **Sun Haven Mines** | 40-floor dungeon | 5 ore types, gems, monster drops, boss at F40 |
| **Beach (East)** | Fishing and foraging | Saltwater fish, coral, shells, beach forageables |
| **Museum** | Donation center | 200+ items to donate, Curator's Blessing reward |
| **Bus Stop** | Fast travel hub | Travel to other zones (unlocked later) |

### Sun Haven Mines

The mines are your primary source of **ores, gems, and combat experience**. The 40 floors are divided into tiers:

| Floors | Ores | Enemies | Key Drops |
|:------|:-----|:--------|:----------|
| 1–10 | Copper, Stone | Slimes, Bats | Copper Ore, Bat Wings |
| 11–20 | Iron, Gold | Skeletons, Cave Spiders | Iron Ore, Spider Silk |
| 21–30 | Gold, Mithril | Ghosts, Golems | Mithril Ore, Soul Shards |
| 31–40 | Mithril, Celestial | Dark Elves, Bosses | Celestial Ore, Dragon Scales |

**Elevator:** Unlocked at floor 10, 20, 30, and 40 — allows instant travel to any unlocked floor.

> **Tip:** Always bring a **Hearty Stew** (or 3) when diving below floor 20. The enemies hit harder and the healing from food is more efficient than potions for long runs.

## 🌿 Nel'Vari — The Elven Forest

Nel'Vari is a lush, magical forest zone populated by elves, fairies, and magical creatures. It has a completely different aesthetic and economic system from Sun Haven.

### How to Unlock Nel'Vari

1. Complete the **"Bridge to Nel'Vari"** quest (appears after reaching Farming Lv 5 or completing early story)
2. Gather the required materials:
   - **5,000 Tickets**
   - **200 Wood + 100 Stone + 50 Iron Bar**
3. Pay the Carpenter — the bridge east of Sun Haven is rebuilt in 3 days
4. Cross the bridge to enter Nel'Vari

### Key Locations in Nel'Vari

| Location | Description | Highlights |
|:---------|:------------|:-----------|
| **Nel'Vari Town Center** | Elven village hub | Unique shops, elven NPCs, daily quests |
| **Nel'Vari Farm** | Second farm plot (40×40) | Permanent Spring climate, unique crops |
| **Nel'Vari General Store** | Elven seed shop | Elven Wheat, Starfruit, Nectar Flower seeds |
| **Nel'Vari Crafting Shop** | Unique recipes | Loom upgrades, elven armor, magic-infused tools |
| **Nel'Vari Mine** | 25-floor dungeon | Magic ore, fairy drops, forest-themed enemies |
| **Fairy Pond** | Hidden area behind waterfall | Rare fish, Fairy Dust foraging |
| **Elven Library** | Knowledge center | Crafting recipe scrolls, lore books |

### Unique Nel'Vari Crops

| Crop | Seed Cost | Grow Time | Harvest | Profit/Day |
|:-----|:---------|:----------|:--------|:----------|
| Elven Wheat | 20g | 3 days | 2–3 | 15g/day |
| Starfruit | 60g | 6 days | 1 | 45g/day |
| Nel'Vari Nectar Flower | 45g | 5 days | 1 | 38g/day |
| Fairy Bloom | 35g | 4 days | 1 | 30g/day |

### Unique Nel'Vari Animals

- **Elven Chicken** (1,200g) — Lays golden eggs (sell for 40g each, higher than regular eggs)
- **Magical Cow** (2,500g) — Produces enchanted milk (80g base, used in elven cooking)
- **Fairy Moth** (3,000g) — Produces Fairy Dust every 3 days (key crafting ingredient)

> **Tip:** Nel'Vari's permanent Spring climate means you can plant Starfruit year-round and never worry about season changes. This makes it the ideal location for a dedicated Keg-wine operation.

## 💀 Withergate — The Undead City

Withergate is an industrial, gothic city inhabited by undead, demons, and monsters. It's the hardest zone to unlock but also offers the highest-end gear, recipes, and income opportunities.

### How to Unlock Withergate

1. Complete the **"Setting Sail"** quest (appears after reaching Combat Lv 10 or completing Sun Haven story)
2. Gather materials for the boat repair:
   - **8,000 Tickets**
   - **50 Mithril Bar + 20 Cloth + 10 Rope + 5 Magic Sprite Dust**
3. The boat at Sun Haven docks is repaired in **5 days**
4. Sail to Withergate from the Sun Haven dock

### Key Locations in Withergate

| Location | Description | Highlights |
|:---------|:------------|:-----------|
| **Withergate Docks** | Arrival point | Boat back to Sun Haven, dock forageables |
| **Withergate City Center** | Urban hub | Multiple shops, city hall, rank-up NPC |
| **Withergate Apartment** | Your Withergate home | Small farm plot on the roof |
| **Withergate General Store** | Zone shop | Shadow Berry seeds, Void Tomato seeds |
| **Withergate Blacksmith** | Void-tier gear | Best weapons and armor in the game |
| **Withergate Mines** | 30-floor dungeon | Void Ore, Shadow gems, skeleton enemies |
| **Rooftop Garden** | Exclusive area | Key from high city rank, rare plants |
| **Sewer Entrance** | Hidden dungeon | Post-game content, toughest enemies |

### Withergate City Rank System

Unlike Nel'Vari which opens fully at once, Withergate gates content behind a **city rank** progression:

| Rank | Requirement | Unlock |
|:-----|:------------|:-------|
| **Rookie** | Complete arrival quests | Basic shop access, apartment |
| **Citizen** | 10 quests + 2,000 Tickets | Second shop row, sewer entrance |
| **Respected** | 25 quests + 10,000 Tickets | Rooftop Garden, exclusive crafting |
| **Elite** | 50 quests + 25,000 Tickets | Best weapon recipes |
| **Champion** | All quests + 50,000 Tickets | Boss re-fight, rare drops, ultimate title |

### Unique Withergate Items

- **Shadow Berry** — Exclusive crop, used in Shadow Brew (best combat buff drink)
- **Void Tomato** — High-value crop for pickling
- **Void Ore** — Found in Withergate mines F15+, used for best gear
- **Shadow Silk** — Monster drop, used for Shadow Armor
- **Withergate Fishing Rod** — Required to fish in Withergate's toxic waters

> **Tip:** City rank quests are the fastest way to advance — prioritize the bulletin board in the City Center. Each rank-up quest chain unlocks new shops with items you can't get anywhere else.

## 🚀 Fast Travel System

Sun Haven has a robust fast travel system that makes navigating the three zones much more manageable.

### Warp Points

Warp points are glowing crystals found in key locations. Once activated, you can teleport between them instantly.

| Warp Point | Zone | How to Unlock |
|:-----------|:-----|:--------------|
| **Farm Portal** | Sun Haven | Active from day 1 |
| **Town Square** | Sun Haven | Walk to town square |
| **Beach** | Sun Haven | Walk to beach area |
| **Mine Entrance** | Sun Haven | Walk to mines entrance |
| **Nel'Vari Entrance** | Nel'Vari | Cross bridge after repair |
| **Nel'Vari Town** | Nel'Vari | Walk to town center |
| **Nel'Vari Mine** | Nel'Vari | Walk to mine entrance |
| **Withergate Docks** | Withergate | Arrive by boat |
| **Withergate Center** | Withergate | Walk to city center |
| **Withergate Mine** | Withergate | Walk to mine entrance |

### Bus System

The **Bus Stop** near the Town Square allows paid travel between zones:

| Route | Cost | Travel Time |
|:------|:----|:------------|
| Sun Haven → Nel'Vari | 50 Tickets | Instant (loading screen) |
| Nel'Vari → Sun Haven | 50 Tickets | Instant |
| Sun Haven → Withergate | 100 Tickets | Instant (requires boat repair) |
| Withergate → Sun Haven | 100 Tickets | Instant |

### Returning to Sun Haven

You can return to Sun Haven from any zone at any time via:
- **Warp point** from the zone's entrance portal
- **Bus stop** in Nel'Vari
- **Boat** from Withergate docks
- **Recall spell** (learned late-game from Museum reward)

> **Tip:** Unlock all warp points as soon as you enter a new zone. The farm portal alone won't cover you — having the town center warp active saves 5 minutes of walking every single day.

## 🎯 Exploration Tips

### Hidden Areas & Secrets

| Secret | Zone | How to Discover |
|:-------|:-----|:----------------|
| **Sun Haven Hidden Pond** | Sun Haven (NE woods) | Break rocks blocking path (Gold Pickaxe required) |
| **Fairy Grove** | Nel'Vari | Go behind the waterfall in the NE corner |
| **Withergate Sewers** | Withergate | Manhole in alley near city center — requires Citizen rank |
| **Ancient Library** | Nel'Vari | Cracked wall behind the main building (Mining Lv 5+) |
| **Sun Dragon Ribcage** | Sun Haven Mines F25 | Side path on floor 25, contains rare artifact |
| **Rooftop Garden** | Withergate 2F | Locked door, requires Respected rank key |
| **Secret Beach Cave** | Sun Haven Beach | Hidden behind large rock at low tide (morning only) |

### General Exploration Tips

1. **Always enter every building.** Some shops and NPC houses aren't marked on the map at first — entering them adds them to your discovery log.
2. **Break every cracked wall.** In all three zone's mines, cracked walls conceal treasure rooms with chests containing rare recipes, gems, and artifacts.
3. **Fish in every body of water.** Different fish spawn in different zones, and some fish are needed for Museum completion. Don't assume the Sun Haven river is the same as the Nel'Vari river.
4. **Forage widely.** Each zone has unique forageables. Withergate's docks have exclusive items not found anywhere else.
5. **Talk to NPCs.** Many secret locations are revealed through NPC dialogue. Befriending certain characters (especially the Mayor, the Librarian, and the Innkeeper) reveals map markers for hidden areas.
6. **Map completion percentage** is tracked in the pause menu. If you're below 100% for any zone, check for undiscovered warp points and unvisited buildings.
7. **Use the Quick Travel** feature from the menu (Y on keyboard, or the map icon). This lets you instantly travel to any unlocked warp point without walking to the physical crystal.
