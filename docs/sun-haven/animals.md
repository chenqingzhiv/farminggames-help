## ❤️ Animal Care & Friendship

**Care actions:** Feed daily (maintains base production), Pet (+1 heart, max 5/day), Brush (+2 hearts, unlocks ★ products), Let outdoors (+0.5 heart/day).

| Hearts | Title | Effect |
|:-------|:------|:-------|
| 0–2 | 😐 Neutral | Base quality only |
| 3–4 | 🙂 Friendly | Chance of ★ (+25% price) |
| 5–6 | 😊 Happy | Guaranteed ★, chance of ★★ |
| 7–8 | ❤️ Best Friends | Guaranteed ★★ (+50%) |
| 9–10 | 💕 Soul Mates | ★★★ (+75%), extra produce |

**Quality tools:** Basic Brush (100 tickets, +2 hearts), Gold Brush (1,000, +4 hearts +★ upgrade), Deluxe Brush (3,000, +6 hearts +★★ upgrade).

> **Tip:** ★★★★★ products sell for 2× base price. A Gold Brush pays for itself within a season through quality bonuses.

## ❄️ Winter & 📊 Profit Comparison

**Winter effects:** No outdoor access, −1 heart/day, −10% production. **Mitigation:** Upgrade barn to Lv 2 before Winter 1, use Gold Brush, Deluxe Barn eliminates winter penalty entirely.

### Daily Revenue: Animals vs Crops vs Fishing

| Activity | Investment | Daily Revenue | Type |
|:---------|:-----------|:--------------|:-----|
| Basic Crops (50 tiles) | 300 Tix | 100–200g | Manual |
| Fishing (max skill) | 500 Tix | 300–500g | Active |
| **4 Chickens + Mayo** | 1,200 Tix | **180g/day** | **Passive** |
| **4 Cows + Cheese** | 2,480 Tix | **440g/day** | **Passive** |
| **4 Goats + Cheese** | 3,040 Tix | **560g/day** | **Passive** |
| **4 Unicorns + Cheese** | 8,000 Tix | **2,080g/day** | **Passive** |

**Mid-game setup:** 4 Goats + Cheese Press (560g/day) + 4 Chickens + Mayo Machine (180g/day) = **740g/day** with 2 minutes of daily work.

> **Tip:** Animals beat crops for passive income. Crops need replanting, watering, and harvesting — animals need only 2 minutes of petting plus occasional feed refills once auto-feed is active.
