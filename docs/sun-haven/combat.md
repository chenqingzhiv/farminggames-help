# ⚔️ Sun Haven — Combat & Magic Guide

Sun Haven stands apart from most farming RPGs with its **real-time combat system**, complete with weapon types, magic spells, skill trees, and challenging dungeon bosses. This guide covers everything you need to dominate all three biomes.

---

## 🗡️ Weapon System Overview

Each weapon type has distinct range, speed, and DPS characteristics:

| Weapon | Speed | Range | Base DPS | Best For |
|:-------|:-----:|:-----:|:--------:|:---------|
| Sword | Fast | Short | 18 | All-around, 3-hit combo finisher |
| Crossbow | Slow | Long | 22 | Kiting, pierces 2 targets |
| Staff | Medium | Medium | 20 | +15% spell damage |
| Spear | Medium | Medium | 24 | Highest raw DPS, charge lunge |
| Dagger | Very Fast | Very Short | 16 | Status stacking, 2 debuffs/sec |

> **Tip:** Keep a **Crossbow** as your secondary for every build. Switching to it during boss AoE phases lets you maintain DPS from safety. Spear has the highest raw DPS but requires precise positioning — missed lunges lock you in recovery frames.

## 🔥 Magic Spells — Element System

| Element | Role | Key Spells | Best Synergy |
|:--------|:-----|:-----------|:-------------|
| 🔥 Fire | Burst damage | Fireball (lvl 1), Meteor (lvl 10), Inferno (lvl 20) | Crossbow |
| 💧 Water | Healing + Farming | Heal (lvl 2), Rain Dance (lvl 8), Tidal Wave (lvl 18) | Spear tanking |
| 🌍 Earth | Defense + AoE | Stone Shield (lvl 3), Earthquake (lvl 12), Petrify (lvl 22) | Sword close range |
| 🌪️ Air | Mobility + Control | Gust (lvl 1), Dash (lvl 6), Tornado (lvl 16) | Dagger debuff stacking |

**Combat mana rotation:** Open with Earth Shield, spam Fireball, Dash out of danger, Heal when below 50% HP. Never let mana drop below 20. Use Mana Potions (50 mana, 5s CD) mid-game and Greater Mana Potions (150 mana, 3s CD) late-game. Food buffs like Mana Pie (+3/sec for 60s) are essential for boss fights.

## 🌲 Skill Tree — Three Builds

| Build | Core Talents | Level Req | Effect |
|:------|:-------------|:---------:|:-------|
| ⚔️ Melee | Iron Skin, Shield Bash, Berserker | 6/10/20 | -15% dmg taken, stun on block, +100% dmg under 30% HP |
| 🏹 Ranged | Steady Aim, Evasive, Sniper | 3/6/20 | +25% dmg, +15% dodge, crits deal 300% dmg |
| 🧙 Mage | Mana Font, Elementalist, Archmage | 3/10/20 | +50 mana, +15% dmg per element, spells cost 50% less mana |

**Recommendation:** Melee for Withergate undead crowds, Ranged for Nel'Vari demons with AoE attacks, Mage for sustained boss DPS. Hybrid characters should invest in Mage + Ranged for maximum flexibility.

## 👾 Enemy Types by Biome

| Biome | Common Enemies | HP Range | Weakness | Key Drop |
|:------|:---------------|:--------:|:---------|:---------|
| ☀️ Sun Haven | Slimes, Bats, Cave Spiders | 25-50 | Fire | Slime Goo, Bat Wing |
| 🔥 Nel'Vari | Imps, Hell Hounds, Shadow Knights | 80-200 | Air / Water | Demon Dust, Dark Shard |
| ❄️ Withergate | Skeletons, Ghosts, Zombies, Liches | 70-300 | Spear / Magic | Bone Fragment, Soul Gem |

**Withergate Lich (300 HP, 35 dmg)** is the hardest standard enemy. It has a shield phase requiring Fire + Earth combo to break. Bring both element types or you'll do 90% reduced damage during shield.

## 🏰 Dungeon Boss Mechanics

| Boss | Location | HP | Key Mechanic | Strategy |
|:-----|:---------|:--:|:-------------|:---------|
| Golem King | Sun Haven F25 | 2500 | Ground pound AoE shockwave | Dodge the ring, attack from behind |
| Shadow Warden | Nel'Vari F30 | 3500 | Teleport + clone | Kill clones instantly, Earth Shield during beam |
| Lich Overlord | Withergate F35 | 5000 | Summon skeletons + life drain | AoE clear adds, save burst for shield phase |
| Dragon Matriarch | Endgame F40 | 8000 | Elemental phase cycling Fire→Water→Earth→Air | Match weapon to phase, mana pots on cooldown |

> **Tip:** Against the Dragon Matriarch, bring **two full stacks** of Greater Mana Potions. Switch weapon per phase: Crossbow (Fire), Sword (Water), Staff (Earth), Spear (Air).
