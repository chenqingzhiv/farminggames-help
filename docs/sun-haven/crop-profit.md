## General Profit Tips

1. **Regrowth crops** (Tomato, Strawberry, Blueberry, Corn, Pineapple, Hops, Grape, Cranberry, Frost Berry) save replanting labor and provide consistent income throughout the season.
2. **High-value single-harvest crops** (Watermelon, Pineapple, Cauliflower, Frost Melon) are best when you have sufficient energy and time to replant repeatedly.
3. **Early season strategy:** Plant fast-growing crops like Carrots or Lettuce in Spring, Radish in Summer, or Barley in Fall to build capital quickly.
4. **Fruit trees** are the ultimate investment — plant them as soon as possible and they will generate passive income across seasons.
5. **Processing crops** (making wine, jam, or pickled goods) can multiply profits by 2–3x but requires crafting stations and additional time.
