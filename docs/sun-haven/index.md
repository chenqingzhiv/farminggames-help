# ☀️ Sun Haven — Game Overview

## About the Game

**Sun Haven** is a fantasy farming RPG developed by **Pixel Sprout Studios**, released in **2023**. Set in a vibrant world of magic and monsters, it combines traditional farming simulation with deep RPG mechanics, including multiple skill trees, spellcasting, and a full-scale main story. The game supports up to **8-player multiplayer**, allowing friends to farm, explore, and fight together.

## 🌍 Three Biomes

Sun Haven features **three distinct biomes** — each with unique crops, enemies, and progression:

### Biome Overview

| Biome | Theme | Crops | Main Skill |
|:------|:------|:------|:-----------|
| 🌿 Sun Haven | Farmland, Elves | Standard crops | Farming |
| 🔥 Nel'Vari | Dark forest, Demons | Magical plants | Combat |
| ❄️ Withergate | Undead city | Alchemical ingredients | Crafting |

## ⚔️ Combat & Skill System

Unlike most farming sims, Sun Haven has an **active combat system** with weapons, spells, and enemy dungeons. You gain experience in **13 different skills** including:

- 🧑‍🌾 Farming — Plant and harvest, unlock higher-tier seeds
- 🎣 Fishing — Catch fish and treasures from lakes and rivers
- ⚔️ Combat — Swords, crossbows, and magic against monsters
- 🧙 Exploration — Movement speed, dungeon traversal, and treasure finding
- 🍳 Cooking — Turn raw ingredients into powerful food buffs

Each skill has its own talent tree with passive bonuses and active abilities. You can specialize or build a balanced character.

> **Tip:** In multiplayer, assign roles — one player focuses on farming and cooking while another handles combat and mining. This division of labor lets your group progress much faster than each player trying to grind every skill individually.
