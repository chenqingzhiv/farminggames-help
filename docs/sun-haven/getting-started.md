# ☀️ Sun Haven — Complete Beginner's Guide

## Welcome to Sun Haven

Sun Haven is not your typical farming sim. Yes, you plant crops and raise animals — but you also fight monsters, explore dungeons, learn magic spells, and manage a town across **three distinct realms**. This guide covers your first day, first week, first season, and the fundamentals of building a thriving farm-and-fantasy operation.

---

## 📅 Day 1-2: Getting Established

### Essential Starting Checklist

| ✅ | Task | Why It Matters |
|:--|:-----|:---------------|
| ✅ | Complete tutorial quest line | Unlocks basic tools, seeds, and crafting bench |
| ✅ | Plant free starter seeds (wheat + sunflowers) | Immediate income generator |
| ✅ | Buy a fishing rod (general store, 200g) | Fishing is your best early money-maker |
| ✅ | Explore farm perimeter for forageables | Free early gold and energy food |
| ✅ | Build the basic crafting bench | Unlocks workbench recipes |
| ✅ | Talk to 5+ townsfolk | Early relationships unlock quests and discounts |

### Day 1 Priority Order

1. **Morning:** Follow tutorial → clear basic debris → accept starter quests
2. **Mid-day:** Visit general store → buy fishing rod + extra wheat seeds
3. **Afternoon:** Plant seeds, water, set up crafting bench
4. **Evening:** Fish for 2-3 in-game hours at the town pond
5. **Night:** Sell fish + forageables → buy more wheat seeds

### Stamina Management

Day 1 is tight on energy. Prioritize:
- **Water your crops first** — dead crops = wasted gold
- **Fish over mining** on day 1 — fishing is lower stamina, higher return
- **Eat forageables** (berries, mushrooms) — each restores 10-15 energy

> **Tip:** Save your first harvest of wheat. Do not sell raw wheat — it is used in early cooking recipes and animal feed. Sell the sunflowers instead; they have better gold-per-stamina ratio.

## ⚔️ Combat & Dungeon Guide

Combat unlocks around **day 3** after you talk to the town guard captain. The **Sun Haven Mines** serve as your first dungeon.

### Dungeon Floors 1-10: Beginner Zone

| Floor | Enemies | Resources | Goal |
|:------|:--------|:----------|:-----|
| 1-3 | Slimes, Bats | Copper ore, Stone | Practice combat, collect basic ore |
| 4-6 | Slimes, Cave Spiders | Copper, Iron | Gather iron for tools |
| 7-10 | Slimes, Bats, Skeleton Guards | Iron, Gems | Unlock elevator at floor 10 |

### Combat Tips for Beginners

1. **Kite enemies** — hit, retreat, hit again. Never stand still trading blows
2. **Eat food between fights** — cooked meals restore much more HP than raw ingredients
3. **Use your starting weapon** until floor 5 — then craft a copper sword
4. **Save monster drops** — slime goo, bat wings, and bones are crafting materials
5. **Bring pickaxe** — every floor has mineable walls with ores

### Skill Points — Where to Invest Early

| Skill | Category | Level Priority | Why |
|:------|:---------|:---------------|:----|
| Farming | 🌱 | **1st to 5** | Unlocks Sprinkler blueprint at level 5 — game-changer |
| Fishing | 🎣 | 2nd to 3 | Better rod + catch higher-value fish |
| Combat | ⚔️ | 3rd to 5 | More HP and better weapon unlocks |
| Mining | ⛏️ | Anytime | More ore per swing |

> **Tip:** Rush **Farming level 5**. The Sprinkler blueprint automates watering, saving you 3+ in-game hours daily. That freed time goes into fishing or mining, which snowballs your income. You can reach level 5 by day 7 if you plant 15+ tiles and harvest twice.

### Common Beginner Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Fix |
|:--------|:-------------|:----|
| Selling raw wheat | Much more valuable as animal feed or flour | Store 50+ wheat before selling any |
| Ignoring fishing | Misses easiest early-game income | Fish 30 mins in-game each evening |
| Spreading skill points thin | Delays critical unlocks (Sprinkler at Farming 5) | Focus one skill at a time |
| Not hoarding ores | Tool upgrades blocked by copper/iron shortage | Save every ore — only sell gems |
| Forgetting dungeon elevator | Re-clearing floors 1-5 every time wastes hours | Unlock elevator at floor 10 ASAP |

> **Final tip:** Sun Haven rewards **focus**. Pick one income stream (crops or fishing) for week 1, double down until it's automated, then expand into combat and exploration. The Sprinkler unlock at Farming 5 is the single biggest quality-of-life upgrade in the early game. Prioritize it above everything else — once you have sprinklers, the game opens up dramatically.
