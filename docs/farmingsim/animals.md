## 🌾 Feed Production Chains

| Feed | Composition | Production Method | Used By |
|:-----|:------------|:------------------|:--------|
| **Grass** | Fresh cut | Mow meadow | Sheep, Horses, Beef Cows |
| **Hay** | Dried grass | Tedder → Windrow → Bale | Cows (winter), Sheep |
| **Silage** | Fermented grass/corn | Forage harvester → Silo | Cows (TMR ingredient) |
| **TMR** | Silage 50% + Hay 30% + Straw 20% | Mixer Wagon ($18K) | Dairy Cows (max yield) |
| **Grain** | Wheat/Barley/Oats | Harvest → Silo | Chickens, Pigs, Horses |
| **Root Crops** | Potatoes, Beets | Harvest → Root Silo | Pigs only |

**TMR (Total Mixed Ration)** boosts dairy cow milk yield by **+40%** over hay. Prioritize a Mixer Wagon once you have multiple cows.

## 🌿 Pasture Rotation & Winter Prep

**Grass regrowth stages:** Short (1d, 1,000L) → Medium (3d, 2,500L) → Long (5d, 4,000L) → Flowering (7d, **5,500L max**).

**4-paddock rotation:** Graze 1 paddock for 3 days, rotate. By day 12, paddock 1 regrows to Stage 3+. Mow excess in summer for winter hay/silage.

**Winter:** Grass stops Nov–Feb. A 50-cow herd needs **720,000 L of stored feed** for 4 winter months. Stockpile hay bales in summer.

### Quick Tips

1. **Chickens first** — fastest ROI, learn feed mechanics cheaply
2. **TMR for dairy** — 40% yield boost over hay alone
3. **Pigs are fertilizer makers** — their value is in the manure cycle, not direct sales
4. **Auto-feeder before milker** — feeding is the bigger time sink
5. **Clean barns = healthy animals** — dirty barns lose 25% production immediately
6. **Sell wool at seasonal peaks** — prices fluctuate like grain
