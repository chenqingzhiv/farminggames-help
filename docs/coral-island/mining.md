# ⛏️ Coral Island — Complete Mining Guide

Coral Island features three massive mines (Earth, Water, and Wind), each with unique enemies, ores, and rewards. This guide covers floor-by-floor strategy, tool progression, combat, stamina management, and profit optimization.

---

## 🔥 Earth Mine (Fire/Lava Themed)

The starting mine, unlocked on Day 5 of Spring. 40 floors total, progressively harder.

### Floor-by-Floor Guide

| Floors | Theme | Key Ores | Gems | Enemies | Boss |
|:-------|:------|:---------|:-----|:--------|:-----|
| 1–10 | Rocky cavern | Copper, Coal | Amethyst, Topaz | Green Slime, Bat | — |
| 11–20 | Lava rock | Copper, Silver | Ruby, Garnet | Fire Slime, Lava Bat | — |
| 21–30 | Magma chamber | Silver, Gold | Emerald, Sapphire | Magma Golem, Fire Spirit | — |
| 31–39 | Deep magma | Gold, Osmium | Diamond, Ruby (t2) | Lava Titan, Magma Wraith | — |
| **40** | Boss room | — | — | — | **Earth Elemental** |

### Ore Distribution & Mining Strategy

| Floor Range | Best For | Recommended Approach |
|:-----------|:--------|:-------------------|
| 1–5 | Copper farming, early artifacts | Use elevator between 1 and 5; break all rocks |
| 10–15 | Silver farming | Ignore copper nodes; target dark rocks |
| 20–25 | Gold (rare), mid-game artifacts | Bring at least Steel Pickaxe |
| 30–35 | Gold farming, osmium nodes | Bring Gold+ pickaxe and healing items |
| 36–39 | Osmium, rare artifacts | Save stamina for boss prep |
| 40 | **Earth Elemental fight** | Bring 10+ healing items, best weapon |

> **Tip:** The elevator unlocks every 5 floors. Always rush to the next elevator floor — don't mine side tunnels until you've unlocked all floors to 40.

## 🌊 Water Mine (Deep Sea Cavern)

Unlocked after Earth Mine completion. 40 floors, water-themed with swimming sections.

### Floor-by-Floor Guide

| Floors | Theme | Key Ores | Gems | Enemies | Hazards |
|:-------|:------|:---------|:-----|:--------|:--------|
| 1–10 | Coastal cave | Copper, Silver | Aquamarine, Pearl | Blue Slime, Pufferfish | Shallow water |
| 11–20 | Underwater tunnel | Silver, Gold | Sapphire, Coral | Electric Eel, Crab | Deep water (need breathing) |
| 21–30 | Abyssal trench | Gold, Osmium | Diamond, Moonstone | Jellyfish Swarm, Shark | Dark water, currents |
| 31–39 | Deep ocean floor | Osmium, Unknown Metal | Black Pearl, Star Gem | Kraken Tentacle, Abyss Watcher | Low visibility |
| **40** | Boss arena | — | — | — | **Water Elemental** |

### Water Mine Specific Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description | How to Handle |
|:---------|:-----------|:-------------|
| 🫧 **Breathing** | Underwater sections have oxygen meters | Craft Kelp Potion or breathing helmet |
| 🌊 **Currents** | Push you in one direction | Walk against current or ride it intentionally |
| 🔦 **Visibility** | Dark sections limit your view range | Craft Glow Ring or bring torches |
| 🐚 **Shell deposits** | Unique to water mine, drop pearls | Break every one you see — pearls are high value |

**Unique Loot:** Old diving gear, ancient anchors, pearl jewelry, coral fragments.

> 💡 **Tip:** Craft at least 5 Kelp Potions before attempting Water Mine floors 20+. Running out of air while surrounded by enemies is a death sentence.

## 💨 Wind Mine (Sky/Air Themed)

Unlocked after Water Mine completion. 40 floors, floating platforms and wind puzzles.

### Floor-by-Floor Guide

| Floors | Theme | Key Ores | Gems | Enemies | Mechanics |
|:-------|:------|:---------|:-----|:--------|:---------|
| 1–10 | Floating gardens | Copper, Silver | Citrine, Peridot | Sky Slime, Wind Sprite | Light gravity |
| 11–20 | Skybridge ruins | Silver, Gold | Topaz, Opal | Harpy, Gust Elemental | Wind gusts, gaps |
| 21–30 | Cloud fortress | Gold, Osmium | Diamond, Sunstone | Storm Golem, Thunder Bird | Lightning strikes |
| 31–39 | Peak summit | Osmium, Stardust | Star Gem, Aurora Gem | Wind Titan, Sky Leviathan | Narrow platforms |
| **40** | Storm eye | — | — | — | **Wind Elemental** |

### Wind Mine Specific Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description | How to Handle |
|:---------|:-----------|:-------------|
| 🌀 **Wind gusts** | Push you upward or sideways | Time your movement between gusts |
| ⚡ **Lightning strikes** | Periodic damage in exposed areas | Use lightning rod trinket or time cover |
| ☁️ **Cloud platforms** | Temporary platforms that disappear | Move quickly — plan route before stepping |
| 🪶 **Feather fall** | Reduced fall damage in lower gravity | Jump further, but control descent |

**Unique Loot:** Feather artifacts, ancient sky maps, storm crystals, air element essence.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Wind Mine has the most complex platforming. If you fall, you lose 5–15 floors of progress. Equip Feather Fall Boots before attempting.

## ⛏️ Tool Upgrade Progression for Mining

| Pickaxe Tier | Upgrade Cost | Required For | Efficiency Gain | Stamina Reduction |
|:-----------|:-----------|:------------|:--------------|:----------------|
| 🟢 **Bronze** | 2,000g + 5 Copper | Earth Mine 1–15 | Base | — |
| 🔵 **Silver** | 5,000g + 10 Silver | Earth Mine 15–30, Water Mine 1–15 | +30% speed | -1 stamina/swing |
| 🟡 **Gold** | 10,000g + 8 Gold | Earth Mine 30+, Water Mine 15–30 | +50% speed | -2 stamina/swing |
| 🔴 **Osmium** | 25,000g + 5 Osmium | All mines full depth | +75% speed | -3 stamina/swing |
| 👑 **Titanium** | 50,000g + 10 Titanium | Post-game content | +100% speed | -4 stamina/swing |

### Priority Upgrade Path

1. **Bronze → Silver** (ASAP) — Unlocks Earth Mine mid-floors and makes early mining bearable
2. **Silver → Gold** (before Water Mine) — You'll need Gold to efficiently mine Earth Mine 30+
3. **Gold → Osmium** (before Wind Mine) — Osmium is required for top-tier gear
4. **Osmium → Titanium** (post-game) — Only for completionists and max efficiency

## ⚔️ Combat in Mines

### Enemy Reference Table

| Mine | Enemy | HP | Damage | Attack Pattern | Dodge Strategy |
|:----|:------|:--|:------|:--------------|:--------------|
| 🔥 Earth | Green Slime | 30 | 5 | Hop toward you, short range | Side step, hit after hop |
| 🔥 Earth | Magma Golem | 120 | 25 | Slow punch + AOE lava burst | Back away, attack after punch |
| 🔥 Earth | Lava Titan | 250 | 40 | Charge + ground slam | Run sideways, not backward |
| 🌊 Water | Electric Eel | 60 | 18 | Dashes in straight line | Sidestep 2 tiles before impact |
| 🌊 Water | Kraken Tentacle | 200 | 35 | Slam + sweep pattern | Jump over slam, duck sweep |
| 🌊 Water | Shark | 150 | 30 | Rush + bite combo | Kite in circles, attack tail |
| 💨 Wind | Harpy | 45 | 15 | Swoop attack from above | Wait for swoop, dodge forward |
| 💨 Wind | Storm Golem | 180 | 30 | Lightning bolt + wind push | Stay behind golem at all times |
| 💨 Wind | Sky Leviathan | 300 | 50 | Charge beam + tornado | Run perpendicular to beam |

### Combat Tips

1. **Learn attack patterns** — Every enemy telegraphs. Watch for the 0.5s wind-up animation.
2. **Use knockback** — Weapons with knockback stats keep enemies away from you.
3. **Food is better than potions** — Stack HP recovery meals that heal over time, not instantly.
4. **Bring a ranged weapon** — Even a basic slingshot helps against flying enemies (Wind Mine).

## 🗺️ Mine Shortcuts & Secrets

### Earth Mine
- **Secret floor 15b** — Hidden behind a cracked wall in the elevator room. Contains a gold vein cluster.
- **Floor 25 shortcut** — Jump over the lava stream to reach a hidden chest (always contains a geode).
- **Floor 38 ladder** — Hidden behind a dark rock that doesn't sparkle. Break every dark wall on 38.

### Water Mine
- **Floor 10 current** — Ride the downward current (don't fight it) to reach a pearl deposit.
- **Floor 22 air pocket** — Hidden in the upper-right corner. Restore oxygen here before descending.
- **Floor 35 shortcut** — Destroy the cracked coral ring to unlock a direct drop to floor 38.

### Wind Mine
- **Floor 8 cloud bridge** — Step on the third cloud from the left → wait 2 seconds → run. Leads to chest.
- **Floor 18 wind tunnel** — Face INTO the wind gust and let it push you UP to a hidden platform.
- **Floor 33 lightning rod** — Stand under the broken lightning rod during a strike → it powers a teleport pad to floor 36.

> 💡 **Pro Secret:** After beating all 3 mine bosses, return to each mine's floor 40. The boss arenas contain hidden chests that respawn every 7 days with rare loot.
