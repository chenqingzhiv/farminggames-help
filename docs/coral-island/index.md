# 🏝️ Coral Island — Game Overview

## About the Game

**Coral Island** is a vibrant tropical farming simulation game developed by **Stairway Games**, released in **October 2023** (version 1.0). Set on a gorgeous Indonesian-inspired archipelago, you inherit your grandmother's old farm and work alongside diverse villagers to restore the island's ecosystem and community.

### Key Features

| Feature | Details |
|:--------|:--------|
| 🎮 Genre | Farming Sim / Life Sim |
| 👥 Mode | Single-player |
| 🏭 Developer | Stairway Games |
| 📅 Release | October 2023 (1.0) |
| 🖥️ Platform | PC, PS5, Xbox Series X/S |

## 🌴 Farming & Ranching

Coral Island's farming system is built around **seasonal crops** suited to a tropical climate. The soil starts depleted and must be cleared of trash and debris — reflecting the game's core theme of environmental restoration. You can raise animals including cows, chickens, goats, and sheep, each requiring outdoor pasture and daily care.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Diving** — Explore the ocean floor to clean trash, discover artifacts, and gather resources
- **Pollination** — Bees and insect farms boost crop yields organically
- **Automation** — Sprinklers and auto-feeders unlock as you progress through the town rank system

## 🤿 Diving & Ocean Restoration

A standout feature of Coral Island is its **underwater exploration**. Using diving gear, you descend into the coral reefs surrounding the island to collect trash, salvage artifacts for the museum, and find rare gems. Cleaning the ocean raises your "Ocean Rank," which unlocks better diving equipment and reveals new areas.

### Ocean Rank Tiers

| Rank | Requirement | Reward |
|:-----|:------------|:-------|
| 🥉 Bronze C | Clean 20 trash | Basic fins upgrade |
| 🥈 Silver B | Clean 100 trash | Oxygen tank upgrade |
| 🥇 Gold A | Donate 10 artifacts | Underwater fast-travel |

> **Tip:** Prioritize upgrading your diving gear before your fishing rod. Diving provides significantly more early-game income and materials than fishing in Coral Island, and cleaning the ocean unlocks new areas packed with valuable forage.
