# 🎪 Coral Island Festivals & Events Guide — Complete Calendar

Coral Island has a lively festival calendar with 12 major events spread across spring, summer, fall, and winter. Each festival offers unique rewards, exclusive items you can't get anywhere else, and a chance to compete against other villagers for prizes. This guide breaks down every event with practical tips to help you win.

> **Tip:** Mark your calendar at the start of each season. Missing a festival means waiting a full year for another shot at those exclusive rewards.

---

## 📅 Festival System Overview

Festivals work on a simple schedule — each season has three events, and nearly all of them start at a specific time on a fixed day. You'll receive a letter in your mailbox the day before reminding you. Most competitions are based on your skill level, item quality, or a simple mini-game.

| Mechanic | Description |
|:---------|:------------|
| ⏰ Start Time | Most events run 10:00 AM — 10:00 PM |
| 📬 Invitation | Mail arrives the day before |
| 🏆 Competitions | Based on skill ranks, item quality, or mini-games |
| 🎁 Participation | Even losing gives small rewards |
| 🪙 Tokens | Earned at events, traded for exclusive items |

## 🌸 Spring Festivals

Spring kicks off with three events that ease you into the festival system. The Spring Festival is the big one — it's your first chance to earn major prizes.

| Festival | Day | Time | Type | Key Reward |
|:---------|:---:|:----:|:-----|:-----------|
| 🌼 Spring Festival | Spring 10 | 10:00 AM | Crop Contest | Spring Star Trophy (decor) |
| 🥚 Egg Hunt | Spring 20 | 9:00 AM | Mini-Game | Golden Egg (sell: 500g) |
| 🐝 Bee-keeping Fair | Spring 28 | 10:00 AM | Item Showcase | Bee House Blueprint |

### 🌼 Spring Festival (Spring 10)

The biggest spring event. Bring your highest-quality crop to the judging stand. The contest ranks entries by quality tier (Normal → Silver → Gold → Osmium), and the winner gets the Spring Star Trophy — a unique decor item worth about 2,000g in the museum display.

**Winning Tip:** Plant Cauliflower or Broccoli early in spring (Day 1). These multi-harvest crops have the highest chance to reach Gold or Osmium quality by Spring 10 if you water them daily and use fertilizer.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Spring Star Trophy + 1,000g
- 🥈 2nd: 500g + Quality Fertilizer ×5
- 🥉 3rd: 300g + Basic Fertilizer ×3

### 🥚 Egg Hunt (Spring 20)

A timed mini-game where you run around the town square collecting hidden eggs. The map is small — about the size of the central plaza — and eggs spawn in predictable spots: behind benches, inside bushes, near the fountain.

**Winning Tip:** Head straight for the **hard-to-reach eggs** behind buildings first. Every player starts from the same spot, so memorizing spawn locations gives you a huge edge. 18 eggs total; you need 12+ to win.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Golden Egg (500g) + Rabbit's Foot
- 🥈 2nd: 3× Quality Fertilizer
- 🥉 3rd: Spring Seeds ×5

### 🐝 Bee-keeping Fair (Spring 28)

Showcase your best honey or bee-adjacent product. This one is more chill — less competition, more of a community fair vibe. Even just showing up with basic honey gets you some rewards.

**Winning Tip:** Processed honey (aged in a cask) scores significantly higher than raw honey. Start aging honey right after the Spring Festival.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Bee House Blueprint + 800g
- 🥈 2nd: Quality Honey ×5
- 🥉 3rd: Wild Seeds ×10

## ☀️ Summer Festivals

Summer brings the big-money events. The Summer Beach Bash and Fireworks Festival are the highlights.

| Festival | Day | Time | Type | Key Reward |
|:---------|:---:|:----:|:-----|:-----------|
| 🏖️ Summer Beach Bash | Summer 10 | 12:00 PM | Mini-Games | Pearl Necklace (accessory) |
| 🎆 Fireworks Festival | Summer 20 | 7:00 PM | Spectacle | Firework Launcher Blueprint |
| 🧀 Summer Feast | Summer 28 | 6:00 PM | Cooking Contest | Gold Frying Pan (tool upgrade) |

### 🏖️ Summer Beach Bash (Summer 10)

A multi-game event on the beach with three mini-games: shell diving, volleyball, and a sandcastle building contest. You earn Beach Tokens from each game and spend them at the prize booth.

**Winning Tip:** Focus on **shell diving** — it uses your diving skill directly. If you've been diving regularly, you'll crush this event. 30+ Beach Tokens in one round is possible with high diving skill.

| Mini-Game | Skill Used | Max Tokens/hr |
|:----------|:-----------|:-------------:|
| 🏐 Volleyball | Luck | 15 |
| 🐚 Shell Dive | Diving Level | 30+ |
| 🏖️ Sandcastle | None (random) | 10 |

**Booth Rewards:**
| Item | Tokens | Notes |
|:-----|:------:|:------|
| Pearl Necklace | 30 | +1 Luck, wearable |
| Starfruit Seeds | 15 | Best summer crop |
| Coral Decor Pack | 20 | Unique furniture set |
| Beach Towel | 10 | Home decor |

### 🎆 Fireworks Festival (Summer 20)

A purely social event — no competition. Watch the fireworks with villagers from 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM. You can talk to all NPCs during this time for a friendship boost. The Firework Launcher Blueprint is a mail participation reward.

### 🧀 Summer Feast (Summer 28)

A cooking competition. Bring your best dish to the judging tent. Scoring depends on dish quality, ingredient rarity, and presentation. The Gold Frying Pan is an absolute game-changer — it reduces cooking time by 25%.

**Winning Tip:** Cook a **5-star meal** using Osmium-quality ingredients. Fish-based dishes (Sashimi, Fish Stew) score well because fishing is easy to level early. Save your best fish all summer for this.

## 🍂 Fall Festivals

Fall festivals lean into harvest celebration and friendly rivalry.

| Festival | Day | Time | Type | Key Reward |
|:---------|:---:|:----:|:-----|:-----------|
| 🎃 Harvest Festival | Fall 10 | 10:00 AM | Crop Contest | Golden Pumpkin (decor, 3,000g) |
| 🏆 Animal Festival | Fall 20 | 10:00 AM | Animal Show | Premium Feed Blueprint |
| 🎨 Artisan Fair | Fall 28 | 10:00 AM | Craft Contest | Artisan Star Trophy |

### 🎃 Harvest Festival (Fall 10)

Same format as the Spring Festival but with bigger stakes. Bring your top-quality fall crop. The Golden Pumpkin prize sells for 3,000g and is also a museum piece.

**Winning Tip:** Pumpkin and Sweet Potato are your best bets. Fertilize heavily from Day 1 of fall. A Gold or Osmium-tier crop is almost guaranteed to win.

### 🏆 Animal Festival (Fall 20)

Your animals are judged on friendship level and product quality. This event rewards you for keeping happy, well-fed animals all year. Heavily favors players who invested in auto-feeders and heaters.

**Winning Tip:** Pet every animal daily, keep the radio on in the coop/barn, and use premium feed for two weeks before the contest. A Best Friend (5❤️) animal with Osmium-quality product is unbeatable.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Premium Feed Blueprint + Heater Blueprint
- 🥈 2nd: Quality Fertilizer ×10
- 🥉 3rd: Hay ×50

### 🎨 Artisan Fair (Fall 28)

Showcase your best artisan product — cheese, mayonnaise, cloth, truffle oil, or aged goods. The scoring heavily favors aged/processed items over raw products.

**Winning Tip:** Age your best cheese or mayonnaise in a cask starting in mid-fall. A Gold-aged cheese beats any fresh product even at Osmium quality.

## ❄️ Winter Festivals

Winter is the quietest season for farming, but the events make up for it.

| Festival | Day | Time | Type | Key Reward |
|:---------|:---:|:----:|:-----|:-----------|
| ⛸️ Winter Festival | Winter 10 | 10:00 AM | Ice Skating Race | Ice Skates (accessory) |
| 🕯️ Festival of Lights | Winter 20 | 6:00 PM | Spectacle | Light Decor Blueprint |
| 🌊 Coral Festival | Winter 28 | 10:00 AM | Diving Contest | Mermaid Pendant (rare) |

### ⛸️ Winter Festival (Winter 10)

An ice-skating race across the frozen lake. It's a simple mini-game with directional controls — tap the right direction at the right time to maintain speed. Bad timing makes you stumble and lose precious seconds.

**Winning Tip:** Practice the course once or twice before competing. The optimal path is a straight line with gentle curves — don't over-correct. Stamina doesn't matter here, only your timing.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Ice Skates (+10% movement speed)
- 🥈 2nd: Winter Seeds ×10
- 🥉 3rd: Hot Cocoa ×5 (restores 100 energy)

### 🕯️ Festival of Lights (Winter 20)

A community celebration where everyone lights lanterns. No competition — it's purely social. Chat with every NPC for a big friendship boost. The Light Decor Blueprint is mailed to you the next day.

### 🌊 Coral Festival (Winter 28)

The biggest diving event of the year. You dive into a special area of the ocean and collect rare coral pieces. Points are awarded by rarity and quantity. The Mermaid Pendant is a unique decoration — also contributes to the museum collection.

**Winning Tip:** Upgrade your diving gear to at least Silver tier before winter. The deeper you can dive, the rarer the coral you'll find. Save stamina potions for this event — you'll need every second underwater.

**Prizes:**
- 🥇 1st: Mermaid Pendant + 2,000g
- 🥈 2nd: Rare Shell Collection (sell: 800g)
- 🥉 3rd: Coral Seeds ×3

## 🏆 General Competition Tips

| Tip | Why It Works |
|:----|:-------------|
| 🌱 Keep your highest-quality items in a chest | The game auto-selects from inventory — don't accidentally sell your best item |
| 🎣 Fish early and often | Many events reward high-quality fish products |
| 🧀 Process everything | Processed goods always score higher than raw |
| 🫶 Socialize on festival days | Every festival gives friendship boosts for talking to NPCs |
| 📦 Save one of every season's best crop | You never know which crop will be the secret contest item |
| ⬆️ Upgrade tools before contests | Tool quality affects mini-game performance |
