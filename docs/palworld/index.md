# 🏹 Palworld — Complete Guide & Database

**Palworld** is an open-world survival crafting game by Pocketpair where you capture, train, and befriend mysterious creatures called **Pals**. Use Pals to fight, build, farm, and automate your base. Often described as "Pokémon with guns and automation," it has become one of the most popular games on Steam.

## 🧭 Core Gameplay

| Activity | Description |
|:---------|:------------|
| 🐾 **Catching Pals** | Weaken wild Pals, throw a Pal Sphere, capture them for your team |
| 🏠 **Base Building** | Build bases with production lines, farms, breeding pens, defence walls |
| ⚙️ **Automation** | Assign Pals to workstations — they mine, craft, cook, water, and transport automatically |
| ⚔️ **Combat** | Fight alongside your Pals against wild Pals, Syndicate thugs, and tower bosses |
| 🧬 **Breeding** | Cross-breed Pals to create offspring with inherited traits and rare mutations |
| 🗺️ **Open World** | Explore six major islands with different climates, resources, and Pals |
| 🌱 **Farming** | Plant, water, and harvest crops on your base for food and crafting |
