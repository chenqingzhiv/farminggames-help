## ♻️ Greenhouse & Field Layout

**Greenhouse priority:** 60% Pumpkin (wine) → 25% Strawberry (jam) → 15% Cranberry (jam). Keeps artisan equipment running at full capacity year-round.

| Layout | Crops | Daily Energy | Season Profit |
|:-------|:------|:------------:|:-------------:|
| Cheap & Fast | 20 Carrot + 10 Bamboo | 30 | ~800g |
| Max Profit | 20 Pumpkin + 10 Cranberry | 60 | ~4000g |
| Artisan Focus | 12 Watermelon + 8 Corn + 10 Strawberry | 55 | ~5500g processed |

**Energy budget:** Base 200 energy/day. Deduct 30 for tools. You have ~170 for watering (1 energy/tile, 2 for magic). Max manageable tiles without spells: ~85. With Rain Dance (3×3 area): ~200 tiles.

**Ultimate endgame:** Full greenhouse Pumpkins → Wine Press → Earth Enchantment on all tiles = **~44,000g per 28-day cycle** from 80 tiles with 4 wine presses.
