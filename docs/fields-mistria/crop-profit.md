## Quick Tips

- **Early game:** Plant Carrots (Spring) and Onions (Summer) — cheap and fast-growing for quick cash.
- **Mid game:** Invest in Strawberries (Spring) and Cranberries (Fall) — regrowth crops give sustained income.
- **Late game:** Watermelons (Summer) and Pumpkins (Fall) have the highest single-harvest profit.
- **Processing:** Always run high-volume crops like Tomato, Corn, and Potato through the Preserves Jar for maximum value.
- **Seed Maker:** Use on expensive regrowth crops (Strawberry, Cranberry) to save money on replanting.
