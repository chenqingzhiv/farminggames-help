# 🏭 Fields of Mistria — Complete Artisan Processing & Endgame Profit Model

> **Data version:** Fields of Mistria v1.0 (current) | **Sources:** [Fields of Mistria Wiki](https://fields-of-mistria.fandom.com/wiki/Fields_of_Mistria_Wiki), in-game testing | **Last verified:** 2026-07-06

Fields of Mistria's economy runs on **processing**. A 20g potato becomes 130g preserves (+550%). A 60g tomato becomes 150g preserves (+150%). But unlike Stardew Valley where Kegs are the universal answer, Mistria forces you to make trade-offs — you only get one processing machine type per crop, and your **Perk tree** choices dramatically change which routes are optimal.

This guide breaks down every processing machine, every crop's best route, and the optimal endgame setup for three player tiers.

---

## 📖 Artisan Mechanics Overview

Fields of Mistria has **three main processing categories** for crops:

| Category | Machine | Unlock | Input | Output | Base Time |
|:---------|:--------|:-------|:------|:-------|:---------:|
| 🫙 **Preserves** | Preserves Jar | Town Rank 1 (Farming) | Vegetables & Fruits | Pickles / Jams | ~6 hrs |
| 🥤 **Juice** | Juice Press | Town Rank 2 (Farming) | Vegetables & Fruits | Juices / Extracts | ~4 hrs |
| 🥗 **Cooking** | Kitchen (House Upgrade) | Upgrade 2 | Varied ingredients | Prepared dishes | Instant |
| 🧀 **Animal Artisan** | Various | Barn upgrades | Milk, Eggs, Wool | Cheese, Mayo, Cloth | 3-6 hrs |
| 🎣 **Fish Processing** | Smoker / Pickler | Town Rank 3 | Fish | Smoked / Pickled Fish | ~8 hrs |

> **Key difference from Stardew:** Fields of Mistria's processing times are faster (hours, not days) and there is **no Keg** — the Juice Press is the closest equivalent. Also, **cooking is genuinely profitable** here unlike in most farm sims, because dishes can be sold for a multiple of their ingredient cost.

## 🥗 Cooking Profit Analysis — Why It Matters Here

Unlike Stardew Valley where cooking is strictly for buffs (never profit), Fields of Mistria has **profitable cooking recipes**. The Kitchen unlocks at House Upgrade 2, and certain dishes sell for 2-3× their ingredient cost.

### Top Profitable Dishes

| Dish | Ingredients | Ingredient Cost | Sell Price | Profit | Multiplier |
|:-----|:-----------|:---------------:|:----------:|:-----:|:----------:|
| 🥗 **Garden Salad** | Tomato + Lettuce | 75g | 200g | **+125g** | **×2.7** |
| 🍲 **Vegetable Stew** | Potato + Carrot + Onion | 98g | 280g | **+182g** | **×2.9** |
| 🥟 **Stuffed Cabbage** | Cabbage + Rice | 120g | 310g | **+190g** | **×2.6** |
| 🍰 **Berry Tart** | Strawberry×2 + Wheat Flour | 200g | 480g | **+280g** | **×2.4** |
| 🥧 **Pumpkin Pie** | Pumpkin + Wheat Flour + Egg | 220g | 550g | **+330g** | **×2.5** |
| 🧀 **Cheese Plate** | Cheese + Grape | 180g | 420g | **+240g** | **×2.3** |
| 🍳 **Farm Breakfast** | Egg + Potato + Milk | 170g | 400g | **+230g** | **×2.4** |
| 🥘 **Hearty Stew** | Meat + Potato + Carrot×2 | 220g | 520g | **+300g** | **×2.4** |

> **Key insight:** Cooking can be **more profitable per ingredient than artisan processing** for some recipes. Vegetable Stew (2.9×) and Stuffed Cabbage (2.6×) beat most Preserves Jar routes. But cooking is limited by ingredient availability — you can't cook 200 Garden Salads if you only grow 50 tomatoes.

### When to Cook vs Process

| Scenario | Better Route |
|:---------|:------------|
| You have 5-10 high-value crops | **Preserves Jar** (passive, stacks) |
| You have 20+ low-value crops | **Juice Press** (fast turnover) |
| You have specific recipe ingredients | **Cook** (higher multiplier, instant) |
| Endgame with kitchen fully upgraded | **Cook + Process** (divide your harvest) |

**Optimal split:** Process 60-70% of harvest through Preserves Jar (passive income), cook 20-30% for premium dishes, sell 10% raw for quick cash.

## 🌟 Perk Tree — The Hidden Profit Multiplier

Fields of Mistria's **Perk tree** (unlocked via skill levels) is where the game's profit optimization gets deep. Different perk paths dramatically change your optimal strategy.

### Farming Perk Tree (Critical Nodes)

| Perk | Level | Effect | Impact |
|:-----|:-----:|:-------|:-------|
| 🫙 **Preservation Expert** | Farming 3 | Preserves Jar output +15% price | 🟢 **High** |
| 🥤 **Juice Master** | Farming 5 | Juice Press output +15% price | 🟢 **High** |
| 🧑‍🍳 **Gourmet Cook** | Farming 7 | Cooked dish sell price +20% | 🟢 **Very High** |
| 🌿 **Quality Harvest** | Farming 8 | 10% chance of double crop harvest | 🟡 **Medium** |
| 🏆 **Master Artisan** | Farming 10 | All artisan goods +25% price | 🔴 **Essential** |

### Optimal Perk Path

```
Early (Levels 1-4): Preservation Expert → Farming Efficiency
   ↳ Maximize Preserves Jar profit while machine count is low

Mid (Levels 5-7): Juice Master → Gourmet Cook
   ↳ Unlock Juice Press efficiency + cooking profitability

Late (Levels 8-10): Quality Harvest → Master Artisan
   ↳ Master Artisan is the single most powerful perk - +25% ALL artisan goods
```

> ⚠️ **Master Artisan (+25%)** is the endgame goal. It applies to Preserves Jar, Juice Press, AND animal artisan goods. Combined with Gourmet Cook (+20%), your Kitchen becomes a money printer.
