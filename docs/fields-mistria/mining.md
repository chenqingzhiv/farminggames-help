# ⛏️ Fields of Mistria — Mining & Combat Deep Dive

Mining in Fields of Mistria is more than an ore run — it's the game's primary combat arena, gear progression pipeline, and mid-game money engine. The mines are gated by story progression and hold 20 floors of increasing danger, each with unique enemies, ores, and loot tables.

---

## 🧱 Mine Structure & Progression

| Aspect | Details |
|:-------|:--------|
| **Total Floors** | 20 (unlocked in 5-floor tiers via story milestones) |
| **Tier 1** | Floors 1–5 (after completing Spring quest "Earthquake Aftermath") |
| **Tier 2** | Floors 6–10 (after reaching Town Rank B) |
| **Tier 3** | Floors 11–15 (after restoring the mine cart) |
| **Tier 4** | Floors 16–20 (endgame, requires Town Rank S) |
| **Shafts** | Random ladder + guaranteed exit at every floor |

> **Tip:** Unlike Stardew Valley, there is **no elevator** in Fields of Mistria. You must clear each floor's threat room to unlock the ladder. Once cleared, the floor stays cleared — you can revisit without fighting again.

### Floor Composition Table

| Floors | Theme | Primary Ores | Common Enemies | Boss |
|:------|:------|:-------------|:---------------|:-----|
| 1–5 | Dirt Cavern | Copper, Tin | Green Slimes, Cave Bats | King Slime (F5) |
| 6–10 | Stone Depths | Iron, Coal | Blue Slimes, Stone Golems, Crystal Bats | Golem Guardian (F10) |
| 11–15 | Magma Core | Gold, Rubies | Red Slimes, Lava Bats, Magma Golems | Magma Warden (F15) |
| 16–20 | Abyssal Vault | Mythril, Gems | Purple Slimes, Shadow Bats, Abyss Golems | Void Construct (F20) |

## 🪄 Magic Combat

Magic is Fields of Mistria's unique combat mechanic — it pulls from a shared mana pool (recharges slowly, or instantly via mana potions).

| Spell | Cost | Effect | Best Against |
|:------|:----:|:-------|:-------------|
| 🔥 Fireball | 15 MP | 30–50 damage, burn DoT 3s | Slimes (all colors), Golems |
| 💧 Water Jet | 12 MP | 20–35 damage, slows enemy | Red Slimes, Lava Bats |
| 🌍 Earth Spike | 18 MP | 40–60 damage, stun 1.5s | Blue Slimes, Abyss Golem |
| ✨ Light Burst | 25 MP | 50–70 damage, AoE | Shadow Bats, Purples |

**Recommended Loadout:**
- **General floors:** Fireball (cheap, effective against most enemies)
- **Floor 6–10:** Earth Spike (Golems are the main threat)
- **Floor 11–15:** Water Jet (Lava enemies resist fire)
- **Floor 16–20:** Light Burst (necessary for Shadow enemies)

> **Tip:** Don't spam magic. Use 2–3 Fireballs to soften a group, then mop up with melee. Mana potions cost 250g each at the Adventurer's Guild — use them only on boss floors.

## 👑 Boss Strategies

### King Slime (Floor 5)

| Stat | Value |
|:----|:------|
| HP | 250 |
| Phase 1 | Jumps toward you, leaving slime puddles (damage zone) |
| Phase 2 (< 50% HP) | Splits into 3 small slimes (each 40 HP) — kill all to win |
| Recommended Gear | Copper Armor + Copper Blade |
| Strategy | Keep moving in a circle. Fireball from range. When it splits, kill the small slimes ASAP — they have no attack but extend the fight |

### Golem Guardian (Floor 10)

| Stat | Value |
|:----|:------|
| HP | 450 |
| Attacks | Ground slam (AoE, slow), Charge (linear), Rock throw (ranged) |
| Weak Spot | Back — takes 50% more damage from behind |
| Recommended Gear | Iron Gear + Iron Broadsword |
| Strategy | Dodge roll through its charge attack to get behind it. Earth Spike stuns it for 3 hits. Never stand still — the slam AoE covers 3 tiles |

### Magma Warden (Floor 15)

| Stat | Value |
|:----|:------|
| HP | 700 |
| Attacks | Fire breath (cone, 80 damage), Magma puddle (DoT zone), Tail sweep (180°) |
| Immune | Fire magic (0 damage) |
| Recommended Gear | Gold Armor + Gold Saber + Sapphire Ring |
| Strategy | This fight is a DPS check. Use Water Jet exclusively (4× damage). When it breathes fire, dodge behind it. The magma puddles last 20 seconds — don't trap yourself in a corner |

### Void Construct (Floor 20)

| Stat | Value |
|:----|:------|
| HP | 1200 |
| Attacks | Shadow beam (linear, 100 damage), Void orbs (homing, 40 damage each), Summon adds (2 Shadow Bats) |
| Immune | Physical (−75%), Shadow (immune) |
| Recommended Gear | Full Mythril Gear + Amethyst Ring |
| Strategy | The hardest fight in the mines. Use Light Burst exclusively. Kill summoned Shadow Bats instantly (they heal the boss on contact). When Void orbs appear, kite them into walls — they despawn after hitting terrain |

**Best money-makers by floor tier:**
- Floor 1–5: Mine **Copper Ore** but don't bother selling — save for upgrades. Money comes from gem nodes (Ruby, Emerald).

- Floor 11–15: **Treasure chests** appear on every 3rd floor. Each averages 800g.
- Floor 16–20: **Mythril Ore** is the jackpot — but keep every piece. Money here comes from Abyss Golem Golem Core drops (120g each, 30% drop rate).

## 🚀 Deep Mining Tips: Reaching Floor 20 Efficiently

1. **Pre-mine prep:** Bring 10 Meals (any cooked dish that restores 30+ energy) + 3 Mana Potions + Pickaxe + Best Weapon
2. **Clear path first, loot later:** Each floor has one threat room. Kill the enemies, take the ladder, then backtrack for ore. Enemies don't respawn after the ladder is unlocked.
3. **Skip non-threatening fights:** Cave Bats and Blue Slimes on later floors waste time. If they're not between you and the ladder, ignore them.
4. **Use pickaxe on golems:** Stone Golems take 3 pickaxe hits to stun, during which you can mine freely. Don't waste weapon durability on them.
5. **Stamina management:** Mining costs 4 stamina per swing, fighting costs 2. Keep a mental budget — if you're under 20 stamina on floor 14, eat a meal before the boss. Magma Warden will oneshot you if you can't dodge.
6. **Best time to mine:** Rainy days. Crops don't need watering, giving you 2 extra hours in the mines.
7. **Floor reset trick:** If a floor spawns too many Crystal Bats (magic resistant), exit to the mine entrance and re-enter — enemy composition reshuffles. This costs 1 in-game hour, so only do it on floors 13+.

> **Tip:** The **Mine Cart system** (unlocked at floor 10) costs 20 Iron Bars to repair but is worth every ingot. It lets you warp directly to floor 10, 15, or 20 from the entrance, saving 15+ minutes of walking per run.
