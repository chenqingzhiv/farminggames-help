## 🔥 How Cooking Works

### The Cauldron
- Craft a **Cauldron** at the Workbench (8 Iron Bar + 4 Copper Bar)
- Place it in your base
- **Interact** to open the cooking menu

### Cooking Mechanics
| Mechanic | Detail |
|:---------|:-------|
| **Ingredients** | Combine 1-4 ingredients in the cauldron |
| **Recipes** | Specific combos produce specific dishes |
| **Buffs** | Most cooked foods give 1-2 stat buffs |
| **Duration** | Buffs last 5-15 minutes (real time) |
| **Stacking** | You can have 1 food buff + 1 potion buff + 1 seasoning active |
| **Quality** | Higher quality ingredients → better buff values |

### Acquiring the Cauldron
1. Defeat **Glurch** and **Ghom**
2. Mine **Iron Ore** in the Clay/Larva biomes
3. Craft **Iron Bars** at the Furnace
4. Build your **Cauldron** at the Workbench

## 🧪 Advanced Cooking Tips

1. **Don't sell boss meat!** Boss meat dishes are far more valuable than selling the raw meat
2. **Grow peppers** — Spicy Pepper is the most versatile cooking ingredient (used in 8+ recipes)
3. **Quality matters** — higher farming skill produces higher quality crops = better buffs
4. **Fish traps + cooking = infinite gold** — catch fish, cook them, sell the cooked version
5. **The Kitchen Counter** (late game upgrade) lets you cook 4 dishes at once
6. **Refrigerator** (late game) extends food buff duration by 50%
7. **Seasons** — in 1.0, placing salt, pepper, or sugar on a dish enhances its buffs
