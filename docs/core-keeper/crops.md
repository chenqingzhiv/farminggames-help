## 🧮 How Farming Works in Core Keeper

Core Keeper uses a **real-time** growth system (not day-based like Stardew). Seeds grow in **minutes** of real time, and crops need:

1. **Tilled soil** — use a Hoe on dirt or ground
2. **Water** — use a Watering Can or sprinklers (water once per growth cycle)
3. **Light** — torches or glow tulips nearby (optional but speeds growth)

> **Growth formula:** Base growth time × (1 − Water bonus − Light bonus). With water and light, crops grow ~40% faster.

## 🌍 Farming by Biome

### 🟫 Dirt Biome (Starting Area)
| Seed Source | What You Get | Strategy |
|:------------|:-------------|:---------|
| Break grass | Carrot Seeds | Plant immediately for food + cash |
| Slime drops | Mixed seeds | Save for later biomes |

### 🟥 Clay Biome
Accessible after defeating Glurch. Clay soil is ideal for:
- **Wheat** — needed for Bread (best early healing food)
- **Pepper** — decent profit, good for Spicy Cuisine

### 🟩 Meadow / Forgotten Ruins
After Ghorm is defeated:
- **Pumpkin** — your best mid-game cash crop
- **Cotton** — needed for armour progression

### 🟦 Sunken Sea (Underwater Farming)
Requires a Diving Set or underwater base. Crops here grow well with natural water:
- **Rice** — grows fast, good food source
- **Coffee** — infinite harvests, great for speed buffs
- **Lotus / Seaflower** — crafting materials

### 🟧 Desert of Beginnings
- **Pineapple** — highest base profit crop
- **Grape** — good profit, fast growth

### 🔥 Forlorn Metropolis (Endgame)
- **Fire Pepper** — best profit per minute in the game
- Requires Galaxite-tier tools to survive this biome

## 🌿 Gardening Skill Guide

Leveling your **Gardening skill** unlocks major farming bonuses:

| Level | Unlock | Effect |
|:------|:-------|:-------|
| 1 | Basic Hoe | Start tilling |
| 2 | Watering Can (Copper) | Water 3 tiles at once |
| 5 | Crop Quality Chance | Base 10% chance of +1 yield |
| 8 | Sprinkler (Tin) | Automates watering |
| 12 | Watering Can (Iron) | Water 5 tiles at once |
| 15 | Double Harvest Chance | 15% chance of double yield |
| 18 | Sprinkler (Gold) | Larger range |
| 22 | Watering Can (Gold) | Water 7 tiles at once |
| 25 | Master Farmer | +25% growth speed, +25% yield |

> **Fast leveling method:** Plant Carrots (fastest growth) in bulk, harvest, replant. Each harvest gives 10–30 XP depending on yield.

| Sprinkler Type | Materials | Range | Water per Cycle |
|:--------------|:----------|:------|:---------------|
| 🔵 **Tin Sprinkler** | 10 Tin Bars, 5 Fibre | 3×3 (1 tile radius) | 1 water |
| 🟡 **Gold Sprinkler** | 10 Gold Bars, 5 Copper, 3 Fibre | 5×5 (2 tile radius) | 1 water |
| 🟣 **Scarlett Sprinkler** | 8 Scarlett Bars, 10 Gold, 5 Fibre | 7×7 (3 tile radius) | 1 water |

> **Automation tip:** Connect sprinklers to a water source via canals or place them on tilled soil. One watering per growth cycle is all a crop needs. Pair with conveyor belts for auto-harvest collection.
