# 🆕 Core Keeper — Getting Started Guide for Beginners

Welcome, Explorer! This guide walks you through **your first hours** in Core Keeper — from waking up in the underground to defeating your first boss.

---

## 🥇 First 10 Minutes: What to Do

When you first spawn, you're in a small cave with nothing but a broken pickaxe and a glowing core.

### 1️⃣ Gather Basics
- **Punch trees** (the glowing mushrooms/crystals) for wood
- **Pick up stone** from the ground
- **Collect fibre** from ground plants
- **Open your starter chest** for a basic hoe and watering can

### 2️⃣ Build Your First Workbench
- Press **Tab** to open your inventory
- Craft a **Workbench** (10 wood)
- Place it with right-click

### 3️⃣ Craft Essential Tools
At your workbench, craft:
| Tool | Materials | Use |
|:-----|:----------|:----|
| 🪓 **Wooden Pickaxe** | 5 wood, 3 fibre | Mine copper/tin |
| 🗡️ **Copper Dagger** | 5 copper bars | Basic melee weapon |
| 🔦 **Torch** | 1 wood, 3 fibre | Light your way |
| 🪣 **Basic Hoe** | 5 wood, 3 fibre | Till soil for farming |
| 🛡️ **Wooden Shield** | 10 wood | Block attacks |

### 4️⃣ Explore and Light Up
- Place torches as you explore — monsters spawn in the dark
- **Don't wander too far** from the Core (your spawn point)
- Follow the dirt paths to find the first biome exits

## 🏠 Building Your First Base

### Where to Build
Build your base **near the Core** — it's your fast-travel hub and safe zone. The area around it is monster-free.

### Essential Structures
| Structure | Materials | Why You Need It |
|:----------|:----------|:----------------|
| 🛠️ **Workbench** | 10 wood | All early crafting |
| 🔥 **Campfire** | 10 wood, 5 fibre | Cook food, light |
| ⛏️ **Furnace** | 10 copper bars | Smelt copper/tin |
| 🗄️ **Chest** | 10 wood | Store items |
| 🛏️ **Bed** | 15 wood, 10 fibre | Set spawn point |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Place a **Bed** near the Core. But don't place it inside the Core's bubble — put it just outside so you can spawn closer to your base.

## 🌱 Getting Started with Farming

Farming is one of the most important skills in Core Keeper. Here's how to start:

1. **Craft a Hoe** at the workbench
2. **Till soil** anywhere with right-click
3. **Plant seeds** you find from breaking grass or slimes
4. **Water regularly** — crops need one watering and then grow on their own
5. **Harvest** when fully grown (they glow slightly)

**Your first seeds** come from:
- Breaking grass in the Dirt Biome → Carrot Seeds
- Killing slimes → random seeds
- Opening chests in the ruins

> See our 🌱 [Crop & Farming Guide](/core-keeper/crops/) for complete crop data and profit tables.

## ⚔️ Combat Basics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|:---------|:-------------|
| **Melee Attack** | Left-click with weapon equipped |
| **Block** | Right-click with a shield equipped |
| **Dodge Roll** | Double-tap a movement direction |
| **Ranged** | Use a bow or slingshot from distance |
| **Healing** | Eat food or use bandages (crafted from fibre) |

### Early Combat Tips
- **Kite enemies** — hit, move back, repeat
- **Always carry food** — passive health regen is tied to being well-fed
- **Prioritize Glurch** (first boss) → craft better gear
- **Avoid larva spit** — it poisons you

## 🎯 10 Pro Tips for New Players

1. **Build a hoe immediately** — wild seeds are free food
2. **Don't sell early ore** — you'll need it all for upgrades
3. **Always carry torches** — darkness = monster spawns
4. **Cook your food** — cooked meals give better buffs
5. **Invest in movement speed** — boots and food buffs save time
6. **Build a moat** around your base for early defence
7. **Use conveyor belts** for auto-sorting once you have tin
8. **Fishing is OP** — fish give great healing and buffs
9. **Explore in one direction** — the map is infinite but biomes are in sequence
10. **Keep the Core upgraded** — it unlocks fast travel and vendor access
