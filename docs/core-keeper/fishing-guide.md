## 🎣 How Fishing Works

### 🛠️ Unlocking Fishing
1. Defeat **Glurch** or **Ghom** (first two bosses)
2. Collect **Fibre** from grass plants
3. Craft a **Basic Fishing Rod** at the Workbench (8 Fibre + 6 Wood)
4. Find any water pool — **press and hold left-click** to cast, **release** when the bobber splashes!

### 📈 Fishing Skill
- Level up by catching fish
- Higher level = better catch quality + rare fish chances
- Skill rewards: faster casting, less reel time, rare fish detection

### ⏰ Time & Location
- Fishing takes **real time** — no day/night cycle
- Different biomes = different fish pools
- Deeper/larger water pools = better fish
- **Fish Traps** (crafted later) provide passive fish income

## 🪤 Fish Trap Guide

### Crafting Recipes
| Trap Type | Materials | Catch Rate | Notes |
|:----------|:----------|:-----------|:------|
| Basic Fish Trap | 25 Fibre + 12 Wood | Low | Crafted at Workbench |
| Sturdy Fish Trap | 20 Fibre + 8 Iron Bar | Medium | Crafted at Iron Anvil |
| Advanced Fish Trap | 15 Fibre + 6 Galaxite Bar | High | Faster collection |

### Placement Tips
1. **Deep water** produces better fish than shallow puddles
2. **Large water bodies** attract rarer fish
3. Place traps **away from each other** (cannibalize catch rates)
4. Check traps every **20-30 minutes** — they stop catching when full
5. The **Sunken Sea** has the best trap ROI

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Upgrade your rod first** — a better rod pays for itself in rare fish
2. **Cook your fish** — many fish are worth 2-3x more as cooked dishes
3. **Fish traps are OP** — 5 traps generate more gold than most crops
4. **The Sunken Sea is the best fishing zone** — highest value fish + Glow Tuna for breathing buff
5. **Legendary fish** require high fishing skill + upgraded rod — save them for the museum!
6. **Sell duplicates** — you only need one of each fish for museum/achievement
7. **Fish during exploration** — every biome has unique fish, so fish as you explore
