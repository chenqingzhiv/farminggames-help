# 🎣 My Time at Sandrock — Fishing & Bug Catching Complete Guide

Sandrock's desert environment isn't short on water — you just need to know where to look. From oases to waterfall lakes, underground caves to deep desert pools, 14 catchable fish each have their own quirks. Combined with the insect catching system, fishing isn't just relaxing — it's a major mid-game income source.

---

## 🔓 Fishing Unlock

**Trigger:** Complete the main quest **"First Fishing Lesson"** (around Day 3-4). Head to Sandrock's Waterfall Lake and talk to **Catori** to receive your first rod — the basic **"Dryland Rod"**.

> 💡 **Tip:** Don't rush to Waterfall Lake right after getting the rod. First, craft **2-3 Basic Bait** (Wood ×2 + Bug ×1) back at your workshop — it boosts fishing efficiency by 50%. Without bait, waiting time for a bite doubles.

## 🎮 Fishing Minigame Mechanics

Sandrock's fishing minigame has **3 phases:**

### 1️⃣ Casting
Hold left mouse button to charge and set casting distance. The bar is split into 3 zones:
- **Near Zone (Green):** Small fish near the shore
- **Mid Zone (Yellow):** General-purpose casting distance
- **Far Zone (Red):** Needs a high-tier rod to reliably catch rare fish

### 2️⃣ Waiting
Once bait is in the water, ripples indicate a fish approaching. **Bite timing** depends on:
- Bait quality (better bait = faster bites)
- The fish species' activity level
- Time of day (bite rate +30% at sunrise/sunset)

### 3️⃣ Reeling
When a fish bites, a **tension bar** appears with White/Green/Red segments:
- **Hold mouse button:** Drains fish stamina (Green zone)
- **Release mouse button:** Recovers rod tension (White zone)
- **Bar turns red:** Line is about to snap — release immediately!

**✅ Technique:** Let go when the fish struggles (red flash), then hold again when it stops. Rhythm is **hold 2 seconds → release 1 second**. Higher-star fish need more rounds.
