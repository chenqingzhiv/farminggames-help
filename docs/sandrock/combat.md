# ⚔️ My Time at Sandrock — Ruins & Combat Guide

Combat in Sandrock is fundamentally different from typical farming sims. You enter procedurally generated ruins, fight waves of rogue AI and monsters, and extract blueprints, data discs, and relics. Master two ruin types, four weapon classes, and a layered gear progression system.

---

## 🏛️ Ruin Types: Breach vs Paradise

### Breach Ruins (Story)

| Attribute | Details |
|:----------|:--------|
| **Purpose** | Story progression, unlocks new areas and blueprints |
| **Layout** | Linear, hand-crafted — same layout every run |
| **Entry Cost** | 5 Data Discs per floor tier |
| **Floor Count** | 1–3 floors per breach, fixed |
| **Difficulty** | Scales with story chapter (Ch 2 = easy, Ch 5+ = hard) |
| **Rewards** | Story relics, unique blueprints, EXP |

### Paradise Ruins (Endless)

| Attribute | Details |
|:----------|:--------|
| **Purpose** | Loot farming, EXP grinding, rare blueprint drops |
| **Layout** | Procedurally generated — 3–5 floors per run |
| **Entry Cost** | 10–50 Data Discs (increases with star level) |
| **Star Tiers** | ⭐ (Lv 10) → ⭐⭐⭐ (Lv 25) → ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Lv 45+) |
| **Difficulty** | Every 2 star tiers adds new enemy types and loot pools |

### Star Tier Unlock Table

| Stars | Recommended Level | Entry Cost | Best Loot |
|:------|:-----------------:|:-----------|:----------|
| ⭐ | 10–15 | 10 Discs | Bronze gear, Common relics |
| ⭐⭐ | 15–22 | 15 Discs | Iron gear, Uncommon relics |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | 25–30 | 20 Discs | Steel gear, Data Discs (×2 drops) |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 35–40 | 30 Discs | Titanium gear, Rare blueprints |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 45+ | 50 Discs | Hazmat suit parts, Legendary relics |

> **Tip:** Do NOT waste Data Discs on Paradise Ruins ⭐ runs. Rush to ⭐⭐ or ⭐⭐⭐ by grinding Breach Ruins first. The loot quality jump from ⭐ to ⭐⭐ is massive — you get gear with actual stat bonuses instead of vendor trash.

## 🗡️ Weapon Classes: Complete Comparison

Weapon choice determines your entire combat rhythm. Leveling a weapon type unlocks special moves at level 5 and level 10.

| Stat | 🗡️ Dagger | ⚔️ Sword | 🔱 Spear | 🔫 Gun |
|:-----|:----------:|:---------:|:---------:|:------:|
| **Base Damage** | 60–80% | 100% | 110–130% | 140–170% |
| **Attack Speed** | Very Fast | Fast | Medium | Slow |
| **Stamina/Attack** | 3 | 5 | 7 | 10 |
| **Range** | Melee (1 tile) | Melee (1.5 tiles) | Melee (2.5 tiles) | Ranged (8 tiles) |
| **Special (Lv 5)** | Backstab (3× crit) | Whirlwind (AoE) | Charge (piercing) | Sniper Shot (2× dmg) |
| **Special (Lv 10)** | Shadow Step (invuln) | Parry Counter | Impale (DoT) | Grenade (AoE 150%) |
| **Best Grinding Tier** | ⭐–⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐–⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐–⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐–⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Sell Price Multiplier** | 0.8× | 1.0× | 1.2× | 1.5× |

### Weapon Recommendations

| Stage | Best Weapon | Why |
|:------|:------------|:----|
| Early (Lv 1–15) | **Iron Sword** | Balanced for new players, good clear speed |
| Mid (Lv 15–30) | **Steel Spear** | Range keeps you safe, charge attack pierces groups |
| Late (Lv 30–45) | **Titanium Gun** | Range + damage lets you kite all enemies safely |
| Endgame (Lv 45+) | **Legendary Spear** | Best DPS vs bosses; gun ammo costs become prohibitive |

> **Tip:** Always carry a **backup weapon** of a different class. A dagger's high crit and zero stamina cost on the first swing makes it excellent for opening every encounter, even if you main spear. Switch to your main weapon after the Backstab.

## 👑 Boss Fights

### Story Boss Overview

| Boss | Chapter | Recommended Lv | HP | Attack Patterns | Strategy |
|:-----|:-------:|:--------------:|:--:|:----------------|:---------|
| **Logan (First Encounter)** | Ch 2 | 12 | 500 | Charge → Sword swing → Throw dagger | Dodge the charge sideways (not backward — it tracks). Attack after throw, he recovers 2s |
| **Stev & Haru** | Ch 3 | 20 | 800 / 600 | Stev: Hammer slam + ground shockwave. Haru: Rapid fire (3 shots) | Separate them. Kill Haru first (lower HP, ranged focus). Use Spear's range to keep Stev at distance |
| **Pen (Cave)** | Ch 4 | 28 | 1200 | Laser beam + Teleport punch + Summon bots | Laser is telegraphed (2s charge). Always face away from walls so you don't get cornered. Bring Heat Resist |
| **Aadit (Berserk)** | Ch 5 | 35 | 2000 | Rage swings (3-hit combo) + Ground pound (AoE) + Regeneration | DPS race. Bring 3 All-Resist Elixirs and your best Titanium weapon. Hit, back off, repeat — don't greed |
| **Guardian (Final)** | Ch 6 | 45+ | 4000 | All previous boss moves + Orbital laser + Summon minions + Phase 2 beam storm | Requires full Hazmat set. Phase 2 (≤25% HP) spawns a rotating beam — stay opposite it. Bring 5 Panaceas |

### Boss Farming Route (Post-Story)

Once you've beaten the story, **Guardian** is the best boss to farm:
- Drops: Legendary Fragment (15%), Titanium Bar ×5 (40%), Hazmat Formula (3%)
- Rerun cost: 30 Data Discs
- Time per kill: 3–5 minutes with endgame gear

## ⭐ Item Quality System

Every crafted item in Sandrock has a **star rating** that affects base stats.

| Stars | Stat Multiplier | Sell Price Multiplier | How to Get |
|:------|:---------------:|:---------------------:|:-----------|
| ⭐ (Base) | 1.0× | 1.0× | Default craft |
| ⭐⭐ | 1.15× | 1.3× | Perfect craft + refining |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | 1.35× | 1.6× | Perfect craft + Quality Gem |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 1.6× | 2.0× | Master craft + Legendary Gem |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 2.0× | 3.0× | Critical success (rare) + all bonuses |

### Quality Gem Sources
- **Quality Gem** (★★★): Paradise ⭐⭐⭐ boss drop (20%)
- **Legendary Gem** (★★★★): Guardian boss (10%)
- **Critical Success** (★★★★★): Random (1%) when crafting with max skill level
