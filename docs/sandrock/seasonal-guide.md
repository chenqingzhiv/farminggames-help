# 📅 My Time at Sandrock — Seasonal & Monthly Guide

Every day in the desert counts. Sandrock has **12 months** per year, 28 days each. This guide breaks down the best Year 1 rhythm month by month.

---

## 🗓️ Climate System Overview

| Quarter | Months | Characteristics | Effects |
|:--------|:-------|:----------------|:--------|
| ☀️ Dry Season | 1-6 | Hot and dry | Water consumption +50% |
| 🌧️ Rainy Season | 7-12 | Occasional rain | Water consumption -30%, mushroom growth |
| 🌪️ Sandstorm Period | 3, 6, 9 | Low visibility | Outdoor efficiency -50%, recommend ruins |

---

## 📆 Month-by-Month Guide (Year 1)

### Month 1: Rebuilding (D1-28)
★★★★★ Complete tutorial → Build stone furnace + workbench (Day 1)
★★★★ Sweep the map for scrap, copper ore → Build Dew Collector
★★★★ Accept commissions daily, prioritize high-reward ones

> **💡 Tip:** Don't farm in Month 1! Water is extremely scarce. Forage cactus and grill it for food.

### Month 2: Getting Established (D29-56)
★★★★★ Upgrade to copper pickaxe + copper axe
★★★★ Explore Breach ruins for yellow data discs
★★★ Buy your first chicken + plant sandrice ×10

**🎉 Founding Day (M2 D15):** Building contest. Prepare Copper Bars ×10 + Iron Bars ×5 + Hardwood ×20. Winner gets "Desert Lamp" blueprint + 3,000 Gols.

### Month 3: Sandstorm (D57-84)
★★★★★ Spend sandstorm days in Breach second floor farming discs
★★★★ Upgrade Workshop to Lv.4, unlock Blender/Oven blueprints
★★★ Build cheese press + buy a goat

### Month 4: Expansion (D85-112)
★★★★★ Upgrade house to Medium → Bedroom energy +25%
★★★★ Buy chili/tomato seeds from the store
★★★ Tame a mount + build a loom

### Months 5-6: Sprint (D113-168)
★★★★★ Unlock Gecko Station ruins + upgrade to iron pickaxe
★★★★ Cooking Contest (M5 D15) — stock up on 3⭐ ingredients early
★★★ Buy a cow + upgrade house to Large

**🎉 Harvest Festival (M6 D15):** Crop quality competition. Bring 3⭐+ rare crops → Golden Watering Can + fertilizer blueprints.

### Months 7-8: Rainy Season (D169-224)
★★★★★ Explore Paradise Lost, upgrade to manganese steel tools
★★★★ Collect rainy season mushrooms + use rainfall to expand farm
★★★ Tame a camel + research silicon wafers in the lab

### Months 9-12: Late Game (D225-336)
★★★★★ Luxury house + Sandrock Depths for titanium ore
★★★★★ Main story completion push
★★★ NPC 10-heart sprint + titanium alloy tools
