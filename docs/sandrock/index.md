# 🏜️ My Time at Sandrock — Game Overview

## About the Game

**My Time at Sandrock** is an open-world crafting RPG developed by **Pathea Games**, set in the same universe as *My Time at Portia*. Released in **November 2023**, this sequel moves from Portia's lush valley to a harsh post-apocalyptic desert town. As a new Builder, you must revitalize Sandrock by crafting machinery, building relationships, and fending off environmental threats.

## 🔧 Workshop System

Unlike traditional farming sims, Sandrock revolves around your **Workshop** — a crafting-based business. You accept commissions from townsfolk, build machines like furnaces and grinders, and manufacture everything from simple nails to advanced engines. Your workshop rank determines access to better blueprints and higher-paying commissions.

### Core Workshop Machines

| Machine | Materials Needed | Purpose |
|:--------|:----------------|:--------|
| ⌛ Furnace | Copper ore + Stone | Smelt basic bars |
| ⚙️ Grinder | Wood + Stone | Process sand and ores |
| 🔩 Assembly Station | Iron bars + Circuits | Build complex items |
| 🪚 Worktable | Wood | Craft starter parts |

## 🏙️ Desert Setting & Exploration

Sandrock's harsh desert environment is central to the experience. Water is scarce and must be managed carefully — you'll need a **dew collector** for hydration and a reliable supply chain for materials. The world is filled with ancient ruins, hazardous ruins for combat-based loot runs, and scattered oases that serve as rest points.

> **Tip:** In the first week, focus entirely on filling commissions for the town board rather than hoarding materials. Each successful commission raises your workshop rank and unlocks better blueprints exponentially faster than free-building. Accept every commission you can physically fulfill.
