# 🎪 My Time at Sandrock Festivals & Events — Complete Guide

Sandrock's festivals are a highlight of desert life. Unlike Coral Island's relaxed pace, Sandrock's events are competitive — you're up against sharp NPCs who bring their best goods. Winning takes preparation, quality materials, and knowing the rules. This guide covers every festival, what to bring, and how to take first place.

> **Tip:** Festivals in Sandrock often give unique blueprints as prizes. Missing a festival means waiting a full year for that blueprint. Set in-game reminders.

---

## 📅 Festival System Overview

Sandrock runs on a seasonal calendar with three major festivals plus several rotating mini-events. The town board posts the schedule at the start of each season.

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|:---------|:-------------|
| 📬 Announcement | Town board + mail 3 days before |
| ⏰ Festival Hours | Usually 8:00 AM — 6:00 PM |
| 🏆 Judging | NPCs bring their own entries — your quality beats theirs |
| 🎁 Participation Prize | Everyone gets something, even last place |
| 🪙 Festival Tokens | Earned at events, spent at prize stalls |
| 🏅 Ranked Prizes | 1st, 2nd, 3rd place get different rewards |

### How Judging Works

Each competitive festival uses a scoring system based on item quality:

| Quality Tier | Score Multiplier | How to Get |
|:-------------|:---------------:|:-----------|
| ⭐ Normal | 1× | Base quality |
| ⭐⭐ Good | 1.5× | Quality fertilizer, higher skill |
| ⭐⭐⭐ Excellent | 2× | Premium fertilizer, high skill |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Perfect | 3× | Max quality + luck bonuses |

> **Tip:** Use **Perfect-quality fertilizer** on crops you intend to enter in festivals. A Perfect-quality item scores 3× higher than Normal — that's often enough to beat even the best NPC entries with room to spare.

## ☀️ Day of the Bright Sun (Winter)

**Date:** Winter 15  
**Type:** Combat Tournament + Celebration

The biggest event in Sandrock's calendar. Part gladiator arena, part town celebration, the Day of the Bright Sun is a two-part festival.

### Phase 1: The Tournament

A bracket-style combat tournament held in the town square arena. You face off against 5-6 NPCs in 1v1 combat. Each round gets harder.

| Round | Opponent | Difficulty | Recommended Weapon Level |
|:------|:---------|:-----------:|:------------------------:|
| 1 | Local Townsfolk | Easy | Any |
| 2 | Grace | Medium | Iron+ |
| 3 | Mi-an | Medium | Steel+ |
| 4 | Burgess | Hard | Steel+ |
| 5 | Owen | Very Hard | Manganese+ |
| 6 (Final) | Mysterious Challenger | Extreme | Titanium+ |

**Winning Tips:**
- **Equip a weapon with lifesteal** — you don't heal between rounds
- **Block more than you dodge** — blocking is more stamina-efficient in 1v1
- **Bring healing food** — eat between rounds (the game lets you)
- **Focus on knockback attacks** — pushing opponents into walls stuns them

### Phase 2: The Feast

After the tournament, there's a town feast from 6:00 PM to midnight. Every NPC attends. This is the best time in the entire year to socialize — you can talk to everyone in one spot and get friendship boosts from all of them.

### Prizes

| Place | Prize | Value |
|:------|:------|:------|
| 🥇 1st | Bright Sun Trophy (decor) + 3,000 Gols | Unique decor |
| 🥈 2nd | Quality Weapon Crate + 1,500 Gols | Random rare weapon |
| 🥉 3rd | Combat Boost Kit + 800 Gols | Buff items |
| Participation | Bright Sun Token ×5 | Trade at prize booth |

## 🧧 Lunar New Year (Spring)

**Date:** Spring 22  
**Type:** Gift Exchange + Festival

Sandrock's Lunar New Year celebration is a cultural event focused on gift-giving, fireworks, and community spirit. The theme is generosity — you're scored on the **quality and quantity** of gifts you give to NPCs during the event.

### How It Works

| Step | Description |
|:-----|:------------|
| 1. Prepare gifts | Craft or buy 5-10 high-quality gifts before the event |
| 2. Exchange phase | Walk around and give gifts to NPCs (10:00 AM — 2:00 PM) |
| 3. Reciprocation | NPCs give you tokens based on gift quality |
| 4. Prize booth | Spend tokens on exclusive Lunar New Year items |

### Best Gifts to Bring

| Item | Quality Needed | Token Value | Easy to Craft? |
|:-----|:--------------:|:-----------:|:--------------:|
| 💎 Gem | Any | 5 | ✅ Mine for them |
| 🧀 Cheese | Good+ | 4 | ✅ Mayo machine |
| 🍳 Cooked Dish | Excellent+ | 6 | ✅ Kitchen required |
| 🌱 Quality Crop | Perfect | 7 | ❌ Needs fertilizer |
| 🧵 Tailored Cloth | Good+ | 4 | ✅ Loom |
| 🧪 Rare Material | Any | 8 | ❌ Deep ruins only |

> **Tip:** **Gems** are the best gift-per-effort ratio. One mining run can net you 15+ gems. Each gem gives 5 tokens. 15 gems = 75 tokens — enough for the top-tier prize.

### Prizes

| Token Cost | Item | Notes |
|:----------:|:-----|:------|
| 20 | Firework Launcher Blueprint | Decorative, one-time craft |
| 35 | Lucky Red Envelope (500-2,000 Gols) | Random cash — RNG |
| 50 | Year of the ___ Decor Set | Rotates yearly |
| 75 | Lunar New Year Outfit (Hat + Coat) | Cosmetic only |
| 100 | Precious Firecracker (museum piece) | Needed for museum collection |

## 🔥 Summer Festival (Summer)

**Date:** Summer 20  
**Type:** Crafting & Workshop Showcase

The Summer Festival is Sandrock's premier workshop competition. You bring your best crafted item — furniture, machinery, or processed material — and it gets judged against entries from other builders in town. This is the festival that rewards your workshop progression most directly.

### Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | How to Maximize |
|:----------|:------:|:----------------|
| ⚙️ Crafting Quality | 40% | Higher skill level = higher base quality |
| 🧪 Material Rarity | 30% | Use rare ores (Manganese, Titanium) |
| 🛠️ Complexity | 20% | Multi-step items score higher |
| 🎨 Appearance | 10% | Decor items have bonus here |

### Best Items to Enter

| Item | Why It Wins | Materials Needed |
|:-----|:------------|:-----------------|
| 🪑 Hardwood Armchair | High complexity + good appearance | Hardwood×20, Cloth×8, Nails×10 |
| ⚔️ Refined Manganese Blade | Rare materials + combat utility | Manganese Bar×15, Rubber Wood×10 |
| 🔩 Precision Gear Set | Multi-step crafting chain | Steel Bar×10, Copper Wire×20, Springs×8 |
| 💎 Gem-Studded Vase | High appearance + gem bonus | Glass×5, Any Gem×3, Silver Frame |

**Winning Strategy:** Start preparing **one month before** the festival. Choose an item, gather the best quality materials you can find, and craft it when your skill level is highest. Eating a crafting buff meal before you craft the item improves its base quality.

> **Tip:** The **Hardwood Armchair** is the most reliable winner. It uses accessible materials, has good complexity, and you can boost its quality with furniture polish (craftable at the workbench).

### Prizes

| Place | Prize | Notes |
|:------|:------|:------|
| 🥇 1st | Master Craft Blueprint Set + 4,000 Gols | Unlocks 3 exclusive recipes |
| 🥈 2nd | Quality Material Crate + 2,000 Gols | Rare ores and parts |
| 🥉 3rd | Workshop Speed Boost + 1,000 Gols | 24-hour buff |
| Participation | Summer Token ×3 | Trade at booth |

## 🗓️ Year-Round Mini-Events

Besides the three major festivals, Sandrock has rotating mini-events that happen on random days:

| Event | Frequency | What Happens |
|:------|:---------:|:-------------|
| 🎵 Busking Performance | Monthly | Musician visits town square, +friendship for attendance |
| 📚 Book Club | Bi-weekly | Bring a book item, discuss with villagers |
| 🧺 Community Clean-Up | Monthly | Pick up trash around town for tokens |
| 🍳 Cooking Demo | Random | Grace demonstrates recipes at the saloon |
| 🧶 Craft Circle | Random | Villagers gather to craft, share patterns |

> **Tip:** Mini-events don't require much preparation. Just show up and participate. The friendship boosts from attending every mini-event add up to 2-3 extra hearts across all NPCs by the end of Year 1.
