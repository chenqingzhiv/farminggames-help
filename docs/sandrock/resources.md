## Gathering Efficiency Tips

1. **Upgrade your pickaxe and axe** as soon as possible — higher tiers unlock access to rarer ores and woods.
2. **Scavenge the Breach daily** for early-game scrap and data disks — it refreshes each in-game day.
3. **Plant Sandrice and Cactus** on your farm for a renewable source of plant materials.
4. **Save Titanium and Magnesium ores** for alloy production rather than selling raw — refined materials are worth significantly more.
5. **Check the train** that passes through Sandrock — sometimes it drops rare materials on the tracks.
6. **Commission board rewards** often include rare materials that are difficult to mine — prioritize these commissions.
7. **Geode processing** at the Grinder station yields gems and rare crafting components — always crack geodes open rather than selling them.
