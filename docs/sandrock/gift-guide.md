## Gift Giving Tips

- **Loved gifts** give the highest relationship boost — always check an NPC's Loved items before visiting.
- **Birthday gifts** provide a massive 3x multiplier on relationship points.
- **Item quality** matters — higher-quality versions of Loved items grant additional points.
- **Weekly limits** apply: you can give each NPC up to 2 gifts per week (1 for spouses).
- **Commission rewards** sometimes include exclusive Loved gifts for specific NPCs.
