# 🔧 My Time at Sandrock — Workshop Optimization Guide

Efficiency is everything in Sandrock. The difference between scraping by and dominating the workshop rankings lies in **production chain optimization, commission selection, and machine layout.** This guide walks you through every optimization lever from day 1 to endgame.

---

## 🏗️ Early Game Machine Layout

| Priority | Machine | Qty | Input → Output | Why |
|:--------:|:--------|:---:|:---------------|:----|
| 1 | Stone Furnace | 2 | 2 Copper Ore + 1 Wood → Copper Bar | First gol-maker |
| 2 | Stone Furnace | 1 | 1 Tin + 1 Copper Ore → Bronze Bar | Unlock advanced machines |
| 3 | Worktable | 1 | — | All intermediate crafting |
| 4 | Dew Collector | 3 | → 3 Water Bottles/day | Passive water income |

**Furnace ratio:** Maintain **3 furnaces per 1 grinder** for the first 30 days. Dedicate one to glass production — it's required for 40% of early commissions.

## 📦 Production Chain Profit Analysis

| Product | Materials Cost | Sell | Margin | Time | Gol/Hour |
|:--------|:-------------:|:----:|:-----:|:----:|:--------:|
| Copper Bar | 8g | 18g | +10g | 30min | 20 |
| Bronze Bar | 12g | 32g | +20g | 45min | 27 |
| **Glass** 🥇 | **6g** | **22g** | **+16g** | **20min** | **48** |
| Tempered Glass | 10g | 55g | +45g | 1.5hr | 30 |

**Best early margin:** Glass at 48 gol/hour beats everything. Keep 10 in stock for commissions. **Best mid-game:** Tempered Glass — manganese investment pays off fast.

## 📋 Commission Strategy

| Tier | Gol Range | Avg Time | Gol/Min | Priority |
|:-----|:---------:|:--------:|:-------:|:--------:|
| 💎 Premium (red) | 800-2000 | 5-10 min | 80-400 | Always accept |
| ⭐ High (blue) | 400-800 | 5-10 min | 40-160 | Next priority |
| 📋 Standard (white) | 100-400 | 3-5 min | 20-133 | Fill gaps |

**Rules:** Only accept same-day deliveries — missed deadlines cost reputation. Stockpile Copper Bar (×5), Glass (×10), Bronze Bar (×5). Skip premium Titanium/Opal commissions early-game. Board refreshes at **8 AM** and **3 PM**.

## 🏠 Workshop Expansion Priority

| Phase | Room | Cost | Benefit | When |
|:------|:-----|:----:|:--------|:-----|
| 1 | Assembly Room | 5000g + 20 Wood | +2 assembly stations | Week 2 |
| 2 | Furnace Room | 8000g + 30 Hardwood | +4 furnace slots | Week 3-4 |
| 3 | Grinder Ext. | 12000g + 10 Tempered Glass | +2 grinder slots | Month 2 |
| 4 | Automation Hub | 50000g + 20 Data Disks | Conveyor system | Month 4+ |

Do not expand until you have **10,000g liquid** — you need operating capital for seeds and fuel.

## 🤖 Automation Milestones

| Rank | Unlock | Impact |
|:----:|:-------|:-------|
| 5 | Conveyor Belts | Connect furnaces→grinders — eliminates 70% walking |
| 7 | Auto-feed Hoppers | Machines pull from chests automatically |
| 9 | Smart Sorters | Route copper to chest A, bronze to chest B |

**Manual flow:** Ore chest → furnace → bar chest → grinder → refined chest → assembly → output. With conveyors, zero movement time.

## 💧 Water Management

| Source | Daily Output | Cost | Best Use |
|:-------|:-----------:|:----:|:---------|
| Dew Collector (basic) | 3 bottles | Free | First 3 months |
| Dew Collector (upgraded) | 6 bottles | 10 Copper Bars | Month 2+ |
| Water Tower | Unlimited | 50g/5 bottles | Emergency only |

**Water efficiency:** Cactus (16 gol/water) → Aloe Vera (15) → Sandrice (12). Plant **Cactus** — half the water of Sandrice. Four upgraded Dew Collectors = 24 bottles/day, enough for 30 cactus tiles with surplus.

## 🏁 Endgame Max Gol/Hour

**Final setup:** 6 auto-fed Furnaces + 3 Grinders + 3 Assembly Stations + 12 Conveyor Belts + 4 Smart Sorters + 40 Cactus planters. Loop: Ore → furnace → bar → grinder → refined → assembly → output, zero movement.

**Max output:** ~16,400 gol/day passive + active (950 gol/hr from Tempered Glass + Titanium Alloy, 2000 gol/day from crops, 3000 gol/day from relics).
