## Top 5 Keg / Preserves Profit

| Crop | Base Price | Product | Product Price | Multiplier |
|:----|:--------|:------|:----|:----|
| Starfruit ⭐ | 750g | Starfruit Wine 🍷 | 2250g | 3x |
| Melon 🍈 | 250g | Melon Wine 🍷 | 750g | 3x |
| Pumpkin 🎃 | 320g | Pumpkin Juice 🧃 | 720g | 2.25x |
| Hops 🌿 | 25g | Pale Ale 🍺 | 300g | **12x** |
| Wheat 🌾 | 10g | Beer 🍺 | 200g | 20x |

> **Hops are the Keg efficiency king!** Though the base price is low, they're harvested daily, take 11 days to mature, then produce every day — a year-round Keg assembly line.
>
> **Note:** Fruits go into Kegs to make **Wine** (3x base price). Vegetables go into Kegs to make **Juice** (2.25x base price). Pumpkin is a vegetable, so it produces Pumpkin Juice, not wine. Blueberries (fruit) produce Blueberry Wine at 150g (50g × 3).
