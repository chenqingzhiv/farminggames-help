# 🐟 Fish Pond Guide — Complete Roe & Pond Products

> Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6.15 | All fish pond species with roe values, aged roe, pond products, and profit analysis

Fish ponds are a **passive income powerhouse** in Stardew Valley. Unlike crops or kegs that require daily attention, a well-stocked fish pond produces roe and special items automatically. This guide covers the **economics of every pond fish** so you know which ones pay off.

*Data sourced from Stardew Valley Wiki v1.6*

---

## 🏗️ Fish Pond Basics

| Item | Cost | Materials | Unlocks At |
|:-----|:----:|:---------|:-----------|
| Fish Pond | 5,000g | 200 Stone + 5 Seaweed + 5 Green Algae | Farming Level 3 |

### How Fish Ponds Work

1. **Population starts at 1 fish.** As it reproduces, population caps increase at 3, 5, 7, and 10 fish
2. Each population threshold may require a **quest item** (bring the fish a specific item)
3. Roe production scales with population — more fish = more roe per day
4. "Pond products" (non-roe items like pearls, diamonds, cindershards) become available at higher populations

### Emptying & Refilling

Fish ponds can be **emptied with a fishing rod** — you catch the fish back. This lets you swap species without demolishing the pond.

## 🧪 Caviar vs Aged Roe

Caviar is a separate product made from **Sturgeon Roe only**, processed in a **Preserves Jar** (not the regular Roe aging mechanism). It's worth comparing:

| Product | Base Price | Artisan Bonus | Processing Time |
|:--------|:---------:|:------------:|:--------------:|
| Aged Roe (Lava Eel) | 1,520g | **3,040g** | ~3 days |
| Aged Roe (Sturgeon) | 500g | **1,000g** | ~3 days |
| **Caviar** | 500g | **700g** | ~3 days |
| Aged Roe (Ice Pip) | 1,360g | **2,720g** | ~3 days |

**Caviar is not a profit leader.** It sells for less than Sturgeon Aged Roe under Artisan profession. Only make Caviar if:
1. You **don't** have the Artisan profession
2. You need Caviar for the **Community Center bundle** or a quest
3. You're role-playing a luxury food producer

> **Tip:** With Artisan, Sturgeon Aged Roe (1,000g) beats Caviar (700g). The Preserves Jar for Sturgeon fish always produces Aged Roe — Caviar only happens when you process **Sturgeon Roe itself** in a Jar (same as regular roe processing, but the output changes). To maximize profit, let the pond's natural Roe aging handle your Sturgeon. Only manually process Stugeon Roe through a Jar if you need Caviar for the Community Center.

## 💰 Pond Profit Strategy — How Many Ponds?

| Scenario | Recommended Ponds | Species | Est. Daily Profit |
|:---------|:----------------:|:--------|:-----------------:|
| Early Game (Yr 1) | 1-2 | Ghostfish, Super Cucumber | ~500-800g/day |
| Mid Game (Yr 1-2) | 3-5 | Lava Eel, Sturgeon, Blobfish | ~3,000-5,000g/day |
| Late Game (Yr 2+) | 5-8 | Lava Eel ×2, Ice Pip, Blobfish, Sturgeon | ~8,000-12,000g/day |
| Massive (Endgame) | 10-12 | Mix Lava Eel, Sturgeon, Blobfish, Rainbow Trout | ~15,000g+/day |

> **Tip:** Rainbow Trout ponds are the only renewable source of Prismatic Shards in the game (outside Skull Cavern farming). If you're aiming for 100% completion, keep **one** Rainbow Trout pond running from Year 2 onward. At 0.08% per day with 10 fish, you'll average one shard every ~3 in-game years — not reliable income, but it's free RNG.

## ⚡ Quick Comparison: Pond vs Keg

| Metric | Lava Eel Pond (10 fish) | Starfruit Keg (10 kegs) |
|:-------|:----------------------:|:----------------------:|
| Setup cost | 5,000g + 200 Stone | 22,000g (kegs) + 4,000g (seeds) + Oak Resin |
| Daily profit | ~900g (aged roe) | ~342g/keg = 3,420g |
| Daily labor | Check once, empty jars | Harvest starfruit, Keg + Jars |
| Requires | One-time quest items | Weekly crop planting |
| **Per-tile income** | **~150g/day** (5×5 pond) | **~57g/day** (tilled + keg) |

> **Conclusion:** Ponds are lower-maintenance than kegs but the kegs win on pure profit density. Build ponds **first** for passive income, then scale kegs when you have the resources. The ideal split is **4-6 ponds + 80-120 kegs** for a balanced mid-game setup.
