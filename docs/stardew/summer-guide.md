## ⚡ Energy & Health Management

Summer's long workdays require careful energy management:
- **Field Snacks** (1 Acorn + 1 Maple Seed + 1 Pine Cone) — craft these in bulk
- **Salads** from the Saloon (220g) — best early energy-to-gold ratio
- **Parsley Soup** — easy forage-based recipe

## ⛏️ Summer Mining Strategy

Use summer to stockpile for Fall:
- Focus on reaching floor 80+ in the mines (gold ore)
- Collect all 4 dwarf scrolls for the Dwarf Shop
- Build quality sprinklers before Fall (need gold bars)
- Stockpile quartz for quality refining

## 🔄 Summer-to-Fall Transition Checklist

- [ ] Build quality sprinklers (gold bars from mines)
- [ ] Stockpile 100+ blueberries in preserves jars/kegs
- [ ] Reach floor 80+ in the mines
- [ ] Save summer forageables for bundles
- [ ] Plant corn on Summer 28 (it grows through Fall)
- [ ] Prepare cranberry budget for Fall 1 planting
