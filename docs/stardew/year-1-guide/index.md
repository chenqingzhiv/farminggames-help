## 🌷 Spring Year 1

### Week 1: Getting Started

| Day | Morning (6-12) | Afternoon (12-6) | Evening (6-2am) |
|:----|:--------------|:-----------------|:----------------|
| **1** | Clear 15 tiles near house. Plant 15 parsnips (free). Water them. | Explore town. Talk to 2-3 NPCs. | Fishing at mountain lake. |
| **2** | Water parsnips. Check TV: if "Luck" is good, head to mines. | Visit beach — collect shells and coral. | Fishing at ocean pier. |
| **3** | Harvest 15 parsnips (~390g). Buy 20 kale seeds. Plant+water. | Clear more farmland. Chop trees for wood. | Fishing at river (below Leah's). |
| **4** | Water kale. Visit Mountain — mine floor 1-5 for copper ore. | Sell what you found. Upgrade backpack at Pierre's. | Fish at mountain lake. |
| **5** | Water kale. Continue mining — focus on copper. | Give gifts: talk to everyone you see. | Check trash cans in town. |

**💰 Profit Tip:** Fishing is your best moneymaker in week 1. A good day of fishing can earn 500-1000g.

### Week 2: Scaling Up

| Day | Key Actions |
|:----|:------------|
| **6** | Mine floors 5-10. Unlock elevator. Collect copper ore. |
| **7** | Water kale. Chop trees at Cindersap Forest. Collect spring onions. |
| **8** | Buy fiberglass rod from Willy (1,800g) — unlocks better fishing. |
| **9** | Fish at mountain lake for Largemouth Bass (great money). |
| **10** | Run to the beach on a lucky day — fish for catfish. |

### 🍓 Egg Festival (Day 13)

This is the **most important day** of Spring:

1. **Bring all your gold** — you should have **2,000-4,000g** saved
2. Buy **strawberry seeds** (~100g each, best value crop!)
3. Plant strawberries immediately — they regrow every 4 days
4. You'll get 2 harvests: Day 16 and Day 20

**Strawberry Profit:** 120g per seed → 2 harvests × 50g = 240g gross - 100g cost = **140g profit per plant**

### Weeks 3-4: Finishing Spring

| Day | Focus |
|:----|:------|
| **14-15** | Strawberries growing. Mine for iron ore (floors 21-30). |
| **16** | First strawberry harvest! Plant more if you have seeds. |
| **17-19** | Fish for gold. Save at least 5k for Summer seeds. |
| **20** | Second strawberry harvest. Total spring profit should be ~10k-15k. |
| **21-24** | Upgrade tools at Clint's. Focus on copper/iron. |
| **25-28** | Clear land for Summer crops. Save 7-10k for blueberry seeds. |

**✅ Spring Goals Checklist:**
- [ ] Community Center: Spring Foraging Bundle (complete!)
- [ ] Community Center: Spring Crops Bundle (save 1 of each)
- [ ] Fishing level 3-5
- [ ] Mine floor 30+
- [ ] At least 8,000g saved for Summer

## ❄️ Winter Preparation

Winter has no crops. Use this time to:

### Week 1-2: Infrastructure
- [ ] **Forage all winter roots** — great for Energy
- [ ] **Upgrade tools** — gold axe/pickaxe/hoe
- [ ] **Build barn + coop** — start animal income
- [ ] **Build quality sprinklers** — aim for 20+ for Spring Year 2
- [ ] **Build tappers** — on oak trees (for Kegs)

### Week 3-4: Exploration
- [ ] **Complete the mines** to floor 120
- [ ] **Visit the Secret Woods** (upgrade axe)
- [ ] **Gift NPCs** — build relationships for recipes
- [ ] **Fishing** — Winter fish are valuable (Sturgeon, Glacierfish)
- [ ] **Plan Year 2** — save 20k for Spring seeds
