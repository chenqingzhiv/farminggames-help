## 🏛️ Stardew Valley Fair — 1000 Star Tokens Strategy

The **Summer 16** Stardew Valley Fair is your chance to get rare items. Here's how to get 1000 tokens easily:

**Best Approach:** Fishing mini-game
- Takes ~5 minutes at the fishing booth
- Easy to score 100+ tokens per attempt with practice
- No need to save items all year

**Alternative (Item-Based):**
Bring high-quality items for the grange display:
- 9 Items × iridium quality + varied categories = max points
- Diamond, Wine, Gold Bar, Iridium-quality crops

**Best Purchase:** **Stardrop** (2,000 tokens) — permanent energy boost. Save your tokens for this.
