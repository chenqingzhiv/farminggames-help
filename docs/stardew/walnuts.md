## 🦜 Parrot Hints (10 Walnuts)

After unlocking the **Parrot Hints** system (talk to the parrot in Leo's Hut), you can **pay 1 walnut** to receive a hint about the next walnut location. The total cost for all hints is 10 walnuts.

> **Tip:** Don't use hints early — explore freely and save hints for the last 20–30 walnuts when you're stuck.

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use a Burglar Ring** when farming Tiger Slimes to maximize drop chances
2. **Fishing Bubbles** significantly increase walnut catch rate — always fish where you see bubbles
3. **Rain totems** work on the island — use one for the Mermaid Puzzle instead of waiting for rain
4. **Journal Scraps** (Buried in the island sand) provide clues — read them as you find them
5. **Crab Pots** are the slowest method — set them early and check daily
6. **Plant lots of crops** on the island farm — every harvest has a small chance to dig up a walnut
7. **Last 10 walnuts** are always the hardest — use Parrot Hints when you're stuck
