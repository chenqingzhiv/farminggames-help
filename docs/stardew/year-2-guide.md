# 📅 Stardew Valley — Year 2+ Advanced Guide

> Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6.15 | Covers post-Community Center progression, Ginger Island, Perfection, and endgame strategies

You made it through Year 1. The Community Center is done (or you went the Joja route — no judgment), your farm is producing, you've got a coop and a barn, and you've kissed at least one villager. So what now? Year 2 is where Stardew Valley opens up in a big way. This guide covers everything you need to know.

---

## 🎉 Congratulations — You Finished Year 1. Now What?

Year 1 is the tutorial. Year 2 is where the **real game** starts. Here's what changes:

| Aspect | Year 1 | Year 2+ |
|:-------|:-------|:--------|
| **Grandpa's Evaluation** | Not available | Day 1 of Year 3 — affects everything |
| **Ginger Island** | Unlocked after CC | Main content zone |
| **Crops** | Basic seasonal rotation | Ancient Fruit, Starfruit, year-round |
| **Money** | 5,000–50,000g season | 100,000g+/day is normal |
| **Skills** | Lv 5–8 typical | Lv 10 Mastery levels |
| **Relationships** | 2–4 hearts with most | 10+ hearts, marriage, kids |

### Your Year 2 Goals (in order)

```
1. Grandpa's Preparation — Get ready for the Day 1 Year 3 evaluation
2. Ginger Island Access — Boat is repaired, go yesterday
3. Farm Layout Overhaul — Sprinklers + Junimo Huts for automation
4. Endgame Money Machine — Ancient Fruit Wine or Truffle Oil
5. Perfection Progress — Start tracking the 7 categories
6. Mastery Levels — Unlock the new 1.6 endgame skill system
```

> **Tip:** Don't feel pressured to rush. Stardew Valley has no time limit (despite Grandpa's deadline). Year 2 is meant to be enjoyed at your pace. That said, planning for Grandpa's evaluation early saves stress later.

## 🌿 Community Center Completion — Now What?

Whether you completed the bundles or bought Joja membership, the reward is the same: **Ginger Island access** and the **Movie Theater**.

### Community Center Rewards

| Unlock | Effect | How to Access |
|:-------|:-------|:---------------|
| **Ginger Island Boat** | Travel to tropical endgame zone | Talk to Willy at the beach |
| **Movie Theater** | New social activity + relationship boost | Enter abandoned JojaMart |
| **Greenhouse** | Year-round farming (already from CC) | Your farm |
| **Bus Repair** | Desert access for Starfruit/upgrades | Already from CC |
| **Minecarts** | Fast travel across map | Already from CC |
| **Glittering Boulder** | Remove by completing Fishing Bundle | Mountain Lake |

### Post-CC Priority List

1. **Repair Willy's boat** (requires 200 hardwood, 5 iridium bars, 5 battery packs)
2. **Set up Greenhouse for Ancient Fruit** — Turn those ancient seeds into a full greenhouse
3. **Unlock Ginger Island** — This is your main Year 2 playground
4. **Visit the Movie Theater** — Great for boosting hard-to-please NPC hearts
5. **Upgrade your house** — Kitchen for cooking, cellar for casks

> **Tip:** If you picked the **Artisan** profession at Farming Lv 10, your Greenhouse Ancient Fruit wine sells for 2,310g per bottle. A full greenhouse (120 tiles) produces 120 Ancient Fruit every 7 days, which turns into 120 Ancient Fruit Wine = **277,200g/week** with Artisan.

## 🏝️ Ginger Island Focus

Ginger Island is the single biggest content drop in the game. Year 2 is when you should dig into it properly.

### What to Do on Ginger Island (Year 2)

| Priority | Task | Why |
|:---------|:-----|:-----|
| 1 | **Plant Ancient Fruit** on Island Farm | Year-round growing, never replant |
| 2 | **Build Island Keg Shed** | Process fruit on-island |
| 3 | **Find Golden Walnuts** | Unlock all island areas (130 total) |
| 4 | **Clear Volcano Dungeon** | Forge upgrades + Qi Gems |
| 5 | **Qi Challenges** | Endgame rewards + perfection items |

### Island Farm Layout Strategy

The Island Farm has 788 arable tiles. A proper layout:

```
Central area: Ancient Fruit × 120 (with Deluxe Retaining Soil)
Left section: Hops × 80 + Kegs × 40
Right section: Starfruit × 60 (hyper speed-gro)
Pathways: Stone paths + Torches (prevent debris spawn)
Storage: 1 Shed with 100+ Kegs
```

**Deluxe Retaining Soil** is the game-changer — it replaces sprinklers on Ginger Island so you don't have to worry about watering. Craft it once, plant Ancient Fruit, and harvest forever.

> **Tip:** The 1.6 update added **Fiber Seeds** (craftable from mixed seeds + sap). Plant these in off-season tiles on the Island Farm to prevent debris from spawning. They grow in 7 days and you can just leave them.

## 👴 Grandpa's Evaluation — How to Get 4 Candles

On Day 1 of Year 3, Grandpa's ghost visits and evaluates your progress. You get **1–4 candles** lit on his shrine.

### Evaluation Criteria

| Points | Requirement | Notes |
|:------:|:------------|:------|
| 2 | 100,000g total earnings | This is cumulative, not current cash |
| 1 | 30+ skill levels (total across all 5 skills) | Each skill maxes at 10, so you need total 30+ |
| 1 | Unlocked Community Center or Joja Warehouse | Both count |
| 1 | 8+ hearts with 5+ villagers | Easy if you gift regularly |
| 1 | 5+ hearts with your pet | Pet them daily |
| 1 | 5+ skill masteries | Any combination |
| 1 | Own a Rusty Key | Earned from donating 60+ museum items |
| 1 | Completed 30+ museum donations | |
| 1 | Caught 25+ fish | |

### 4-Candle Guarantee Strategy

To guarantee 4 candles with no stress:

```
1. Make 100,000g (easy — just sell crops all Year 2)
2. Get all 5 skills to level 6+ (passive play, just do activities)
3. Befriend 5 villagers (gift on birthdays + loved gifts)
4. Pet your dog/cat every single day
5. Complete the Community Center
6. Donate at least 30 items to the museum
```

> **Tip:** You can get re-evaluated at any time by placing a **Diamond** in Grandpa's Shrine. So if you miss the 4 candles on Day 1, just fix whatever you missed and try again. The shrine is behind your house.

### 4-Candle Reward

| Candle Count | Reward |
|:------------:|:--------|
| 1 | None (try again) |
| 2 | Statue of Grandpa (produces iridium ore daily) |
| 3 | Statue of Grandpa (yields more iridium) |
| **4** | **Statue of Perfection** — produces 2–8 Iridium Ore EVERY DAY |

The **Statue of Perfection** is the best reward in the game by far. It gives you a steady iridium supply without ever touching the Skull Cavern again. That iridium funds your Sprinkler upgrades, tool upgrades, and endgame gear.

## 🏆 Perfection Tracker

The **Perfection Tracker** was added in 1.5 and expanded in 1.6. It tracks your progress toward 100% completion.

### How to Check

Visit **Qi's Walnut Room** on Ginger Island (requires 100 Golden Walnuts). The tracker shows:

| Category | Requirement | Notes |
|:---------|:------------|:-------|
| **Shipping** | Ship 1 of every item (full list) | 1.6 added new items |
| **Cooking** | Cook every recipe | 1.6 added new recipes |
| **Crafting** | Craft every recipe | 1.6 added new recipes |
| **Fishing** | Catch every fish | Includes Legendary Fish |
| **Artifacts** | Donate all artifacts to museum | 1.6 artifact list updated |
| **Minerals** | Donate all minerals to museum | |
| **Stardrops** | Collect all 7 stardrops | |
| **Golden Walnuts** | 130 total | |
| **Achievements** | Complete every achievement | 1.6 added new ones |
| **Upgrades** | Max house, all tools iridium | |

> **Tip:** The Perfection Tracker doesn't require 100% of everything — some items are optional. Qi's Walnut Room shows exact counts for each category. **Perfection is mid-to-late Year 2 minimum, usually Year 3+.**

## 💰 Endgame Money Strategies

### Strategy 1: Ancient Fruit Wine (Best Overall)

| Setup | Cost | Weekly Profit |
|:------|:-----|:-------------|
| Greenhouse (120 tiles) + Artisan | Ancient Seeds from seed maker | 277,200g/week |
| Island (120 tiles) + Artisan | Ancient Seeds from seed maker | 277,200g/week |
| Combined (Greenhouse + Island) | ~50 Ancient Seeds to start | **554,400g/week** |

**Setup time:** Takes 2–3 seasons to scale from 1 Ancient Seed to full production. Use Seed Makers on every harvest until you reach capacity.

### Strategy 2: Truffle Oil (Passive, Less Setup)

| Setup | Cost | Daily Profit |
|:------|:-----|:-------------|
| 12 Pigs + Gatherer/Artisan | 19,200g | 8,700g/day |
| Barn Deluxe + Auto-Grabber | 25,000g | Fully passive |
| **Year-round with Ginger Island** | Pig barn on island | Same rate year-round |

**Setup time:** 2 weeks to buy pigs, 2 weeks for them to mature.

### Strategy 3: Starfruit Wine (Highest Raw Profit)

| Setup | Cost | Notes |
|:------|:-----|:-------|
| Greenhouse Starfruit | 400g/seed | Replant every 13 days |
| Island Starfruit | 400g/seed | Use Hyper Speed-Gro |
| Wine price (Artisan) | **3,150g/bottle** | Highest per-bottle value |

**Downside:** Costly seeds (400g each) and must replant. Best combined with Seed Maker to reduce seed costs.

### Strategy 4: Fairy Rose Honey (New 1.6)

| Setup | Setup Cost | Daily Profit |
|:------|:-----------|:-------------|
| 1 Bee House + Fairy Rose | 2,500g + 400g seeds | 952g/hive |
| 20 Bee Houses on Island | 50,000g | 19,040g/day |

**1.6 change:** Fairy Rose Honey now sells for 952g base. With Artisan profession, that's **1,190g per honey**. A field of 100 Bee Houses surrounded by Fairy Roses makes 119,000g every 4 days with almost zero daily work.

> **Tip:** The **best endgame setup** combines all strategies: Greenhouse Ancient Fruit Wine (steady), Island Starfruit Wine (peak profit), and a Deluxe Barn of Pigs on Ginger Island (passive daily income). You'll clear 1 million gold per season without breaking a sweat.

## 🌟 Mastery Levels (1.6 Feature)

Version 1.6 added **Mastery** — when you hit Lv 10 in all 5 skills, you start earning Mastery Points. Each Mastery Level unlocks powerful new abilities.

| Mastery | Unlock | Effect |
|:-------:|:-------|:-------|
| **Farming** | Statue of Blessings | Daily random blessing |
| **Mining** | Statue of Dwarven Kings | Daily ore/geode |
| **Foraging** | Mystic Seeds | Grow mystic tree |
| **Fishing** | Skill Books | Unlock special tackles |
| **Combat** | Trinket Forge | Craft trinkets |

### Recommended Mastery Order

```
1. Farming → Statue of Blessings gives daily random buffs (best QoL)
2. Combat → Trinket Forge (trinkets are 1.6 endgame gear)
3. Mining → Free geodes and ore daily
4. Fishing → Skill books for XP bonuses
5. Foraging → Mystic seeds are nice but lowest priority
```

**Trinkets** are the big 1.6 endgame addition. Gear items like the Fairy Box (auto-heal), Magic Quiver (ranged attack), or Parrot Egg (pet parrot). These go in your trinket slot and provide permanent bonuses.
