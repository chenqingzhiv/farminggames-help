# 🎃 Pumpkin vs Cranberries: Fall's Heavyweight Showdown

Fall in Stardew Valley presents farmers with two titan crops: **Pumpkins** and **Cranberries**. Every new player faces this choice — and the answer depends entirely on your playstyle and goals.

This guide breaks down **every factor** so you can make the right call for your farm.

---

## 📊 Quick Stats Comparison

| Stat | 🎃 Pumpkin | 🍒 Cranberry |
|:----|:-----------|:-------------|
| **Seed Cost** | 100g | 240g |
| **Growing Time** | 13 days | 7 days → regrows every 2 days |
| **Harvests per Season** | 1 | 5 |
| **Base Sell Price** | 320g | 75g |
| **Energy (raw)** | +132 | +38 |
| **HP (raw)** | +59 | +17 |
| **Keg Output** | Juice (315g) / Pale Ale | Wine (225g) |
| **Preserves Jar** | Pickles (490g) | Jelly (290g) |
| **Quality chance** | Higher base value = bigger star impact | Volume over value |

**Raw profit verdict:** Cranberries win on **total seasonal gold** from volume. Pumpkins win **per-plant gold and per-slot efficiency** for processing.

## 💰 Profit Analysis

### 🎃 Pumpkin: Raw Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|:-------|:------|
| Seeds | 100g |
| Sell price (no quality) | 320g |
| **Profit per plant** | **220g** |
| Profit per day | 220g ÷ 13 = **16.9g/day** |
| 100 plants (raw) | 220g × 100 = **22,000g** |
| Pickled (Preserves Jar) | 490g → 390g profit = **30g/day** |
| Juice (Keg) | 315g → 215g profit = **16.5g/day** |

**Best if:** you have Preserves Jars ready. Pickled Pumpkins are among the best processing crops in the game.

### 🍒 Cranberry: Raw Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|:-------|:------|
| Seeds | 240g |
| Sell price (no quality, first harvest) | 75g |
| **Profit per first harvest** | 75g - 240g = **-165g** (loss!) |
| Subsequent harvests (4× 75g) | 300g |
| **Total profit per plant** | **-165 + 300 = 135g** |
| Profit per day | 135g ÷ 28 = **4.8g/day** |
| 100 plants (raw, all season) | 135g × 100 = **13,500g** |
| Wine (Keg) | 225g → net per harvest: 225g - 0g (no more seeds) = **900g over season** |
| Jelly (Preserves) | 290g → **1,160g over season** |

> **⚠️ Important:** Cranberries take 7 days to mature, meaning **2 harvests in years 1–2** if planted on Fall 1, or **3 harvests** if the season starts with Speed-Gro.

## 🏆 The Verdict: Which Should You Pick?

### Pick 🎃 **Pumpkin** if:
- ✅ You have **Preserves Jars** ready (pickled pumpkin = 490g)
- ✅ You want **one-and-done** — plant once, harvest once, process all
- ✅ You're going for **Giant Crops** (3×3 pumpkins can merge into giant harvests)
- ✅ You need **high single-item value** for the **Fall Fair** grange display
- ✅ You have **Deluxe Speed-Gro** (reduces 13→10 days)

### Pick 🍒 **Cranberry** if:
- ✅ You want **daily income** — harvest every 2 days, steady cash flow
- ✅ You're **early game year 1** with limited processing
- ✅ You want to **fill Kegs** steadily (cranberry wine sells well)
- ✅ You have **Seed Maker** (1 cranberry = 1-4 seeds, self-sustaining after first batch)
- ✅ You need **consistent energy** for mining — cranberries are cheap to eat

## ⚡ Speed-Gro Impact

| Crop | Base | Speed-Gro (10%) | Deluxe Speed-Gro (25%) | Hyper Speed-Gro (33%) |
|:----|:----|:----------------|:-----------------------|:----------------------|
| 🎃 Pumpkin | 13 days | 11 days | 10 days | 9 days |
| 🍒 Cranberry | 7→2 regrow | 6→2 regrow | 5→2 regrow | 5→2 regrow |

With **Deluxe Speed-Gro**, pumpkins mature in 10 days = 220g/day, cranberries get 1 extra harvest = 160g total/plant.

**Best combo:** Deluxe Speed-Gro + Pumpkin = **dominant choice** for processing.

## 🥇 Giant Crops — The Pumpkin Exclusive

Pumpkins are one of only **3 crops** (cauliflower, melon, pumpkin) that can grow into **Giant Crops**:

- **How:** Plant in 3×3 grids. Each day, any mature 3×3 has a ~1% chance to merge
- **Harvest:** 15–21 pumpkins per giant (not 9!)
- **Aesthetics:** Giant crops don't die on season change — they persist as farm decorations

Cranberries have no giant crop mechanic.

## 🧪 Processing Comparison

| Process | Pumpkin | Cranberry |
|:--------|:--------|:----------|
| **Raw sell** | 320g (1 harvest) | 375g (5 harvests) |
| **Keg** | 315g wine (1×) | 225g × 5 = 1,125g |
| **Preserves Jar** | 490g pickles (2d) | 290g × 5 = 1,450g jelly (2d each) |
| **Seed Maker** | 220g→2 seeds avg (ok) | 75g→2 seeds avg (excellent for self-sustain) |

**Processing winner:** Cranberries if you have **many machines**. Pumpkins if **machines are limited**.

## 🧾 Season Strategy: Mix Both!

The optimal Fall strategy uses both:

| Week | Action |
|:----|:-------|
| **Fall 1** | Plant 50-75% of field with **Speed-Gro'd pumpkins**, rest with cranberries |
| **Fall 6-7** | First cranberry harvest → sell raw or preserve |
| **Fall 13** | Pumpkin harvest → **process everything in preserves jars** |
| **Fall 14+** | Cranberries every 2 days → steady wine/jelly production |
| **Fall 26-28** | Final cranberry harvest → process or sell raw |

> 📊 **Sample 100-tile farm:** 60 pumpkins (pickled = 29,400g) + 40 cranberries (jellied = 14,400g) = **43,800g total** with average quality.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I get pumpkins from the Seed Maker?**  
A: Yes! Place any crop in a Seed Maker for 1-4 seeds. Cranberries have better ROI on Seed Making since you recoup seeds faster.

**Q: Do cranberries regrow after season end?**  
A: No. All crops die on Fall 28 regardless of growth stage. Plan accordingly.

**Q: Is pumpkin juice or pickles better?**  
A: Pickles (490g) beats Juice (315g) significantly. Always preserve pumpkins.

**Q: How many cranberry plants for steady Keg production?**  
A: Each Keg produces wine every 7 days. 35 cranberry plants → 5 harvests of 35 = 175 berries → 175/7 days = 25 Kegs run continuously.

| Goal | Best Crop |
