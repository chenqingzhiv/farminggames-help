# ❄️ Complete Winter Guide

Winter is the most unique season in Stardew Valley — you can't plant any crops outdoors (except in the greenhouse). But winter is actually the **busiest season**, because it's the time for:

- ⛏️ **Rushing through the mines**
- 🎣 **Catching winter-exclusive fish**
- 🌲 **Foraging winter wild berries + gathering**
- 💰 **Planning farm layout + upgrading tools**
- 🎄 **Participating in winter festivals**

---

## Winter Core Money-Making Strategy

| Income Method | Expected Income | Notes |
|:--------------|:----------------|:------|
| Greenhouse Crops | 5000-20000/day | Blueberries/Cranberries/Ancient Fruit |
| Mine Descending | 3000-8000/day | Gems + Ore + Geodes |
| Winter Root Foraging | 500-1500/day | Winter Roots sell for 200-400g |
| Fishing (Limited) | 1000-3000/day | Glacierfish has high value |
| Artisan Processing | 2000-8000/day | Animal products + Fruit wine |

**Winter's #1 money maker: The Greenhouse.** If you unlocked the greenhouse in fall, rely on greenhouse crops for winter income. If you haven't unlocked it yet → Rush through the mines!

## Greenhouse Planting Recommendations

You can't plant outdoors in winter, so greenhouse output is the economic lifeline:

| Tier | Planting Plan | Daily Income |
|:-----|:--------------|:-------------|
| 🌱 Early | Blueberries×60 + Hops×30 | 3000g/day |
| 🌿 Mid | Cranberries×40 + Blueberries×60 | 5000g/day |
| 🌳 Late | Ancient Fruit×120 | 20000g/day |

**What to prepare before spring:** In the last week of winter, stock up on spring seeds (Parsnips/Strawberries/etc.) fertilizer and upgrade your watering can, so you can plant first thing on Spring 1.

## ❓ Winter FAQ

**Q: What to do when there's nothing to do in winter?**  
A: Go to the mines! Winter is the best time to rush through the mines. Reaching floor 120 unlocks the elevator for permanent benefits.

**Q: What to bring when mining in winter?**  
A: Food (Salads/Bread), weapon, pickaxe. Bring plenty of bombs for faster progress.

**Q: What should I prioritize in my first winter?**  
A: ① Unlock the greenhouse (if you haven't) ② Upgrade tools ③ Rush the mines to floor 80+
