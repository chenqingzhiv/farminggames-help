## How to Redeem Codes in Palia

1. Launch Palia and enter the game world
2. Press **ESC** to open the main menu
3. Click the **"Redeem Code"** button (or navigate to Settings → Redeem Code)
4. Enter the code exactly as shown (codes are **case-sensitive**)
5. Click **Redeem** — rewards will appear in your inventory

> 💡 **Tip:** Codes usually expire within **7–14 days**, so redeem them as soon as you see them!

## ℹ️ About Redeem Codes

Palia is a **free-to-play** MMO, and its primary monetization is through the in-game shop. Redeem codes offer a way to receive:

- **Cosmetics** — Outfits, gliders, accessories
- **Decor** — Furniture and housing items
- **Gold & Currency** — In-game gold and premium currency
- **Consumables** — Boosters and utility items

Codes are typically released to celebrate milestones, holidays, or community events.
