# 🏹 Palia Hunting & Bug Catching Complete Guide

Hunting and Bug Catching are the core skills for obtaining leather, meat, insect materials, and gold in Palia. This guide covers everything from unlocking your first bow to the best gold-farming routes.

---

## 🎯 Hunting Unlock

Complete the main quest **Learning the Ropes** (given by Hassian) to receive your first **Makeshift Bow** and basic arrows. Afterward, talk to Hassian to buy advanced bow and arrow recipes.

| Item | Details |
|:-----|:--------|
| Unlock Condition | Complete the Learning the Ropes quest |
| Guild Representative | Hassian |
| Currency | Hunting Medals |
| Skill Cap | Lv 10 (further levels give achievement rewards) |

## 🏹 Bow Upgrade Path

| Bow | Level Required | Materials | Notes |
|:----|:--------------:|:----------|:------|
| Makeshift Bow | Lv 1 | Starting gift | Short range, low damage |
| Standard Bow | Lv 3 | 20 Sapwood Plank + 5 Copper Bar + 200g | Improved range |
| Fine Bow | Lv 6 | 10 Heartwood Plank + 5 Iron Bar + 1,500g | Longer range, better accuracy |
| Exquisite Bow | Lv 9 | 5 Flow-Infused Plank + 5 Palium Bar + 3,000g | Best range, fastest draw |

### Arrow Types

| Arrow | Effect | Best For |
|:------|:-------|:---------|
| Standard Arrow | Base damage | Normal Sernuk / Spotted Chapaa |
| Fine Arrow | High damage | Mid-tier creatures and above |
| Slowdown Arrow | Slows target | Azure Chapaa / Bluebristle Muujin |
| Dispel Arrow | Cancels magic effects | Proudhorn / Azure / Bluebristle |

> **Tip:** Carry both Fine Arrows and Dispel Arrows in your inventory and switch with right-click. Against magic creatures, hit them with one Dispel arrow first, then swap to Fine arrows for damage.

## 🐞 Bug Catching Complete Guide

### Smoke Bomb Types

| Type | Unlock Level | Effect |
|:-----|:------------:|:-------|
| **Standard Smoke Bomb** | Lv 1 | Basic catch, scares nearby bugs |
| **Sneaky Smoke Bomb** | Lv 3 | **Doesn't scare** nearby bugs — great for dense areas |
| **Buzzy Jar** | Lv 6 | Shows nearby rare/epic bug locations on the minimap |
| **Honey Lure** | Lv 8 | Spawns a swarm of bugs (including rares) at the landing site |
| **Smoke Candle** | Consumable | Approach bugs without triggering escape while sneaking |

### Best Bug-Catching Routes

1. **Kilima Night Route**: Fountain → Library Garden → Farm → Riverside Grass (night moths, fireflies)
2. **Bahari Day Route**: Pulsewater Plains → Seaside → Waterfall Area (rare butterflies, jewel beetles)
3. **Honey Lure Method**: Pick an open spot in Bahari → use Honey Lure → chain-catch with Sneaky Bombs

> **Tip:** Set your game's Foliage graphics setting to Low — ground-crawling bugs become much easier to spot. Buzzy Jar + Smoke Candle is the golden combo for catching epic bugs.

## 📅 Weekly Want Keep List

NPC Weekly Wants may ask for the following hunting/bug items. **Keep these instead of selling:**

- **Sernuk Meat / Chapaa Meat** — Cooking ingredient, NPCs often want cooked meat
- **Any starred bugs** — Multiple NPCs love rare bugs (Elouisa, Auni)
- **Magic Leather** — Crafting high-end gear and furniture
- **Quality Leather / Thick Hide** — Essential for equipment upgrades
- **Raw Meat** — Keep a stock of 30+

> **Tip:** When the Weekly Wants refresh on Monday, check if any hunting-related items are requested. Prioritize completing NPC tasks before selling excess materials.
