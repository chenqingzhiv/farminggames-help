# 🗺️ Palia Maps & Zones Guide

Palia's world spans two main zones — Kilima Valley and Bahari Bay — connected by a network of trails, stable fast-travel routes, and vertical shortcuts using your glider. This guide covers every zone, key landmark, navigation trick, and how to unlock areas as you progress through the game.

> **Tip:** Unlike traditional open-world MMOs, Palia's maps are compact and handcrafted. Everything is within a few minutes' walk once you know the routes. The key is learning the vertical shortcuts.

---

## 🌍 Map Overview

Palia currently features two main explorable zones, with hints at future expansions. Each zone has its own distinct biome, resources, and activities.

| Zone | Approx. Size | Unlock Requirement | Key Features |
|:-----|:------------:|:------------------|:-------------|
| **Kilima Valley** | ~0.5 km² | Starter zone (immediately available) | Town, farm, NPC homes, central lake, beginner resources |
| **Bahari Bay** | ~1.0 km² | Complete **"The Void"** quest (Early Main Story) | Mining caves, high-tier resources, epic fishing, Proudhorn Forest |
| **???** | TBA | Not yet released | Teased as a third zone in development |

**Connecting the zones:** A single overland route runs between Kilima Valley and Bahari Bay through a canyon pass near the southeast corner of Kilima. The stable system also connects the two.

## 🌊 Bahari Bay — The Mid-Game Zone

Bahari Bay is larger, more dangerous, and packed with high-value resources. You unlock it by progressing the main story quest **"The Void"** — roughly 8-12 hours into the game depending on your pace.

### How to Unlock Bahari Bay

1. Complete the main quest **"The Dragon's Legacy"** (learn about the Phoenix)
2. Complete **"The Void"** quest — Jina and Hekla will guide you to the Bahari Bay pass
3. Walk through the canyon path (southeast of Kilima) or take the stable from Kilima Town

> **Tip:** Don't rush to Bahari Bay before you have at least a Fine Bow and an Iron Pickaxe. The resources are tougher, the animals hit harder, and you'll struggle without upgrades.

## 🏃 Fast Travel System

Palia's fast travel uses **Stable Signs** — wooden signposts with horse icons found throughout both zones. You unlock the ability to use them early in the main quest.

### How Stables Work

1. Walk up to any Stable Sign and interact with it
2. A menu shows available destinations with gold costs
3. Pay the fee to instantly teleport to the chosen sign
4. The cost increases with distance

### Fast Travel Cost Table

| Route | Cost | Travel Time (walking) |
|:------|:----:|:--------------------:|
| Kilima Town ↔ Library | 50g | ~2 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Badruu's Farm | 50g | ~1.5 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Remembrance Beach | 50g | ~3 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Bahari Pass | 80g | ~4 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Pulsewater Plains | 150g | ~8 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Pavel Mines | 150g | ~10 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Bahari Coast | 150g | ~9 min |
| Kilima Town ↔ Hideaway Bluffs | 150g | ~11 min |
| Pulsewater Plains ↔ Pavel Mines | 80g | ~3 min |
| Pulsewater Plains ↔ Bahari Coast | 80g | ~2.5 min |
| Pulsewater Plains ↔ Hideaway Bluffs | 100g | ~3.5 min |

> **Tip:** Fast travel costs are trivial once you have a steady income (under 200g per hop). Use stables liberally — the time saved is worth far more than 150g. Never walk across the entire map when you can teleport.

## 🧭 Navigation Tips & Shortcuts

Mastering movement in Palia is about understanding the terrain and using your **Glider** effectively.

### Vertical Shortcuts with the Glider

The Glider is your most important navigation tool. You unlock it through the main story, and it changes how you traverse both zones.

| Trick | How To | Best Use |
|:------|:-------|:---------|
| Cliff Launch | Run off a high ledge, deploy glider | Skip entire climbing routes |
| Updraft Canyon | Launch from high cliffs toward Bahari | Fastest way between zones |
| Tree Launch | Climb a tall tree, jump + glide | Reach elevated ore deposits |
| Building Leap | Jump from NPC roof spans | Town navigation shortcuts |
| Coast Glide | Use wind currents along Bahari coast | Rapid east-west travel |

**Key glider routes:**
- **Kilima Library → Town:** Jump off Library cliff, glide directly south to town center — saves 2 minutes of walking
- **Bahari Bluffs → Coast:** Leap from Hideaway Bluffs, glide west to Bahari Coast pier — saves 3+ minutes
- **Pavel Mines → Flooded Steps:** Exit Pavel Mines upper entrance, glide east across the valley

### Wall Jumping

Palia has a hidden mobility secret — **wall jumping**. If you jump against a wall and immediately jump again while pressing into the wall, you can climb surfaces that aren't marked as climbable.

| Surface Type | Wall Jumpable? | Notes |
|:-------------|:--------------|:------|
| Stone Cliffs | Yes | Best surface, consistent |
| Wood Walls | Sometimes | Building exteriors |
| Fences | No | Too low |
| Trees | No | Climb marked trees instead |
| Cave Walls | Yes | Pavel Mines shortcuts |

> **Tip:** Wall jumping is essential for reaching hidden ore deposits in Pavel Mines. The Palium nodes on the upper ledges are only reachable via wall jumps or careful gliding from higher ground.

### Terrain Navigation Guide

| Terrain Type | Movement | Best Strategy |
|:-------------|:---------|:--------------|
| **Flat ground** (Kilima town, Pulsewater Plains) | Sprint freely | Use speed boost (shift) |
| **Grassy hills** (Most of Kilima) | Slight slowdown | Follow paths, avoid steep angles |
| **Wooded areas** (Proudhorn Forest, Elderwood) | Moderate slowdown | Stick to animal trails |
| **Rocky cliffs** (Bahari highlands) | Climbing only | Wall jump + glider combo |
| **Water** | Swimming (very slow) | Avoid — use bridges or glide over |
| **Caves** (Pavel Mines) | Walking only | Mark your path, caves loop around |

### Quick Travel Routes Between Zones
