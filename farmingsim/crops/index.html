    Fertilizer Effects

    Fertilizations Yield Bonus Suggestion
    0 times (no fertilizer) Base yield ❌ Wasting the field
    1 time +15% ✅ At least 1 to start
    2 times +30% ✅ Must-do at mid-game
    3 times (perfect) +50% ⭐ Pursuing maximum yield

    Core strategy: Save money with wheat as a beginner → pivot to sunflowers/canola mid-game → plant soybeans + grapes late-game. 2 fertilizations offer the best value; the marginal benefit of a 3rd is diminishing.

    🌿 Season-by-Season Planting Guide

    Spring Crops (Plant Mar–Apr)

    Crop Why Plant in Spring Best For
    🌾 Wheat Fast growth, harvest before fall busy season ✅ Beginners, cow feed
    Barley Quickest turnaround, harvest by Jun ✅ Second crop in rotation
    Oats Fastest growth (5 days), horse feed ⚠️ Moderate profit
    Canola High price/1000L, short growth window ✅ Mid-game cash crop
    🥔 Potatoes Highest profit per ha, needs full season ✅ Dedicated potato fields
    Sugar Beet High volume, excellent for processing ✅ Sugar mill chain

    Summer Crops (Plant Apr–May)

    Crop Why Plant in Summer Best For
    🌽 Corn Thrives in warm weather, highest yield volume ✅ Silage & grain
    🌻 Sunflowers Fast summer growth, decent return ✅ Steady income
    Soybeans Highest base price, profit king ✅ Best ROI per day
    Sorghum Drought-tolerant, low input cost ✅ Dry or low-fertility fields
    🌾 Rice Needs warm water and paddy fields only ✅ Late-game expansion
    Cotton Long growth but excellent fabric profit ✅ Processing chain synergy

    Fall Activities (Sep–Nov)

    Task Details
    🚜 Harvest everything Before first frost — postponed harvest loses yield
    🌾 Plant Winter Wheat/Barley Grows through winter, harvests early spring next year
    🧑‍🌾 Apply fertilizer Prep fields for next season
    🛠️ Service equipment Repair & store before winter downtime
    📦 Sell grain Check prices — fall often has the year's best prices for grain crops

    Winter Tasks (Dec–Feb)

    Task Details
    🐄 Animal care Focus on feeding, breeding, and product collection
    🪵 Logging / forestry Best off-season income source
    🏗️ Build processing plants Flour mill, oil press, bakery, sugar mill
    📊 Plan next season Review market trends, pre-order seeds, buy land
    🚜 Equipment upgrades Shop off-season sales and used vehicle market

    🔄 Crop Rotation Recommendations

    Plan Field Setup Why It Works
    🟢 Beginner 2-Field A: Wheat → Barley; B: Canola → Oats Simple, minimal equipment, steady $
    🟡 Mid-Game 3-Field 1: Potatoes → Winter Wheat; 2: Corn → Soybeans; 3: Sunflowers → Canola Balances high-profit crops with soil-resting periods
    🔴 Advanced 4-Field 1: Sugar Beet → Wheat; 2: Potatoes → Barley; 3: Corn → Soybeans; 4: Cotton → Canola Maximum diversity, year-round harvest, processing synergy
    🌱 Hay Rotation Grass (3 yr) → Corn (2 yr) → Wheat (1 yr) Low management, perfect for animal feed operations

    Rotation Tips

    • Follow high-profit crops (potatoes/sugar beet) with wheat/barley — they benefit from residual fertilizer
    • Don't plant the same crop twice in a row — yield penalty applies
    • Plant corn after potatoes — potatoes break up soil, corn loves the loose ground
    • Use winter crops to keep fields producing through the cold months
