    Quick Tips

    • Early Spring: Plant Carrots and Radishes — fast turnaround for early cash flow.
    • Summer staple: Tomatoes are excellent early-regrowth crops for consistent income.
    • Fall jackpot: Pumpkins are the #1 profit crop in the game — plant as many as you can.
    • Winter farming: Crystal Cauliflower matches summer melons for profit/day.
    • Processing priority: Put Melon, Pumpkin, and Watermelon through Kegs for massive profit boosts.
    • Regrowth crops: Strawberry, Cranberry, and Frost Berry save replanting costs over long seasons.
