    🥥 Complete Golden Walnut Guide — All 130 Locations

    Golden Walnuts are the primary currency on Ginger Island (Stardew Valley 1.5+). They unlock the Island Farmhouse, Resort, Volcano shortcuts, Parrot Express fast travel, and more. There are 130 Golden Walnuts to find. This guide covers every single one.

    Data from Stardew Valley Wiki v1.6

    📊 Quick Reference: Total Walnuts by Area

    Area Walnuts Key Activities
    🏝️ Ginger Island West 20 Foraging, farming, talking to Leo
    ⛏️ Volcano Dungeon 18 Mining, chests, combat
    🏖️ Ginger Island South 15 Fishing, coral, mermaid puzzle
    🌴 Ginger Island East 16 Dig site, field office puzzles
    🌊 Ginger Island North 8 River fishing, mining
    🐒 Leo's Hut / Jungle 12 Talking to Leo, parrot drops
    🦜 Parrot Hints 10 Pay 1 walnut per hint
    🎣 Fishing 5 Fishing mini-game (all island waters)
    🦀 Crab Pots 5 Crab pots in island waters
    🎲 Darts / Pirate Cove 5 Darts game & NPC gifts
    🎪 Island Trader 3 Trade items to Island Trader
    ⚔️ Volcano (Mobs) 3 Tiger Slime drops
    🍌 Banana / Mango trees 3 Plant fruit trees on island
    🏆 Field Office 3 Complete Island Field Office bundles
    🧩 Mermaid Puzzle 5 Solve the flute block puzzle
    ✨ Misc / Secrets 10 Various hidden spots
    Total 130

    🏝️ Ginger Island West (20 Walnuts)

    Foraging & Farming (12 Walnuts)

    # Location How to Get
    1–3 Beach/West shore Forage — 3 walnuts buried in the sand (dig spots)
    4 Farm area Behind the broken house — dig spot
    5 River near farm Fish ≥ 2 walnuts from the river (bubbles increase chance)
    6–9 Tillable soil Plant crops on the island — each harvest has a small chance
    10 West path Panning spot west of the farm
    11 Parrot Express station Behind the large tree near the station
    12 Gem birds area On the ground near the east exit

    Journal Scrap #1 — Bird Puzzle (5 Walnuts)

    Gem Bird Location (Tap the bush) Walnuts
    💜 Amethyst Bird North of farm, near river 1
    💚 Emerald Bird South of farm, near beach 1
    ❤️ Ruby Bird East of farm, near dig site path 1
    💎 Diamond Bird West of farm, near jungle entrance 1
    All 4 correct Return to the center stone pedestal 1 bonus

    Leo (3 Walnuts)

    # Condition Walnuts
    1 First meeting with Leo 1
    2 Reaching 3 hearts with Leo 1
    3 Reaching 6 hearts with Leo 1
    4 Reaching 9 hearts with Leo 1 (bonus — counts as "talk to Leo" rewards)

    🏖️ Ginger Island South (15 Walnuts)

    Fishing (5 Walnuts)

    # Spot Notes
    1–3 South shore pier Cast into the ocean from the pier
    4 Tide pools (left) Small pool on the west side
    5 Tide pools (right) Small pool on the east side

    Mermaid Puzzle (5 Walnuts)

    Step Action
    1 Wait for rain (any weather works — rain is required for the mermaid to appear)
    2 Go to the south beach — find the mermaid sitting on a rock
    3 Interact with the flute block nearby
    4 Play the notes matching the mermaid's song: 1-3-5-3-1 (clicks on blocks from left to right)
    5 Collect 5 Golden Walnuts from a chest that appears

    Coral / Beach (5 Walnuts)

    # Location How to Get
    1–3 South beach Dig spots in the sand
    4 Tide pool rocks Hidden behind the large rock
    5 Near the Resort Behind the Resort changing area

    🌴 Ginger Island East — Dig Site (16 Walnuts)

    Dig Site Surface (8 Walnuts)

    # Location How to Get
    1–4 Dig spot clusters Artifact spots in the dig site area
    5 Behind the tent Dig spot behind Professor Snail's tent
    6–7 River fishing Fish in the dig site river (2 walnuts)
    8 Hidden bush Bush near the south exit

    Excavation & Field Office (3 Walnuts)

    # Task Details
    1 Complete Snake set Donate all 4 Snake parts to Field Office
    2 Complete Bat set Donate all 4 Bat parts to Field Office
    3 Complete Frog set Donate all 4 Frog parts to Field Office

    Journal Scrap #2 — Gem Bird (5 Walnuts)

    Gem Bird Location Walnuts
    💜 Amethyst North of dig site 1
    💚 Emerald East of dig site 1
    ❤️ Ruby South of dig site 1
    💎 Diamond West of dig site 1
    Bonus Center pedestal 1

    🌊 Ginger Island North (8 Walnuts)

    # Location How to Get
    1–3 River Fish in the north river
    4 Mining spot Panning spot near the river
    5 Bush (northwest) Hidden behind the bush near the Volcano entrance
    6 Behind the lava pool Dig spot near the lava pool
    7 Gem bird north Behind the Volcano (requires Journal Scrap #3)
    8 Bonus gem bird Center pedestal near north area

    ⛏️ Volcano Dungeon (18 Walnuts)

    Regular Floors (10 Walnuts)

    # Floor Range How to Get
    1–5 Floors 1–5 Check every crate and barrel — 1–5 walnuts hidden
    6–8 Floors 6–8 Hidden in pots and crates
    9 Floor 9 Hidden crate on this floor only
    10 Floor 10 (Shop) Chest behind the Dwarf merchant

    Treasure Rooms (5 Walnuts)

    # How to Get
    1 Open the golden chest in the Floor 5 treasure room
    2 Open the golden chest in the Floor 10 treasure room
    3 Rare drop from treasure room chest (any floor)
    4 Behind the treasure room doorway
    5 Hidden in corner of treasure room

    Volcano Top / Forge (3 Walnuts)

    # Location
    1 Behind the Forge anvil
    2 Outside the Forge, west side
    3 Hidden behind the pillar near exit

    🎲 Pirate Cove (Island West — Night) (5 Walnuts)

    # Activity How to Get
    1–3 Darts game Score 3+ points in the Pirate Darts mini-game
    4 Talk to Pirate Give a gift to the pirate (any gift works once)
    5 Hidden behind barrels In the back of the Pirate Cave

    🐒 Leo's Hut & Jungle (12 Walnuts)

    # Condition Walnuts
    1 Reach 3 hearts with Leo 1
    2 Reach 6 hearts with Leo 1
    3 Reach 9 hearts with Leo 1
    4–10 Parrot drops (Leo's Hut) The parrot in Leo's Hut drops walnuts over time
    11 Tree behind hut Dig spot behind Leo's Hut
    12 Jungle path Hidden in the jungle path near the exit

    🎣 Fishing (5 Walnuts)

    # Location Notes
    1 Island West — river Cast near the bridge
    2 Island South — ocean Cast from the pier
    3 Island East — river Dig site river
    4 Island North — river North river near Volcano
    5 Pirate Cove — ocean Night fishing, west cove

    All fishing walnuts require Cast ≥ 4 tiles away from shore for best results.

    🦞 Crab Pots (5 Walnuts)

    # Location How to Get
    1 Island West shore Place crab pot, catch something
    2 Island South shore Same
    3 Island East shore Same
    4 Island North river Same
    5 Pirate Cove Place in the cove water

    Each unique location has a chance to produce a walnut from a crab pot catch. Move pots around.

    🍌 Tree Fruits (3 Walnuts)

    # Tree Action
    1 Banana Tree Harvest from a Banana Tree planted on Ginger Island
    2 Mango Tree Harvest from a Mango Tree planted on Ginger Island
    3 Any fruit tree Harvest from any fruit tree (Banana or Mango) that's fully grown

    ⚔️ Tiger Slime Drops (3 Walnuts)

    # Enemy Drop Rate Notes
    1–3 Tiger Slime (Volcano) ~50% Kill Tiger Slimes in the Volcano Dungeon

    🦜 Parrot Hints (10 Walnuts)

    After unlocking the Parrot Hints system (talk to the parrot in Leo's Hut), you can pay 1 walnut to receive a hint about the next walnut location. The total cost for all hints is 10 walnuts.

    Tip: Don't use hints early — explore freely and save hints for the last 20–30 walnuts when you're stuck.

    🛒 Island Trader (3 Walnuts)

    # Trade Cost
    1 Trade a Taro Root 1 Taro Root
    2 Trade a Poi 1 Poi
    3 Trade a Tropical Curry 1 Tropical Curry

    ✅ Unlock Priority Order

    Walnuts Spent Unlock Why
    10 Island Farmhouse Sleep on the island — no more rushing back
    20 Island Resort Opens Pirate Cove, NPC visits, beach activities
    10 Parrot Express Fast travel between island zones
    5 Volcano Shortcut #1 Skip first 5 Volcano floors
    5 Volcano Shortcut #2 Skip first 10 Volcano floors
    20 Tent (Dig Site) Sleep at the dig site
    1 per hint Parrot Hints Use when stuck

    Total to unlock everything needed: ~71 walnuts. The remaining 59 are needed for the Perfection Tracker (100% completion).

    💡 Pro Tips

    1. Use a Burglar Ring when farming Tiger Slimes to maximize drop chances
    2. Fishing Bubbles significantly increase walnut catch rate — always fish where you see bubbles
    3. Rain totems work on the island — use one for the Mermaid Puzzle instead of waiting for rain
    4. Journal Scraps (Buried in the island sand) provide clues — read them as you find them
    5. Crab Pots are the slowest method — set them early and check daily
    6. Plant lots of crops on the island farm — every harvest has a small chance to dig up a walnut
    7. Last 10 walnuts are always the hardest — use Parrot Hints when you're stuck
