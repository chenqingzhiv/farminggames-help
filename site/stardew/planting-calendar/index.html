    Day Activity Planting Notes
    22 🌱 Harvest strawberries — Continue regrow cycle
    23 🎣 Fishing — Target salmon (river)
    24 💃 FLOWER DANCE — Attend the dance in town
    25 🌱 Water strawberries — Keep the income flowing

    27 🎣 Fishing — Prepare for summer
    28 🌱 Final spring harvest — Sell remaining crops

    💃 Flower Dance (Spring 24): - Location: Town (by the lake) - Requirements: At least 4 hearts with a bachelor/bachelorette to dance with them - Hat available: Flower Crown (500g at booth)

    💡 Calendar Tips

    1. Strawberries on Spring 13 are mandatory — Buy every seed you can afford. They pay for themselves in 2 harvests.
    2. Blueberries > Starfruit raw, but Starfruit wine > Blueberry — Plant blueberries for steady income, starfruit for kegging.
    3. Pigs in Fall are the best seasonal income — Place them under trees, collect truffles, keg the jelly.
    4. Winter = Mining season — No crops to tend, so skull cavern or mines are the best use of time.
    5. Greenhouse is year-round — Plant ancient fruit (once you have seeds) for unlimited income.
    6. Festival items matter — Strawberry seeds (Spring 13), Rare Seeds (Fall 6), free fertilizer (Summer 6).
    7. Plan your keg count — Build kegs before summer. You need 50+ for blueberry/starfruit wine production.
    8. Save your gold in Year 1 — Don't spend on decorations. Invest in seeds, kegs, and animals.

    All dates and prices are based on Stardew Valley 1.6.9. Festival dates are fixed and cannot be skipped. Some festivals have weather dependencies.
