    ✨ Complete Trinkets Guide (1.6 Update)

    Trinkets are a brand-new equipment slot introduced in Stardew Valley 1.6. They provide unique passive or active abilities and can only be obtained in the late game. You can equip one trinket at a time alongside your two rings.

    Trinkets are found by defeating monsters, opening treasure chests, and completing special orders — they are endgame items that significantly change your playstyle.

    📊 Detailed Trinket Stats

    S-Tier Trinkets

    🏹 Magic Quiver

    Property Value
    Effect Fires homing arrows at nearby enemies every 1.5 seconds
    Damage 10-20 damage per arrow (scales with combat level)
    Range Medium (8 tiles radius)
    Source Skull Cavern monsters (drop rate ~1%)
    Best For Auto-targeting slimes, bats, and serpents while you mine

    ⭐ Rating: 5/5 — Best combat trinket

    "The Magic Quiver is the ultimate QoL trinket — it automatically clears bats and serpents while you focus on mining. Essential for deep Skull Cavern runs."

    ✨ Fairy Box

    Property Value
    Effect Summons a pet fairy that heals 10-20 HP every 5 seconds
    Range Unlimited — fairy follows you
    Source Mines deep floors (110-120), rare drop from monsters
    Best For Combat-heavy runs, Skull Cavern sustain

    ⭐ Rating: 5/5 — Best survival trinket

    "Free healing without food consumption. The Fairy Box makes you nearly immortal in combat — just focus on dealing damage while the fairy handles recovery."

    🐍 Basilisk Paw

    Property Value
    Effect 100% immunity to all debuffs (slime slow, bat blindness, spider web, etc.)
    Source Volcano Dungeon monsters (very rare drop)
    Best For Skull Cavern (debuffs are deadly there)

    ⭐ Rating: 5/5 — Best defensive trinket

    "Debuff immunity is massive. No more slime slowness, no more bat blindness, no more spider webs. The Basilisk Paw transforms your combat experience."

    A-Tier Trinkets

    🦜 Parrot Egg

    Property Value
    Effect A parrot follows you and picks up foraged items + digs up artifacts
    Pickup Range 5 tiles around you
    Source Trade Golden Walnuts with Ginger Island parrots
    Best For Foraging runs, artifact hunting

    ⭐ Rating: 4/5 — Excellent QoL

    "Parrot Egg automates foraging — the bird collects everything within range. Great for Spring Onion season and artifact farming on Ginger Island."

    🪙 Golden Spur

    Property Value
    Effect +10% mining speed; enemies have a chance to drop stone
    Source Skull Cavern monsters (rare drop)
    Best For Deep mining, staircase building

    ⭐ Rating: 4/5 — Mining-focused

    "Faster mining AND bonus stone from kills = more stairs for deep Skull Cavern dives. A solid choice for dedicated miners."

    🔱 Deluxe Spook Fish

    Property Value
    Effect Reveals hidden items AND monsters on your screen
    Source Upgrade Spook Fish with Qi Gems (at Qi's Walnut Room)
    Cost 1 Spook Fish + 10 Qi Gems
    Best For Secret note completion, artifact hunting

    ⭐ Rating: 5/5 — Information trinket

    "See all hidden goodies on the map — secret artifacts, hidden notes, even monsters. This is the completionist's dream trinket."

    B-Tier Trinkets

    Trinket Rating Why Not S-Tier
    Ice Rod ⭐⭐⭐ Unreliable freeze chance — good but inconsistent
    Anvil ⭐⭐⭐ Doubling ore is great, but mining trinkets are more useful in combat
    Lifesaver ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Shield is strong, but Fairy Box provides better sustain
    Treasure Totem ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Useful for artifact spots but limited use
    Magic Bait Dispenser ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Convenient but not game-changing
    Fossilized Skull ⭐⭐⭐ Ghost skull pet is cosmetic + minor damage
    Spook Fish ⭐⭐⭐ Only shows hidden items — Deluxe version is much better

    🎯 Best Trinket for Each Playstyle

    Playstyle Recommended Trinket Reason
    🗡️ Combat/Exploration Magic Quiver Auto-targeting keeps you safe while exploring
    🛡️ Survival/Defense Fairy Box Passive healing saves food and prevents deaths
    ⛏️ Deep Mining Golden Spur Faster mining + stone for stairs
    🌿 Foraging/Farming Parrot Egg Auto-collects foraged items
    🔍 Completionist Deluxe Spook Fish Reveals ALL hidden secrets
    🏹 Balanced Basilisk Paw Debuff immunity = smooth gameplay

    💡 Pro Tips

    1. Trinkets are endgame items — don't expect them before reaching the bottom of the Mines
    2. You can only equip one trinket — choose carefully based on your current activity
    3. Fairy Box + Vampire Ring = almost infinite sustain in combat
    4. Magic Quiver + Burglar's Ring = kills + double loot while mining
    5. Basilisk Paw is mandatory for Qi's dangerous mines challenges
    6. Combine trinkets with rings at the Forge — rings, not trinkets, can be combined
    7. The Spook Fish upgrade path is worth the Qi Gems — Deluxe Spook Fish is incredible for secret notes
