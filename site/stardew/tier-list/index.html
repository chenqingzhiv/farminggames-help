    🏆 Complete Crop Tier Ranking

    Data version: Stardew Valley 1.6 | Based on daily profit, growth speed, and regrow ability

    S Tier — Must-Plant Legends

    These crops are the absolute core of profit-making. Recommended for both beginners and veterans.

    Tier名称说明
    S ⭐ Starfruit The strongest Summer crop. Base sell price 750g, 3150g when kegged. Grow year-round after unlocking Greenhouse. 26.92g/day. Core of Keg-focused strategy
    S 🍓 Strawberry King of Spring! Buy seeds at Egg Festival (Spring 13), regrows every 4 days. Mass plant in Year 2 Spring. 20.83g/day profit
    S 🫐 Blueberry One of the Summer regrow giants. Harvest every 4 days, yields 3 berries per harvest. Excellent energy efficiency. 20.80g/day base profit
    S 🫐 Cranberry Fall regrow king. Harvest every 3 days, yields 2 per harvest. Fall economic pillar. 18.89g/day profit

    A Tier — Excellent Crops

    Not the absolute strongest, but pairing them with Keg/processing yields great profit, or they excel in specific scenarios.

    Tier名称说明
    A 🌿 Hops Keg efficiency king! Harvest daily after 11 days growth. Pale Ale sells for 300g (10x value). Greenhouse essential. ~16.50g/day profit
    A 🎃 Pumpkin High-value Fall crop. Can become giant (3-15x harvest). 16.92g/day profit. Seeds available Thursday at Oasis
    A 🍈 Melon High-value Summer crop. Can become giant, 1050g when kegged. 14.17g/day profit. Great for gifts
    A ☕ Coffee Bean Expensive early but a game-changer later. Plant once, permanent production, daily harvest. Coffee provides speed buff. Full auto-production line at scale
    A 🥦 Cauliflower High-value Spring crop. Can become giant. Required for bundles. 7.92g/day profit. Explosive profit when giant
    A 🍇 Grape Fall regrow crop, harvest every 3 days. Great profit when made into wine. 16.00g/day profit
    A 🌾 Wheat Plantable in Summer and Fall. Beer gives 20x value! Pairs well with Kegs for fast, high-return production

    B Tier — Decent

    Decent profit with specific uses or advantages, but can't compete with the top-tier crops.

    Tier名称说明
    B 🥔 Potato Early Spring money maker. Chance for 2-6 potatoes per harvest, actual profit varies. ~5g/day profit, recommended early on
    B 🌶️ Hot Pepper Summer regrow crop, harvest every 3 days. Shane's favorite gift. 10.67g/day profit
    B 🍅 Tomato Summer regrow, harvest every 4 days. 9.26g/day profit. Used in many cooking recipes
    B 🍆 Eggplant Fall regrow, harvest every 5 days. 11.20g/day profit. Good for players who don't want to harvest daily
    B 🥬 Kale Fast Spring growth (6 days). 6.67g/day profit. Bundle item, good for a quick start
    B 🥕 Radish Fast Summer crop (6 days). 8.33g/day profit. 225g when kegged
    B 🫘 Yam Fall crop (10 days). 10g/day profit. Bundle requirement, Linus' favorite
    B 🥬 Rhubarb Spring, 100g seeds (Oasis), 13 days mature. 9.23g/day profit. Can be made into wine
    B 🌿 Amaranth Fast Fall crop (7 days). 11.43g/day profit. Decent energy restoration

    C Tier — Not Recommended

    Low profit, limited use. Don't plant unless for specific needs (bundles, gifts, decoration).

    Tier名称说明
    C 🌿 Parsnip Beginner crop. Low price, only 3.75g/day profit. Grow only what you need for bundles
    C 🌽 Corn Spans Summer and Fall, but only 7.5g/day profit. Saves space — plant once for two seasons
    C 🌻 Sunflower Summer, expensive seeds (200g), produces seeds but low profit. 4g/day profit. Mostly for oil
    C 🌺 Poppy Summer flower, 5.71g/day profit. Mainly for Chef gifts and Lucky Lunch
    C 🌹 Fairy Rose Fall flower, 200g seeds, 7.5g/day profit. Mainly for Jas gifts
    C 🥬 Bok Choy Fast Fall crop (6 days). 5g/day profit. Grow only what you need for bundles
    C 🌷 Tulip Spring flower, 1.67g/day profit. Purely decorative. Evelyn's favorite
    C 💐 Blue Jazz Spring flower, 2.86g/day profit. Sandy's favorite gift, limited other uses

    Tier Rating Criteria

    Grade Daily Profit Range Description
    S >18g/day Must-plant, profit core. Prioritize Greenhouse + main farm plots
    A 10-18g/day Excellent, even better with processing. Recommended to plant moderately
    B 5-10g/day Decent, useful in specific scenarios. Fine for beginners in transition
    C <5g/day Not recommended for profit only. Limited to bundles/gifts/decor

    💡 Key Advice: Once you unlock the Keg, prioritize Starfruit/Hops for brewing — profit rate increases 10x. Keep Greenhouse planted with Starfruit + Hops year-round.
