    🍁 Complete Fall Guide

    Fall is the most profitable season in Stardew Valley! Cranberries provide continuous yield + Pumpkins sell at a high price, combined with keg profits to double your income.

    Fall Crop Recommendations

    Crop Price Days to Mature Regrowth Season Income Rating
    Cranberries 🫐 75g/2 berries 7 days Every 2 days Highest ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
    Pumpkin 🎃 320g 13 days One-time High ⭐⭐⭐⭐
    Amaranth 🌿 150g 7 days One-time Medium ⭐⭐
    Eggplant 🍆 60g 7 days Every 4 days Medium ⭐⭐
    Yam 🥔 160g 10 days One-time Medium ⭐

    New farm suggestion: Cranberries×80 + Pumpkins×40

    Fall Festivals

    Date Festival Key Point
    Fall 16 Stardew Valley Fair Save Iridium-quality items + Diamonds + Wine in advance
    Fall 27 Spirit's Eve Get the Golden Pumpkin

    Fall Key: Prepare for Winter

    1. Upgrade sprinklers (prepare for the greenhouse)
    2. Stockpile seeds (for Spring 1 planting rush)
    3. Unlock the greenhouse (if you haven't yet)

    🎣 Fall Fishing

    Fall fishing adds unique seasonal catches:

    Fish Location Time Sell Price (Iridium) Notes
    Salmon River 6am-7pm 300g Fall-only
    Walleye River 12pm-2am (rain) 315g Requires rain
    Tiger Trout River 6am-7pm 300g Also in Gingersnork
    Angler River (north of farm bridge) N/A 2,000g Legendary fish — one per save
    Midnight Carp River 10pm-2am 750g Fall/Winter, Sewer

    Pro Tip: Use a Trap Bobber + Dish o' the Sea to catch the Angler. Bring the best rod you have and aim for max casting distance.

    🌿 Fall Foraging & Gathering

    Item Location Sell Price Use
    Hazelnut Forage on ground 60g Fall forage bundle
    Blackberry Bushes (daily in Fall) 20g Blackberry cobbler
    Chanterelle Secret Woods 160g Life Elixir
    Common Mushroom Secret Woods 80g Fried Mushroom
    Red Mushroom Mines 120g Gift for Demetrius

    🏛️ Stardew Valley Fair — 1000 Star Tokens Strategy

    The Summer 16 Stardew Valley Fair is your chance to get rare items. Here's how to get 1000 tokens easily:

    Best Approach: Fishing mini-game - Takes ~5 minutes at the fishing booth - Easy to score 100+ tokens per attempt with practice - No need to save items all year

    Alternative (Item-Based): Bring high-quality items for the grange display: - 9 Items × iridium quality + varied categories = max points - Diamond, Wine, Gold Bar, Iridium-quality crops

    Best Purchase: Stardrop (2,000 tokens) — permanent energy boost. Save your tokens for this.

    💰 Fall Profit Maximization

    Activity Daily Gold Notes
    Cranberries (200 plants) ~3,000g Continuous harvest, low effort
    Pumpkin (100) + Keg ~2,500g High profit per plant
    Ancient Fruit + Keg ~4,000g If unlocked from greenhouse
    Preserves Jars (Cranberries) +40% value Continuous input
    Kegs (Hops saved from Summer) ~500g/day Already grown, just process

    Bottom Line: Cranberries for steady passive income + Pumpkins (kegged) for big batches = optimal Fall economy. Don't forget to plant corn on Summer 28! It survives into Fall for a head start.
