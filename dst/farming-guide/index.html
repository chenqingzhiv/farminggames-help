    🌱 Don't Starve Together — Complete Farming & Gardening Guide

    This guide covers the Gardening system introduced in the 2022 QoL update.

    Farming in Don't Starve Together underwent a major overhaul in 2022. Gone are the simple "plant and wait" days — the new system adds soil nutrients, crop families, plant stress, and giant crops as rewards for optimal care.

    🧮 Farming Basics

    The 3 Core Resources

    Every crop you plant consumes and produces three nutrients:

    Resource Effect Depleted By
    🟤 Compost (Grey soil) General growth nutrient Leafy crops
    🔵 Manure (Manure pile) Root development Root crops
    🔴 Growth Formula (Bubbles) Stem/fruit growth Fruiting crops

    Think of it like this: Each crop family depletes its own nutrient type while replenishing the other two.

    Crop Families

    Family Examples Consumes Produces
    🥬 Leafy Cabbage, Kale, Onion 🟤 Compost 🔵 Manure + 🔴 Growth Formula
    🥕 Root Carrot, Potato, Radish 🔵 Manure 🟤 Compost + 🔴 Growth Formula
    🌽 Fruiting Corn, Tomato, Eggplant, Pumpkin 🔴 Growth Formula 🟤 Compost + 🔵 Manure

    Family Rotation Strategy

    The key to healthy soil is rotating crop families:

    Season 1: Plant Leafy → Season 2: Plant Root → Season 3: Plant Fruiting

    This rotation keeps all nutrients balanced without needing fertiliser.

    🌟 Giant Crops

    When you plant 4+ of the same crop in a 3×3 grid and meet all conditions perfectly, crops can merge into a Giant Crop — yielding 3–6 regular crops when harvested with a Pitchfork.

    Giant Crop Requirements

    Crop Nutrient Balance Water Requirement Family Neighbours
    🥬 Giant Cabbage Balanced or high Compost Fully watered ≥4 Leafy nearby
    🌽 Giant Corn Balanced or high Growth Fully watered ≥4 Fruiting nearby
    🥕 Giant Carrot Balanced or high Manure Fully watered ≥4 Root nearby
    🍆 Giant Eggplant Balanced or high Growth Fully watered ≥4 Fruiting nearby
    🌶️ Giant Pepper Balanced or high Growth Fully watered ≥4 Fruiting nearby
    🥔 Giant Potato Balanced or high Manure Fully watered ≥4 Root nearby
    🎃 Giant Pumpkin Balanced or high Growth Fully watered ≥4 Fruiting nearby
    🍅 Giant Tomato Balanced or high Growth Fully watered ≥4 Fruiting nearby
    🧅 Giant Onion Balanced or high Compost Fully watered ≥4 Leafy nearby
    🌿 Giant Garlic Balanced or high Manure Fully watered ≥4 Root nearby

    How to Guarantee Giant Crops (Step by Step)

    1. Plant 9 seeds of the same crop in a 3×3 grid
    2. Tend each plant twice — once mid-growth, once near harvest
    3. Water every day if no rain
    4. Use a Gardeneer Hat to see plant health indicators
    5. Check Family Pressure — keep 4+ of the same family in range
    6. Harvest with a Pitchfork for 3–6x yield

    💡 Pro Tip: Giant crops do NOT spoil when left on the ground. You can leave them as emergency food reserves!

    💧 Fertiliser & Soil Management

    Fertiliser Types

    Item Compost Manure Growth Formula Source
    💩 Manure 0 +16 +2 From beefalo/koalefant
    🌿 Rot +8 0 0 Spoiled food
    🌺 Guano 0 +12 +4 From birds/pigs
    🧪 Growth Formula Starter +8 +8 +8 Crafted (nitre + ash + rot)
    🚫 Compost Wrap +24 0 0 Crafted (papyrus + rot + manure)
    🪱 Mushroom Planter Used for mushrooms — — Underground

    Soil Nutrient Reader

    Craft a Gardeneer Hat (3 × Silk, 1 × Grass Suit, 1 × Any Crop Seed) to see: - Current nutrient values for each plot - Plant health indicators (happy, neutral, stressed) - Growth stage progress - Moisture level

    🐝 Automated Farming

    Bee Farm Integration

    • Place Bee Boxes near your farm
    • Bees pollinate crops, increasing growth speed
    • Crops within 15 tiles of 4+ bee boxes grow ~15% faster

    Thresher + Brimstone Trap Automation

    Advanced players can: 1. Plant crops inside a walled pen 2. Use Brimstone Traps (from Wagstaff's skill tree) for early warning 3. Let Pigs/Bunnymen tend crops (they reduce stress)

    🔟 Farming Tips Summary

    1. Rotate families — Leafy → Root → Fruiting avoids fertiliser dependency
    2. Plant 9 of the same crop in a 3×3 grid for giant crop potential
    3. Talk to your plants — hovering near them with the Gardeneer Hat on reduces stress
    4. Use Rain Collectors — rain provides free watering
    5. Store Giant Crops — they never spoil when left in the ground
    6. Bee Boxes nearby — free pollination boost
    7. Don't overcrowd — keep ≥4 tiles of spacing for other plant families
    8. Craft Gardeneer Hat first — you cannot see nutrient values without it
    9. Plant season-appropriate crops — they grow faster and with less stress
    10. Grow Cabbage + Tomato — Pierogi + Salsa Fresca covers HP and hunger basics
