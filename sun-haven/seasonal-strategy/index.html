    📅 Sun Haven — Seasonal Strategy Guide

    Data version: Sun Haven 1.7 | Season-by-season breakdown for all three zones

    Sun Haven runs on a four-season calendar similar to Stardew Valley, but Winters here aren't a dead zone — there's always something profitable to do. This guide covers each season in detail, plus the unique zones Nel'Vari and Withergate.

    🌍 Season System Overview

    Each season lasts 28 days (4 weeks of 7 days). Crops are season-specific unless grown in Nel'Vari or on your farm with season-extending magic.

    Season Weather Temp Effect Special Event
    🌸 Spring Rain 40% of days Perfect for crops Spring Festival (Day 15)
    ☀️ Summer Sun 70%, Storms 15% Crops grow +10% faster Summer Solstice (Day 21)
    🍂 Fall Wind 30%, Rain 30% Foraging yields +1 per bush Harvest Festival (Day 14)
    ❄️ Winter Snow 60%, Clear 20% Crops on farm grow −25% slower Starfall Night (Day 25)

    Tip: Winter's crop penalty only applies to outdoor farm tiles. Your greenhouse, Nel'Vari farm, and indoor plots are unaffected. Plan accordingly.

    🌸 Spring — The Fresh Start

    Spring is all about building your foundation. Your farm is clearing out, tool upgrades are starting, and every ticket matters.

    Best Spring Crops

    Crop Cost (per seed) Growth Regrowth Sell Price Gold/Day
    Strawberry 80g 8 days Every 4 days 120g 15g/day (raw)
    Tomato 50g 6 days Every 3 days 80g 13.3g/day (raw)
    Potato 40g 6 days No 100g 10g/day (raw)
    Green Bean 30g 10 days Every 3 days 60g 5.5g/day (raw)
    Carrot 20g 5 days No 45g 5g/day (raw)

    Processed Spring Profits:

    Crop → Product Machine Sell Price Gold/Day
    Strawberry → Wine Keg 360g 180g/day
    Tomato → Pickled Preserves Jar 210g 210g/day
    Potato → Juice Juice Press 175g 175g/day

    Tip: Strawberry is the king of Spring if you have Kegs. With 20 Strawberry plants + 5 Kegs you'll make 3,600g every 4 days from wine alone.

    Spring Priorities

    1. Day 1–5: Clear farm debris, plant 30 Carrots for quick cash, build Workbench
    2. Day 5–10: Upgrade Hoe to Copper, plant Strawberries, build a Keg
    3. Day 10–15: Save tickets, buy a Chicken and Mayo Machine
    4. Day 15: Spring Festival — buy Starfall seeds if available
    5. Day 15–20: Expand crop area to 50+ tiles, build second Keg
    6. Day 20–28: Focus on Mining — reach floor 15 for Copper/Iron ores

    Spring Checklist

    • [ ] Clear all farm debris (rocks, weeds, logs)
    • [ ] Plant 30+ Carrots for early cash
    • [ ] Build Workbench, Furnace, and Anvil
    • [ ] Transition to Strawberries by Day 8
    • [ ] Buy 1 Chicken + Mayo Machine
    • [ ] Upgrade Pickaxe to Copper
    • [ ] Attend Spring Festival (Day 15)
    • [ ] Reach mine floor 15

    ☀️ Summer — The Growth Season

    Summer's +10% growth speed makes it the most productive farming season. Crops mature faster and storms provide free watering.

    Best Summer Crops

    Crop Cost Growth Regrowth Sell Price Gold/Day
    Blueberry 60g 8 days Every 4 days 100g 12.5g/day (raw)
    Corn 50g 10 days Every 3 days 90g 9g/day (raw)
    Melon 100g 10 days No 250g 15g/day (raw)
    Hot Pepper 40g 8 days Every 3 days 70g 8.8g/day (raw)
    Sunflower 30g 6 days No 80g 8.3g/day (raw)

    Processed Summer Profits:

    Crop → Product Machine Sell Price Gold/Day
    Blueberry → Wine Keg 300g 150g/day
    Corn → Pickled Preserves Jar 225g 225g/day
    Melon → Juice Juice Press 438g 438g/day

    Tip: Melon Juice is the highest gold-per-day crop in Summer. One Juice Press processing a melon every 12 hours makes 438g/day — better than Kegging Starfruit in some other games.

    Summer Priorities

    1. Day 1–5: Plant 20+ Blueberries, 10 Melons, build 3 Kegs
    2. Day 5–10: Upgrade Barn to Lv 2 (auto-feed), buy a second animal
    3. Day 10–15: Build Cheese Press if you have a Cow/Goat
    4. Day 15–20: Expand to 60+ crop tiles, build 3 Preserves Jars
    5. Day 21: Summer Solstice festival
    6. Day 21–28: Mining focus — aim for floor 25+, stockpile Iron

    Summer Checklist

    • [ ] Plant 20+ Blueberries and 10 Melons
    • [ ] Build 3+ Kegs and 2+ Preserves Jars
    • [ ] Upgrade Barn to Lv 2 (auto-feed)
    • [ ] Buy at least 1 Cow for steady milk → cheese
    • [ ] Stockpile Wood and Stone for Fall preparations
    • [ ] Attend Summer Solstice
    • [ ] Reach mine floor 25

    🍂 Fall — The Harvest Season

    Fall brings the highest-value single crops — Pumpkins are the standout. This is also the best time to push deep into the mines before Winter.

    Best Fall Crops

    Crop Cost Growth Regrowth Sell Price Gold/Day
    Pumpkin 320g 12 days No 790g 39g/day (raw)
    Grape 80g 10 days Every 4 days 120g 12g/day (raw)
    Sweet Potato 60g 8 days Every 3 days 90g 11.3g/day (raw)
    Eggplant 50g 7 days Every 5 days 85g 8.5g/day (raw)
    Amaranth 40g 6 days No 95g 9.2g/day (raw)

    Processed Fall Profits:

    Crop → Product Machine Sell Price Gold/Day
    Pumpkin → Pickled Preserves Jar 1,580g 1,580g/day
    Grape → Wine Keg 360g 180g/day
    Sweet Potato → Juice Juice Press 158g 158g/day

    Pumpkin is the single most profitable crop in Sun Haven. A single Preserves Jar processing one Pumpkin per day makes 1,580g/day. With 10 jars, that's 15,800g/day.

    Fall Priorities

    1. Day 1–5: Plant 10+ Pumpkins, build 5 Preserves Jars
    2. Day 5–10: Plant Grapes for Kegging, build 5 Kegs
    3. Day 10–14: Stockpile Wood heavily — you'll need it for Winter prep
    4. Day 14: Harvest Festival — buy exclusive recipes
    5. Day 15–20: Push to mine floor 35+, collect Gold Ore
    6. Day 20–28: Final crop harvest, convert everything to artisan goods

    Fall Checklist

    • [ ] Plant 10+ Pumpkins on Day 1
    • [ ] Build 5+ Preserves Jars
    • [ ] Plant Grapes for Keg processing
    • [ ] Attend Harvest Festival
    • [ ] Stockpile 500+ Wood for Winter
    • [ ] Upgrade Pickaxe to Gold
    • [ ] Reach mine floor 35+
    • [ ] Save at least 15,000 Tickets for Winter investments

    ❄️ Winter — The Mining Season

    Winter penalizes outdoor crops (−25% growth), so focus shifts to mining, fishing, and animal products. Your greenhouse and any indoor plots are your only crop zones.

    Winter Crops (Greenhouse / Indoor Only)

    Crop Cost Growth Regrowth Sell Price Gold/Day
    Crystal Fruit 120g 7 days Every 5 days 180g 18g/day (raw)
    Snow Root 80g 6 days No 140g 10g/day (raw)
    Frost Berry 100g 10 days Every 4 days 150g 10.7g/day (raw)

    Processed Winter Profits:

    Crop → Product Machine Sell Price Gold/Day
    Crystal Fruit → Juice Juice Press 315g 315g/day
    Frost Berry → Wine Keg 450g 225g/day

    Winter Priorities (Non-Farming)

    Winter is the time to push endgame content.

    Activity Why Target
    Mining No crop distractions Reach floor 50+ (Mithril/Void ores)
    Fishing Winter fish are valuable Complete fish collection
    Combat Dungeon clearing Defeat Golem King and Shadow Warden
    Town Buildings Catch up on renovations Broken Bridge + Fountain
    House Upgrades Save and upgrade Reach Spacious or Grand Home
    Animal Focus Passive income all season 8+ animals with full artisan setup

    Winter Checklist

    • [ ] Move all crops to greenhouse / indoor plots
    • [ ] Plant Crystal Fruit for Juice Press production
    • [ ] Focus on mining — reach floor 50+
    • [ ] Complete animal barn upgrades to Lv 3+
    • [ ] Upgrade house to at least Spacious Home
    • [ ] Complete Broken Bridge renovation
    • [ ] Build Town Square Fountain
    • [ ] Fish for Winter-exclusive species
    • [ ] Attend Starfall Night (Day 25)
    • [ ] Prepare crop plan for next Spring

    Tip: Don't ignore animals in Winter. With a Lv 3 barn (auto-pet + auto-feed), animals produce at full rate regardless of weather. 8 Unicorns with Cheese Presses make 4,160g/day with zero effort.

    🌿 Nel'Vari & Withergate — Other Zones

    Nel'Vari (Unlocked mid-Summer)

    Accessible via the portal in the Sun Haven forest. Nel'Vari has its own season cycle and crops.

    Nel'Vari Crop Season Growth Sell Price Special
    Shadow Berry Any 8 days 200g +25% wine value
    Nether Wheat Any 6 days 60g Used for special breads
    Void Tomato Any 7 days 150g Best for Preserves Jar
    Moon Melon Any 12 days 500g Highest base price

    Nel'Vari priority: Unlock as soon as you can (around Day 15 of Summer). Start with 10 Void Tomatoes for Preserves Jar processing.

    Withergate (Unlocked late-Fall)

    Accessible via train from Sun Haven station (required 20,000 Tickets ticket). Harder enemies, better ores.

    Withergate Focus Why Target
    Void Ore mining Best bars for endgame gear Floor 10+ of Withergate mines
    Demon Dust farming Needed for Celestial crafting Imps and Shadow Knights
    Withergate exclusive recipes Best consumables in game Cooking Station upgrades
    Black Market Rare items and furniture Save 20,000+ Tickets

    Tip: Don't rush Withergate in your first year. Focus on Sun Haven and Nel'Vari until you have at least Mithril-tier gear. Withergate enemies hit hard and have 200+ HP.

    📊 Yearly Planning Tips

    Month-by-Month Priority

    Month #1 Priority #2 Priority Gold Target
    Spring 1 Clear farm, plant Carrots Build Workbench + Furnace 5,000
    Spring 2 Strawberries + Kegs Buy Chicken 10,000
    Spring 3 Expand crops, mine Barn upgrade Lv 2 15,000
    Spring 4 Save for Summer Reach mine F20 20,000
    Summer 1 Blueberries + Melons Build 3+ Kegs 30,000
    Summer 2 Add animals Build Cheese Press 45,000
    Summer 3 Expand to 60+ tiles Nel'Vari unlock 60,000
    Summer 4 Mine for Gold Ore Preserves Jar x5 80,000
    Fall 1 Pumpkins + Preserves Build 5 more jars 120,000
    Fall 2 Grapes + Kegs Stockpile Wood 160,000
    Fall 3 Push mine F35+ Upgrade Pickaxe 200,000
    Fall 4 Convert all to artisan Save for Winter 250,000
    Winter 1 Greenhouse crops Animal focus 280,000
    Winter 2 Push mine F50+ Town renovations 320,000
    Winter 3 Fishing collection House upgrade 360,000
    Winter 4 Prep for Spring/NG+ Starfall Night 400,000

    Year 2+ Strategy

    Once you've completed one year, you can start a New Game+ or continue building your empire:

    1. Max all skills — Get Crafting, Mining, and Combat to Level 10
    2. Complete all renovations — All 8 town buildings restored
    3. All house upgrades — Reach Estate (Lv 6)
    4. Luxury animal farm — All 4 Unicorns with Deluxe barn
    5. Withergate endgame — Celestial gear, Dragon Matriarch defeat

    Tip: In New Game+, you keep your house upgrades and skill levels. Focus on story quests you missed the first time around — some quests are season-locked.
