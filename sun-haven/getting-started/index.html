    ☀️ Sun Haven — First Week Guide

    Day 1-2: Getting Established

    Your adventure begins in Sun Haven Town, a lush elven settlement surrounded by farmland. Follow the introduction quests — they teach the basics of farming, crafting, and combat. By the end of day 1, you should have planted your first crops and completed the initial townsfolk introductions.

    Day 1 Essentials

    • [ ] Complete the tutorial quest line
    • [ ] Plant the free seeds you receive (wheat and sunflowers are your starting crops)
    • [ ] Visit the general store and buy a fishing rod
    • [ ] Explore the immediate farm area for forageables
    • [ ] Set up your basic crafting bench

    🌱 First Week Crop Plan

    Crops in Sun Haven grow quickly compared to other farming games. Focus on high-turnover crops:

    Crop Grow Time Seed Cost Profit Margin
    Wheat 2 days 5g 15g per tile/day
    Sunflower 3 days 10g 20g per tile/day
    Tomato 4 days 15g 25g per tile/day

    ⚔️ Combat & Dungeon Tips

    Combat unlocks around day 3 after you receive your first weapon from the town guard. The Sun Haven Mines are gentle for beginners — slimes and bats are the main threats.

    Beginner Combat Tips

    1. Kite enemies — Hit, retreat, hit again. Don't trade blows.
    2. Eat food for healing between fights — cooked meals restore much more HP than raw ingredients
    3. Upgrade your pickaxe first — better mining gear = better ores = better weapons
    4. Save monster drops — they're used in crafting recipes later

    💰 Best Early Money

    Method Income Per Day Notes
    🌾 Wheat farming 200-400g 2-day harvest cycle, non-stop profit
    🎣 Fishing 300-500g Fish are plentiful early on
    🗡️ Mine drops 400-700g Ores and gems from floor 1-10
    🍳 Cooking 500-800g Once you unlock the kitchen

    Tip: Spend your first week heavily invested in Farming skill. Reaching Farming level 5 by day 7 unlocks the Sprinkler blueprint, which automates watering and frees up 3+ hours of in-game time daily. That time can then go into mining or fishing, which snowballs your income dramatically for week 2.
