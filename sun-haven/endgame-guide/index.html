    🌟 Sun Haven — Endgame Guide

    Data version: Sun Haven 1.7 | Covers post-story content, marriage, children, Nel'Vari and Withergate farms, and 100% completion goals

    Sun Haven doesn't end when the credits roll. In fact, some of the game's richest content only unlocks after you've completed the main story across all three zones. This guide covers everything you can do in the endgame: expanding your empire across two additional farms, building a family, completing the Museum, unlocking every secret, and optimizing your late-game income.

    🏁 What Counts as Endgame in Sun Haven

    Unlike Stardew Valley where the endgame is largely self-directed, Sun Haven has a structured endgame with explicit goals. You are considered to be in the endgame when:

    • You've completed the main story quests in all three zones (Sun Haven, Nel'Vari, Withergate)
    • You have access to all three farms (main farm, Nel'Vari farm, Withergate apartment/plot)
    • Your skill levels are 50+ in your primary skills
    • You're working toward Museum completion and Exploration 100%

    📜 Main Story Completion

    The main story of Sun Haven spans three distinct zones, each with its own quest chain and final boss.

    ☀️ Sun Haven Storyline

    The first zone's story revolves around restoring the town and uncovering the mystery of the Sun Dragon. Key milestones:

    Milestone Quest Reward
    Clear first mine levels "Into the Mines" Combat unlocks
    Reach floor 25 "Depths of the Earth" Access to Sun Dragon skeleton
    Collect Sun Dragon shards "Dragon Restoration" Sun Haven story completion
    Defeat the Sun Dragon boss Final zone quest Major story advancement

    🌿 Nel'Vari Storyline

    After repairing the bridge east of Sun Haven (cost: 5,000 Tickets + materials), Nel'Vari opens. Its story focuses on the Elven Forest and the corruption spreading through it.

    Milestone Quest Reward
    Repair the bridge "Bridge to Nel'Vari" Zone access
    Clear Nel'Vari dungeon floors "Forest Depths" Unique crafting recipes
    Purify the corruption "Heart of the Forest" Nel'Vari farm upgrade
    Defeat the Forest Guardian Zone finale Major magic rewards

    💀 Withergate Storyline

    Unlocked by repairing the boat at the Sun Haven docks (cost: 8,000 Tickets + advanced materials). Withergate is an undead-themed city zone with its own rank system.

    Milestone Quest Reward
    Repair the boat "Setting Sail" Zone access
    Complete City Hall tasks "First Impressions" Withergate rank unlocks
    Clear Withergate mines "Underground Empire" Void ore access
    Defeat the Withergate boss "City of the Dead" finale Endgame gear

    Tip: Complete all three main storylines before diving deep into the Museum or skill grinding. Each zone's finale unlocks unique items, recipes, and farm upgrades that make the completionist grind dramatically easier.

    💍 Marriage and Children

    Dating

    Each zone has its own marriage candidates. Sun Haven has 12 candidates, Nel'Vari adds 4 elven candidates, and Withergate adds 4 undead/demon candidates — for a total of 20 marriageable NPCs.

    How to date: 1. Befriend the NPC to 8+ hearts (gifts, daily conversation, quests) 2. Purchase the Locket from the General Store (500 Tickets) 3. Give the Locket to the NPC → they become your boyfriend/girlfriend 4. Continue raising hearts to 10+

    Wedding

    Once a NPC reaches 10+ hearts while dating them:

    1. Purchase a Wedding Ring (2,000 Tickets from the General Store)
    2. Propose — the wedding is scheduled for the next day
    3. The ceremony takes place at the Sun Haven town square
    4. Your spouse moves into your farmhouse

    Spouse benefits: - They water crops, feed animals, or repair fences daily - They sometimes give you breakfast (a cooked meal with buffs) - They can have a child (see below) - Higher heart NPCs provide better daily help

    Children

    After marriage, once you've upgraded your farmhouse to include a nursery (3rd house upgrade, 15,000 Tickets + materials):

    1. Wait 14+ days after marriage
    2. Talk to your spouse — they will ask if you want a child
    3. Answer yes → child arrives in 20 days
    4. You can have up to 2 children

    Child progression: - Toddler stage: 20 days after birth — runs around the house - Child stage: 40 days after birth — follows you outside occasionally - Teen stage: 60 days after birth — begins helping with small farm tasks

    Tip: If you're trying to 100% the game, children are optional. They don't affect Museum completion or Exploration goals. Marry for the spouse perks (automatic animal feeding is a huge time saver), not completion.

    🌿 Nel'Vari Farm

    Once you complete the Nel'Vari storyline, you unlock a second farm in the elven forest. This farm has unique properties:

    Feature Details
    Size 40×40 tiles (slightly smaller than main farm)
    Soil type Magical — grows Nel'Vari-exclusive crops faster
    Water source Natural spring — no sprinkler needed for 8×8 area
    Climate Permanent Spring — grow any Nel'Vari crop year-round
    Buildings Can place barns, coops, and crafting stations

    Nel'Vari exclusive crops:

    Crop Season Grow Time Profit/Day Special
    Elven Wheat All 3 days 25g/day Used in elite cooking recipes
    Starfruit All 6 days 45g/day Best profit in zone
    Nel'Vari Nectar Flower All 5 days 38g/day Exclusive dessert ingredient
    Fairy Bloom All 4 days 30g/day Needed for Museum bundle

    Why farm Nel'Vari: The permanent Spring climate means you can grow high-value crops continuously without seasonal rotation. Starfruit wine from a Keg chain is one of the best endgame moneymakers.

    💀 Withergate Progression

    Withergate operates on a city rank system rather than a traditional relationship mechanic.

    City Ranks

    Rank Requirement Unlock
    Rookie Complete arrival quests Basic shop access
    Citizen 10+ quests completed, 2,000 Tickets paid Permanent apartment, second row of shops
    Respected 25+ quests, 10,000 Tickets paid Access to Withergate crafting unlocks
    Elite 50+ quests, 25,000 Tickets paid Best weapons, exclusive recipes
    Champion All Withergate quests, 50,000 Tickets paid Withergate boss re-fight, rare drops

    Withergate Crafting Unlocks

    Withergate offers some of the best gear in the game:

    Item Materials Effect
    Void Sword 15 Void Bar + 5 Essence 75 ATK, +10% Life Steal
    Shadow Armor Set 12 Void Bar + 3 Shadow Silk each piece High DEF + Shadow Resistance
    Necromancer Staff 20 Void Bar + 10 Essence + 5 Bones Summons skeleton minions
    Withergate Fishing Rod 8 Void Bar + 3 Shadow Wood Fish in Withergate's toxic waters

    Tip: Farming the Withergate mine floors 15–25 for Void Ore is mandatory for endgame gear. Bring the best combat buff meals you have — these floors are significantly harder than anything in Sun Haven or Nel'Vari.

    🏛️ Museum Completion

    The Sun Haven Museum accepts donations of rare items across several categories. Completing the Museum is one of the most satisfying completionist goals.

    Museum Categories

    Category Total Items Key Hard-to-Find Items
    Minerals 40+ Star Ruby, Void Crystal, Celestial Ore
    Gems 25+ Rainbow Gem, Moonstone (Withergate drops)
    Fish 35+ Sunfish, Void Eel, Golden Koi
    Crops 30+ Starfruit, Nectar Flower, Shadow Berry
    Artifacts 45+ Dragon Relic, Elven Crown, Withergate skull
    Monster Drops 30+ Dragon Scale, Fairy Dust, Void Essence

    Completion reward: Unlocking all Museum items grants a permanent "Curator's Blessing" buff — +15% sell price on everything, +2 to all skills. This is the single best permanent buff in the game.

    Museum Tips

    1. Always donate the first of everything. Never sell a mineral, fish, or artifact until you've checked if the Museum needs it.
    2. Fish in all three zones. Some fish only appear in Nel'Vari rivers or Withergate's toxic waters.
    3. Mine deep. The best gems and minerals are below floor 25 in each zone's mine.
    4. Grow every crop. Even crops you don't need for profit — grow one of each for the agriculture display.

    🗺️ Exploration Goals

    Sun Haven tracks Exploration as a percentage — visiting every named location, finding hidden areas, and discovering secrets.

    Hidden Areas

    Area Zone How to Find
    Hidden Pond Sun Haven (north-east forest) Path blocked by rocks, clear with Gold Pickaxe
    Fairy Grove Nel'Vari (behind waterfall) Requires bridge repair + quest item
    Withergate Sewers Withergate (manhole in alley) Requires City Rank: Citizen
    Sun Dragon Skeleton Sun Haven Mines F25 Story-mandatory, but easy to miss the side paths
    Ancient Library Nel'Vari Hidden behind cracked wall, requires mining
    Rooftop Garden Withergate Locked door on Withergate 2F, key from rank rewards

    Exploration Tips

    • Mark your map. The in-game map notes areas you've visited as "discovered" — check it regularly for gaps
    • Talk to everyone. NPCs sometimes hint at secret locations after reaching certain friendship thresholds
    • Break cracked walls. In all three zone's mines, cracked walls hide treasure rooms
    • Fish everywhere. Some fishing spots are hidden behind trees or rocks — reveal them with tool upgrades

    💰 Endgame Money-Making

    Once you have access to all three zones, your income potential skyrockets. Here are the best endgame farms:

    1. Starfruit Wine Empire (Nel'Vari)

    Investment Setup Cost Daily Income
    20 Kegs + 4 Sprinklers + Nel'Vari farm 15,000 Tickets 3,600g/day
    50 Kegs + 10 Sprinklers + Nel'Vari farm 35,000 Tickets 9,000g/day
    100 Kegs + 20 Sprinklers 70,000 Tickets 18,000g/day

    Starfruit grows in 6 days in Nel'Vari's permanent Spring. Starfruit Wine sells for 225g per bottle. With 50 Kegs producing every 3 days, that's 3,750g per harvest cycle.

    2. Unicorn Cheese Factory

    Setup Cost Daily Income
    4 Unicorns + 4 Cheese Presses 8,000 Tickets 2,080g/day
    10 Unicorns + 10 Cheese Presses 20,000 Tickets 5,200g/day

    Unicorn Milk (120g base) → Unicorn Cheese (520g) = 4.3× multiplier. This is passive income — collect milk, press cheese, sell.

    3. Withergate Void Tomato Pickling

    Void Tomatoes grow in 4 days and turn into Pickled Void Tomatoes (390g each). With the Withergate apartment's small farm plot + sprinklers:

    • 40 Void Tomato plants + 10 Preserves Jars
    • Setup: 6,000 Tickets
    • Daily income: ~2,400g/day

    4. Honey + Cheesecake Hybrid

    Set up 5 Bee Houses on your main farm. Collect honey every 3 days. Combine with milk → cheese → Honey Cheesecake (390g each, 210g profit).

    Bee Houses Daily Honey Daily Cheesecake Potential Daily Income
    5 ~8 honey 2–3 cheesecakes ~500g/day
    20 ~33 honey 8–10 cheesecakes ~2,000g/day

    ✅ Completion Checklist

    Use this checklist to track your endgame progress:

    • Main Story: Sun Haven ☐ Nel'Vari ☐ Withergate ☐
    • Marriage: Spouse ☐ Children ☐ Maximum House Upgrade ☐
    • Nel'Vari Farm: Unlocked ☐ All Crops Grown ☐
    • Withergate: Rank: ___ ☐ All Shops Unlocked ☐
    • Museum: 25% ☐ 50% ☐ 75% ☐ 100% ☐
    • Exploration: 25% ☐ 50% ☐ 75% ☐ 100% ☐
    • Skill Max: Farming ☐ Mining ☐ Combat ☐ Fishing ☐ Foraging ☐ Cooking ☐ Crafting ☐ Exploration ☐
    • Money Goals: Steady 5,000g/day ☐ 10,000g/day ☐ 25,000g/day ☐

    Final Tip: Don't rush the endgame. Sun Haven has hundreds of hours of content across all three zones. The most fun is discovering hidden quests, building the perfect farm layout in Nel'Vari, and chatting with the 20 marriage candidates — none of which is mandatory and all of which is rewarding.
